Samsung LCD TV panel procurement from Taiwan-based LCD suppliers is expected to reach 10-12M units in 2009. Chi Mei Optoelectronics (CMO) supplies the most to Samsung. About 40% of Samsung's 25-26 million LCD TVs sold in 2009 will be from Taiwan. The trend is expected to continue. So, what does this mean? When you are planning to purchase a Samsung LCD TV stick to those with S-PVA panels, if possible. Source: DIGITIMES
