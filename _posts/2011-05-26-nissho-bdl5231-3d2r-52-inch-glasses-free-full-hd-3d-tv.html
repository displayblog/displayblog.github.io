Nissho → CrunchGear: Nissho Electronics announced its BDL5231-3D2R, a 52-inch 3D TV that does not require glasses. You get full all 1920x1080 pixels in 3D, which was developed by Dimenco Displays. The specs of the BDL5231-3D2R are: 60Hz refresh rate, 2000:1 contrast ratio, 700 nits of brightness, and a 8-ms response time. Priced at about US$20,850 and available in Japan.
