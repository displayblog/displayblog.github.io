NewsWire: The Samsung D9500 series is a 74-inch 3D LCD TV with a LED backlight. Samsung claims the D9500 is the largest 3D LCD TV in the world. The 240Hz active shutter 3D TV is quite thin at just 7.98 mm (0.31 inches). Priced at KRW1900,000 (about US$17,650).
