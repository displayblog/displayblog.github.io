DisplaySearch Blog:

Approximately 700,000 LCD TVs were shipped by brands in India during the month of October, which coincides with the great Indian festival of Diwali. This is a huge increase from 2008, when 745,000 LCD TVs were shipped in the entire year.

The top three brands were Sony, Samsung, and LG.
