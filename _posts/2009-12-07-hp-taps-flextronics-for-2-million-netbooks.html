Flextronics landed orders for two million netbooks for 2010 from HP. Flextronics will build them for US$45 each. DIGITIMES reports the industry average cost to build a netbook is $70 and will likely result in pressure to reduce component costs. Look forward to some really cheap netbooks from HP next year. Source: DIGITIMES
