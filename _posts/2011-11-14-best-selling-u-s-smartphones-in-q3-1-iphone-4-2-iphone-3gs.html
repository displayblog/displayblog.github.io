via Dan Frommer: According to NPD the best-selling smartphones in Q3 were:

  1. Apple iPhone 4
  2. Apple iPhone 3GS
  3. HTC EVO 4G
  4. Motorola Droid 3
  5. Samsung Intensity II

The iPhone 4 and iPhone 3GS are probably selling quite well in the U.S., but keep in mind NPD doesn't get data from Walmart, Sam's Club, and Costco to name a few.
