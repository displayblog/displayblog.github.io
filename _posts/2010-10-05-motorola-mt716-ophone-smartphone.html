The Motorola MT716 is the Chinese version of the Droid but with the Cliq's keyboard and running the OPhone mobile operating system. Like the Droid the 3.7-inch LCD packs a 854x480 pixel format, and probably IPS. Specs include: 8 megapixel camera with dual-LED flash, 720p video capture, front-facing camera with 640x480, TD-SCDMA (it is China), WiFi-compatible WAPI connectivity, CMMB TV, and 600MHz Texas Instrument's OMAP3430 CPU. Source: Motorola
