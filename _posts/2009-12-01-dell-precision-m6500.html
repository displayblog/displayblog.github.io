Dell: The Dell Precision M6500 is a 17-inch notebook PC sporting an Intel Core i7-920XM CPU, 16GB max DDR3 RAM, up to three storage devices with optional RAID and two GPU choices: ATI's FirePro M7740 or NVIDIA's Quadro FX 3800M. The 17-inch LCD sports a 1920x1200 resolution and a LED backlight. The price of entry is a steep $2749. The Covet version adds an anodized aluminum chassis in bright orange and a cover glass over the LCD and starts at an incredible $4219.
