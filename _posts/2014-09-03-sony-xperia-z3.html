re/code: Sony's new flagship smartphone is the IP58 certified Xperia Z3 and sports a 5.2-inch IPS LCD with a pixel format of 1920x1080. Let's just stop there: both the LG G3 and Samsung Galaxy Note 4 feature 2560x1440 displays.

I do like the symmetric front and back glass design of the Xperia Z3; the iPhone 4/4s design is my favorite. The aluminum frame is curved a bit and Sony made the Xperia Z3 thinner: 8.2mm → 7.3mm (the iPhone 5s is 7.6mm thick).

The camera uses a 1/2.3-inch Exmor RS image sensor and BIONZ image processor mated to a 25mm wide angle Sony G lens. ISO sensitivity goes all the way up to 12,800.

With its 3100mAh battery Sony claims the Xperia Z3 will last a full two days on a single charge. The Z3 comes in white, bronze, silver, and black. One nifty feature: you can play PlayStation 4 games right on your Xperia Z3 devices.

Update 2014.09.03 14:45

According to c|net Sony confirmed the Xperia Z3 will be available through T-Mobile in the U.S. in the final months of 2014.
