Westinghouse Digital SK-32H240S Specifications

  • Display: 32-inch TFT LCD
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9
  • Pixel Format: 1366x768
  • Contrast Ratio: 1200:1
  • Color Gamut: 75% NTSC
  • Viewing Angle: 176/176
  • Response Time: 8ms
  • Connectivity: Composite (2), S-Video (2), HDMI (2), VGA, Component (2)
  • Tuner: ATSC, ATSC, Clear QAM
  • Power Consumption: 150W (operational), 3W (standby)
  • Dimensions: 31.4x22.0x6.0-inch (w/o stand), 31.4x24.39x8.9-inch (w/stand)
  • Weight: 29.0lbs (w/o stand)

The Westinghouse Digital SK-32H240S is a 720p capable 32-inch LCD TV. The pixel format is 1366x768 and has a decent 1200:1 contrast ratio. There are opinions that suggest the human visual system is not capable of distinguishing the extra detail from a 1080p capable 32-inch LCD TV viewing from appropriate distances. So on a 32-inch LCD TV, you're good to go with just a 1366x768 pixel format.

The viewing angle isn't as wide as some other LCD TVs but it is good enough at 176/176. You most likely will not miss the 2 degrees. The 8ms response time isn't the fastest out there and you'll probably be able to see some motion blur: just try to stay away from sports and watch romantic comedies with your significant other. It also might be a good idea to calibrate the SK-32H240S for best results. The best part about the SK-32H240S is its price: around US$500.
