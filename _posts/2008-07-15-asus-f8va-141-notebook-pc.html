ASUS F8Va Specifications

Display: 14.1" Wide TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1440 x 900
Webcam: 1.3MP (Swivel)
GPU: 1GB ATI Radeon HD3650
CPU: 2.53GHz Intel Core 2 Duo T9400, 1066MHz FSB
Chipset: Intel PM45
RAM: Up to 4GB DDR2 800MHz
HDD: 320GB SATA
Connectivity: HDMI, E-SATA, VGA, WiFi, USB 2.0 (5), FireWire
Optical: Blu-ray
OS: Microsoft Vista
Availability: July 2008 in Korea
Price: MSRP US$1299

From the looks of ASUS' F8Va, the 14.1" wide notebook PC is pretty modern. I'm not sure if the back of the LCD is carbon fiber, but it sure looks that way. The material for the chassis seem to be a combination of magnesium alloy and aluminum. Looks nice. [Updated with more pictures after the link.]

The 14.1" wide TFT LCD display sports a higher than normal pixel format of 1440 x 900 and should provide ample working space for those on the go. The ATI Radeon HD3650 is one of the higher-end GPUs and it is loaded with a ginormous 1GB of VRAM. So if you're compelled to frag a few on the plane, with the 2.53GHz T9400 CPU combo, the F8Va shouldn't disappoint.

Source: AVING, Notebook Review
