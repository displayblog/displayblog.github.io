HP → Engadget: The HP Mini 1103 is a 10.1-inch business-oriented netbook sporting a 1024x600 pixel format and a LED backlight. Most likely the LCD is of the TN variety so won't be all that great viewing at angles. Graphics is powered by Intel's GMA 3150.

The Mini 1103 weighs 2.78 pounds and is powered by Intel's 1.66GHz N455 or 1.83GHz N475. Maximum RAM is limited to just 2GB. The Mini 1103 is probably a competent performer but that slight elongation of the neck (that portion that connects the chassis to the display) makes it look weird.
