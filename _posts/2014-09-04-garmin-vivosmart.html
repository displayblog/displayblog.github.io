Dana Wollman, Engadget:

The company just announced the $170 Vivosmart band, a fitness tracker that shows texts, emails, calls, calendar appointments, game notifications and music playback controls. For the time being, at least, it might just be the most versatile fitness device you're going to find.

The Garmin Vivosmart sports a curved OLED display, and is waterproof down to 50 meters. More functional than fashionable.
