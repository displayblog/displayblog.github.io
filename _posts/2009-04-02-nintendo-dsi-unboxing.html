Nintendo DSi Specifications

Display: Dual 3.25" (3.0" on DS Lite)
Camera: Dual 0.3MP (640 x 480)
Brightness: 5 settings (4 settings on DS Lite)
CPU: 133MHz ARM
RAM: 16MB (4x DS)
Storage: 256MB Internal Flash, SD/SDHC
Connectivity: WiFi
Battery: 840mAh Lithium Ion, 9-14 hours (lowest brightness setting)
Availability: April 5, 2009 in the US

Photo Courtesy: Engadget

Nintendo's DSi is "... slimmer, sleeker, lighter..." than the DS Lite. The DSi sports larger LCDs: 3.25" versus the dual 3.0" LCDs that were on the DS Lite. One more brightness setting and at the lowest brightness setting you can get 9-14 hours. That's quite a bit less than the 15-19 hours on the DS Lite.

There are also two 0.3MP cameras: one on the front and one on the back. Video conferencing? Or having your face up on a game? Sounds like fun. The DSi can also connect to the Internet using the built-in WiFi. I'm guessing you can play with other DSi players that are on the wireless network. The DSi should hit stores in just a few days. There are a ton more pictures over at Engadget.
