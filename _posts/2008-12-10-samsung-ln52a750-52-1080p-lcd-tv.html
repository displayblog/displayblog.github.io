Samsung LN52A750 Specifications

Display: 52" PVA (Patterned Vertical Alignment) TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 50,000:1 (dynamic)
Viewing Angles: 178/178
Response Time: 4ms
Frequency: 120Hz
Connectivity: HDMI (4), Composite (2), VGA, S-Video, Component (2)
Power Consumption: 290W (operational), <1W (standby)
Dimensions:
52.0 x 33.9 x 13.3" (w/stand)
52.0 x 31.7 x 4.2" (w/o stand)
Weight: 80.7lbs (w/stand), 66.4lbs (w/o stand)
Price: MSRP US$2899.99 ($1936.34 at Amazon.com)

Samsung's LN52A750 is a beautifully designed 52" LCD TV. It sports the Touch of Color theme. You can barely tell from the picture but it has a touch of red in the black chassis. The LN52A750 features a 1920 x 1080 pixel format so 1080p HD content is fed and displayed without any scaling. 720p HD content will be scaled up but will experience a slight decline in quality. The 52" LCD TV has a 120Hz frequency (double from the normal 60Hz) and that gets rid of almost all motion blur. Love watching sports? The LN52A750 won't disappoint. A response time of 4ms is plenty fast while the viewing angles are excellent at 178/178. This 7 Series LCD TV comes with four HDMI connections: a must if you watch a lot of TV, movies, and play a lot of games.

There are 188 customer reviews at Amazon.com and 158 of them gave the LN52A750 a 5 out of 5 star rating.Â Some negatives were the glass covering that reflected back some light. I would recommend turning off all lights when watching a movie on this beautiful LCD TV but if you have to have some lights turned on, make sure they are not positioned near you: on top, beside or behind the TV would be better. Not so good SD content: this is not surprising. I would stick to a tube TV for SD viewing if that is an option. Some of the positives were invisible touch sensitive controls, computer connection to HDMI easy and without lag, PS3 Blu-ray movies look awesome, etc. It is very important to feed good quality HD video into a TV like the LN52A750.
