Microsoft, Windows Phone division President, Andy Lees via winrumors:

You know, are we going to use Windows Phone 7 to produce tablets? Well, that is in conflict with this strategy. We view a tablet as a sort of PC. We want people to be able to do the sorts of things that they expect on a PC on a tablet...

Prior to the iPhone, didn't Microsoft think a smartphone as a sort of PC? Now the company has seen the iPad and for some reason still views it as a sort of PC.
