HP 3.5" Digital Photo Frame Specifications

Display: 320 x 240
Internal Storage: Holds 45 pictures
External Storage: SD, SDHC, MMC
Availability: Now in Europe
Price: US$76

Gizmodo: HP has a digital photo frame and it's a small one, 3.5" to be exact. The 3.5" TFT LCD sports a 320 x 240 pixel format and can read photos from SD, SDHC, and MMC cards. It is portable, but I wonder why you would carry this thing around when you have a mobile phone. The US$76 is very affordable, but quite expensive if you consider you actually don't need it. The box design is intriguing as it has a green theme and a ribbon. Maybe this is a special edition for an occasion.
