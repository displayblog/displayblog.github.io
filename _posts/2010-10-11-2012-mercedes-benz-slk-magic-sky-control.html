In all likelihood the 2012 Mercedes-Benz SLK will feature the company's Magic Sky Control. So many things are magic these days... The sunroof of the SLK is normally opaque but with the press of a button the glass turns transparent. I would assume it will be only a little while before we see other windows get this magical treatment. Source: Auto Express
