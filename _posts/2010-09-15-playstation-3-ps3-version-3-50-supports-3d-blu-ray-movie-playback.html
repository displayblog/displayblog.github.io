The upcoming Version 3.50 update to Sony's PlayStation 3 (PS3) will add support for 3D Blu-ray playback. The date is September 21st and probably will be worldwide.

I was curious as to how many 3D Blu-ray titles there were. I didn't think there were going to be that many. So I went to Amazon and did a quick search of "3d blu-ray" and out came 311 results. That's quite a bit. Source: Joystiq
