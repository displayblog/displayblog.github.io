The Unreal Engine 3 powers games like BioShock, Gears of War, Mass Effect, etc. and now it runs on the iPhone 3GS (iPod touch too). Watch the demo on YouTube. Epic makes use of OpenGL ES 2.0 and plans to license the engine in the near future. Does hat means near-certain death to the DS and PSP? Sooner or later Apple will be touting 100,000 games on the App Store. And when that happens I don't think the DS or PSP has much of a chance. The next generation iPhone and the iPod touch will have a good chance of becoming the ultimate portable device that you must take everywhere you go for almost anything you want to do.
