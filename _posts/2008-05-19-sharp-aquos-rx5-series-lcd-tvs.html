Sharp AQUOS RX5 Series LCD TVs
Sizes: 46" (LC-46RX5), 52" (LC-52RX5), 65" (LC-65RX5)
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 3,300:1, 1,500:1 (Living Contrast), 20,000:1 (Dynamic)
Brightness: 450 cd/m2
Frequency: 120Hz
Video Processing: 12-bit BDE
Input: HDMI (3)
Color: Black, Brown
Availability: July 1, 2008

Sharp introduced twenty-two LCD TVs with the AQUOS RX5 Series at the top. The RX5 series comes in three sizes with the 65" being the largest. All three sport a pixel format of 1920 x 1080 and a frequency of 120Hz for motion blur-free video viewing. The dynamic contrast ratio of 20,000:1 is nice. All three uses Sharp's Advanced Super View (ASV) LCD panel that provides excellent front screen performance.

You'll need to be quick as these are all limited production. For instance, the top-of-the-line LC-65RX5 model will be limited to just 3,000 units while the smaller LC-52RX5 is limited to just 5,000. There will be 10,000 units available for the smallest model, LC-46RX5.

Source: Electronista, Akihabara News, Engadget
