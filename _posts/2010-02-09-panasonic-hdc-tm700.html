Akihabara News: The HDC-TM700 from Panasonic is the world's first camcorder that records 1080/60p. To store 1080/60p at 28Mbps the HDC-TM700 has 96GB of internal memory. If that's not enough you can add up to 64GB more via a SDXC card.
