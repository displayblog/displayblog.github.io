The Verge: The Lenovo LePad K2010 / IdeaTab K2 is a 10.1-inch Ice Cream Sandwich tablet with a 1920x1200 IPS LCD, which I believe is a first. IPS is a solid start and add to that a resolution of 224 ppi. It falls short of "Retina", but is nonetheless impressive.
