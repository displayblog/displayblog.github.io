Dell's Vostro 1015 starts at US$429 with these specs: 15.6-inch LCD, 2.2GHz Celeron 900 CPU, 2GB DDR2 800MHz RAM, 8x DVD+/-RW, Intel GMA 4500MHD GPU, 160GB 5400RPM hard drive, WiFi BG, 2MP webcam, 6-cell battery. A business-geared full-blown notebook PC for the price of a netbook. Sounds good to me. Except I don't like the offset trackpad. Source: Dell via Engadget
