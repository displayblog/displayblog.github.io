Iiyama's ProLite T2250MTS is a 21.5-inch LCD monitor with a 1920 x 1080 resolution. The unique feature of the T2250MTS is that it is multitouch capable with up to two touch points. You can slide out the included stylus from the bottom right corner to draw or directly on the 21.5-inch LCD. Other specs include:

  • Brightness: 270 cd/m2
  • Response Time: 5ms
  • Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
  • Connectivity: DVI-D, VGA
  • Availability: December in Japan

Gestures such as rotating, moving, double-clicking, and changing the size of a picture are supported. For the price of over US$600, the T2250MTS is on the expensive side especially in light of HP's Compaq L2105tm going for just $299.
