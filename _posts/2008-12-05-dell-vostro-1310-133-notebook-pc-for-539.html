Dell Vostro 1310 Specifications

Display: 13.3" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1280 x 800
Webcam: None
GPU: Intel GMA X3100
CPU: 2.0GHz Intel Core 2 Duo T5870 (800MHz FSB, 2MB L2 Cache)
RAM: 3GB DDR2 SDRAM 667MHz
HDD: 250GB 5400RPM SATA
Optical: Slot Load 8x DVD+/-RW with DL DVD+/-R
Connectivity: WiFi (802.11g)
OS: Windows Vista Home Basic SP1
Battery: 9-cell Lithium Ion
Price: $1132 ($599 now at Dell)

Dell's Vostro 1310 is a no-nonsense 13.3" notebook PC. The 13.3" TFT LCD sports a pixel format of 1280 x 800 and graphics is powered by Intel's GMA X3100. Don't expect to be able to play any games that require some GPU power; stick to the original Starcraft and you'll be just fine. NotebookReview reviewed the 2.1GHz version and concluded that the Vostro 1310 was "an impressive budget notebook with clean looks and solid performance." Dell is selling the Vostro 1310 right now for just $599 with free shipping.
