DigiTimes:

Pegatron Technology has landed orders for 10 million units of iPhone 5 to become the second ODM of the smartphone. Shipments will start in September, according to industry sources.

ODM? I don't think Pegatron had anything to do with designing the iPhone. If these industry sources are correct, the timing of the next iPhone should go something like this: in September, Pegatron begins to ship and Apple announces the next iPhone, with availability in retail slipping to October a possibility.
