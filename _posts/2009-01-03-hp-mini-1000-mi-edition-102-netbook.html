HP's Mini 1000 Mi (Mobile internet) Edition is expected to be available soon. The Mini 1000 Mi Edition will likely feature a Linux OS, instant on/off, and have easy access to email, internet, pictures, video and music via HP's Mobile Internet Experience software.

The LCD will either be a 8.9" or 10.2" both sporting a 1024 x 600 pixel format. The chassis will be just 1" thick and weigh 2.25lbs. The keyboard will be large enough for most at 92% of full-size. Prices start at $379.99.

Source: HP via Portable Monkey via Engadget
