CDT's Eclipse system consists of 192 digital source-measure channels and has been adapted for use at high temperatures. The equipment is designed for testing emissive display devices for measuring lifetime under constant conditions such as current, voltage or luminance. The Eclipse system will enhance Merck OLED Materials' productivity in developing OLED materials.
