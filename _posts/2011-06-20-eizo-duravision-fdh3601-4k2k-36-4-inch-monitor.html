The Eizo DuraVision FDH3601 is a 36.4-inch LCD monitor that sports a pixel format of 4096x2160 or 4K2K. Resolution at 127 ppi sounds less spectacular. Other specs include: LED BLU, 700 nits, 1000:1 CR, DVI-D (2x), and DisplayPort (2x). Priced at a stratospherically high US$36,000. Available September 7.
