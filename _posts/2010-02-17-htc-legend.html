HTC's Legend is an Android smartphone that ups the ante compared to the company's Hero: Android 2.1, updated Sense UI, more RAM (384MB vs. 288MB), and Adobe Flash 10.1 support. I wonder how good the Flash experience will be considering the Legend has a 600MHz CPU. But what really gets me is the smallish 3.2-inch 480x320 AMOLED multitouch display. Let's get rid of that huge bottom bezel, the button and instead add a bigger display with more pixels. Keep the physical buttons; I like those. Source: HTC
