Commodore UMMD 8010/F Specifications

Display: 10.2" Wide TFT LCD
Pixel Format: 1024 x 600
CPU: 1.6GHz VIA C7-M
HDD: 80GB
RAM: 1GB
Connectivity: WiFi, Bluetooth (optional)
Price: About US$600

Commodore. Commodore 67 comes to my mind. Ah, the good ol days. This time Commodore is busting out and joining the netbook party with its 10.2" UMMD 8010/F. It sure isn't going to be as easy to remember as the Commodore 67.

The UMMD 8010/F sports a 10.2" wide TFT LCD with a pixel format of 1024 x 600, pretty much like everyone else (ASUS N10, Lenovo IdeaPad S10, MSI Wind U100, etc.). Commodore's netbook doesn't rock an Intel Atom but instead VIA's C7-M CPU. Commodore isn't the only one that's using VIA's C7-M. Everex, Pioneer, Maxdata and a couple of others all sport it.Â The US$600 price tag seems to be a bit on the high side with a 80GB HDD, 1GB RAM and WiFi.
