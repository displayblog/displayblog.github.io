Nokia Supernova Specifications

Display: 2.2" TFT LCD
Pixel Format: 240 x 320
Camera: 3.2MP (still images), 640 x 480 (video), LED Flash, 8x Digital Zoom
Storage: 30MB built-in, microSD
Audio: FM Radio with RDS, MP3, MP4, AAC, ACC+, eAAC
Video: MPEG4, 3GP
Connectivity: A-GPS, GPRS, EDGE, GSM, Bluetooth 2.0
Battery: 860mAh Lithium Ion (5.4 hours talk, 300 hours standby)
Dimension: 3.9 x 1.9 x 0.6" (98 x 48 x 15mm)
Weight: 3.5oz (99g)
Availability: Q4'08
Pricing: EUR 180

What a beautiful phone! Although it has four more buttons on the front face than I would prefer, Nokia has done a marvelous job in simplifying the design and making it sexy at the same time. The 2.2" TFT LCD with a pixel format of 240 x 320 sounds a bit smallish and with not enough pixels, but hopefully Nokia will follow up with a larger display with more pixels soon. The 3.2MP camera should take some decent pictures but I don't see any advanced technologies such as image stabilization or face detection so image quality might not be up to high-end standards. Nokia 7610 Supernova has style, but other than that, there doesn't seem to be anything super about it. Quite unfortunate since it has so much external looks going for it...
