Panasonic TH-42PH10UKA Specifications

Display: 42" Wide Plasma Display Panel
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1024 x 768
Contrast Ratio: 400:1 (Static), 10,000:1 (Dynamic)
Brightness: 1200 cd/m2
Tuner: No
Connectivity: RS232, VGA, HDMI, Component
Power Consumption: 380W (Operational), 0.2W (Standby)
Dimensions: 40.2 x 24.0 x 3.5" (Without Stand)
Weight: 57.3lbs
Price: MSRP US$1445

Panasonic's TH-42PH10UKA is a very popular plasma display that is used for professional applications that do not require a tuner. The 42" plasma display features a 1024 x 768 pixel format in a 16:9 aspect ratio. The XGA pixel format is a little on the low side, but with professional applications such as digital signage that are seen from far away, having the most pixel count doesn't help much since most of the graphics and fonts are pretty big. The dynamic contrast ratio of 10,000:1 helps in generating some good images while the 1200 cd/m2 brightness allows for viewing content in brightly lit areas. The TH-42PH10UKA can be found for much much less than that MSRP at online merchants.

Source: Panasonic
