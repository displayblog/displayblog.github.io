Philips Essence 42PES0001 Specifications

Display: 42" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 66,000:1 (dynamic)
Response Time: 2ms
Frequency: 100Hz
Connectivity: HDMI (3)

Price: Â£1999

The Essence 42PES0001 LCD TV from Philips is unique. The 42PES0001 sports a 42" TFT LCD with a 1920 x 1080 pixel format and features an ultra-fast 2ms response time and a doubled frequency of 100Hz (European standard is 50Hz) and that significantly reduces motion blur. So if you're a sports fan and watch a lot of sports, the 42PES0001 should be on your list of TVs to consider. Philips used a separate connection hub that includes the speakers, tuner, video connections and power connection to trim down the thickness and weight of the display itself. The 42PES0001 looks nice and if you are planning to mount it up on a wall the separate connection hub should save you from having to attempt awkward acrobatics with your neck.
