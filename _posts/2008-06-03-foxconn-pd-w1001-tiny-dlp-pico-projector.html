Foxconn PD-W1001 Specifications
Display Technology: DLP
Pixel Format: 854 x 480
Brightness: 25 ANSI Lumens
Availability: Q2'09

I thought Foxconn's PD-S2900 was small. The company's PD-W1001 makes the PD-S2900 look supersized! Foxconn claims that the DLP projector is smaller than a mobile phone, and I think that is definitely the case. To see a picture of a real pico projector is cool.
