Skype 4.2 Beta for Windows supports 720p @ 30fps HD video calling. Skype is working with webcam manufacturers to offload HD video calling from the CPU. That means you won't need to upgrade your entire computer just to do HD video chats on Skype. LG and Panasonic are working on HD webcam-integrated TVs so Skype works directly on those TVs. Make it easy for the grandpas and grandmas of the world and I think Skype has got a hit. Source: Akihabara News
