Samsung LN40A630 Specifications

Display: 40" (39.9") Wide TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 40,000:1 (Dynamic)
Viewing Angle: 178/178
Response Time: 4ms
Frequency: 120Hz (Auto Motion Plus)
Connectivity: HDMI (3), Composite (2), S-Video, Component (2), VGA
Video Processing: DNIe
Tuner: NTSC and ATSC
Other: Energy Star compliant
Dimensions:

    38.9 x 26.4 x 9.4" (with stand)
    38.9 x 24.0 x 3.8" (without stand)

Weight: 41.2lbs (with stand), 35.7lbs (without stand)
Price: US$1699.99

Samsung's LN40A630 LCD TV is a 40" 1080p unit that sports the company's Touch of Color design. The frequency is doubled to 120Hz that should help significantly reduce motion blur. Samsung's Auto Motion Plus technology is applied to the 120Hz frequency that inserts a created frame based on the one before and after to smooth out the jaggies. The LN40A630's dynamic contrast ratio is 40,000:1 and should give you contrasty enough images.

In a world where energy consumption is a major concern, the LN40A630 is Energy Star compliant and should help in maintaining a low level of energy use. Of course, the best way to reduce energy consumption is to go for a smaller sized LCD TV and to watch less TV.
