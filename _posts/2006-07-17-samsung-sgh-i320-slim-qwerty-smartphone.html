On July 4, Samsung announced the SGH-i320 with a 2.2" TFT LCD: 240 x 320 (QVGA) resolution and 65,000 colors. Windows Mobile 5 for Smartphone OS powers the EDGE and GPRS-enabled smartphone. It also has a MP3 player that can play files in MP3, AAC/AAC+, WMA, WAV and OGG formats. The SGH-i320 has video recording, dual speakers, a speakerphone, is Bluetooth capable, has a USB port and plenty of memory: 120MB embedded and a microSD slot. The unit launched in Europe in July and will spread throughout Europe and South East Asia shortly, according to Samsung's press announcement.
