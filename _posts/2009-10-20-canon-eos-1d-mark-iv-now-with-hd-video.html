Canon released the EOS-1D Mark IV, upgrading its flagship DSLR. One of the most exciting features, to me at least, is the inclusion of 1080p HD video recording capabilities.

  • 16.1 megapixel APS-H CMOS sensor
  • ISO: 50-102,400
  • Improved photodiodes and gapless microlenses
  • Dual Digic 4 processors
  • M-RAW and S-RAW capability
  • Enhanced Custom Functions
  • 45 AF points, including 39 cross-type sensors
  • Control the number and orientation of selectable focusing points
  • New AF point expansion option
  • Improved AI Servo II AF predictive focusing algorithms
  • New AF-related custom functions for greater control
