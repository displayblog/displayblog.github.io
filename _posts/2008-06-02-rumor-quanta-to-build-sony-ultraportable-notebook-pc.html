According to Quanta Computer, Sony will be introducing an ultraportable notebook PC using VIA's OpenBook reference design. Sony's ultraportable will sport a 8.9" TFT LCD with a pixel format of 1024 x 600, perfect for surfing! Knowing how Sony prices its ultraportable gadgets, I would hazard to guess that this one will be one expensive little ultraportable.
