Samsung Mobile Display will exhibit a production-ready 32-inch OLED TV panel at SID 2009. There are two: 14.1-inch and 31-inch. I'll focus on the 31-inch here. The 31-inch OLED TV panel is the world's first OLED panel with a 1920 x 1080 pixel format. Contrast ratio, like almost all other OLED panels, is 1,000,000:1. Color gamut is also more than 100% NTSC. And, of course, thickness is just 8.9mm (.35-inch). Fine Metal Mask (FMM) technology is used for mass production. When will this 32-inch 1080p OLED TV panel come to market? Hopefully soon.
