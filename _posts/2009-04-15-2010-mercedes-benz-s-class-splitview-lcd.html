In-car dialogues are about to go instinct. And Mercedes-Benz is going to make sure with its new Splitview LCD in the company's top-of-the-line S-Class vehicles. Splitview allows the driver to see one thing and the passenger to a different image from the same LCD. Check out the video above. If you're the impatient sort you can jump to 1:20 (1 minute 20 seconds) to see the LCD transition from driver view to passenger view.
