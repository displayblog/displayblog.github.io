ASUS VW246H Specifications

Display: 24" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 20000:1 (dynamic)
Response Time: 2ms (GTG)
Brightness: 300 cd/m2
Viewing Angles: 170/160
Connectivity: HDMI, DVI-D, VGA
Adjustability: Tilt
Power Consumption: 60W (operational), 2W (standby)
Dimensions: 22.4 x 16.1 x 9.1"
Weight: 12.1lbs

The ASUS VW246H is a 24" LCD monitor with a 16:9 aspect ratio sporting a pixel format of 1920 x 1080. Yes, much like most wide LCD TVs. If you're watching 1080p HD content, you won't get any black bars and the video will completely fill your screen. The VW246H has a super-fast gray-to-gray response time of 2ms thanks to the TN TFT LCD. Viewing angles at 170/160 are somewhat limited so make sure you're looking at the 24" LCD monitor straight on. If you need color fidelity you should look for LCD monitors that use PVA or IPS LCD technology. I am glad to see that ASUS still kept a DVI-D connection. It is a pain to go to eBay (don't get it at retail because the prices are outrageous!) and purchase a DVI-to-HDMI cable just to connect your new LCD monitor that only has a HDMI connection. Yes, VGA is always an option but not if you're looking for digital clarity. The brightness at 300 cd/m2 is sufficient for most. The VW246H only tilts, so if you require height adjusting capability, you're out of luck with this one. But if you're on a really tight budget and looking to grab a 24" 1080p LCD monitor, Newegg is selling the VW246H for just $280. Quite the bargain!
