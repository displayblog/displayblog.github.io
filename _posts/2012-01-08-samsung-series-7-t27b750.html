AnandTech: Looks nice, right? Well, don't be fooled. The display is 27 inches and you'd think it would sport a 2560x1440 pixel format. But then you'd be wrong. The T27B750 'premium' Series 7 monitor packs an anemic 1920x1080, good for a resolution of 81.59 ppi. That's not all. The display technology? Without a doubt Samsung's IPS-equivalent PLS. That should be the answer, but no, it's a piece of crap TN. And who cares if the price is just US$599 if what you're getting looks good only when it's off.
