HP EliteBook 6930p Specifications
Display: 14.1" Wide TFT LCD with optional LED Backlight
GPU: Intel GMA X3100 or ATI Mobility Radeon HD 3450 with 128MB or 256MB
CPU: Intel Core 2 Duo 8000 or 9000
Chipset: Intel Mobile 45
RAM: 4GB DDR2
Storage: 5400RPM 250GB SATA HD (optional second HD for RAID 0/1)
Optical: DVD Burner
Connectivity: WiFi (802.11b/g/n), Bluetooth 2.0, EV-DO (optional), HSPDA (Optional), USB 2.0 (3), VGA, Microphone, Headphone, FireWire, Ethernet, ExpressCard/54, SD
Webcam: 2MP (optional)
Other: Meets MIL-STD 810F specifications, Inner Magnesium Shell, Energy Star qualified
Battery: 15 hours (with optional Ultra Capacity)
Dimension; 13 x 9.6 x 1.23"
Weight: 4.7lbs (2.1kg)
Availability: Summer 2008

HP's EliteBook 6930p doesn't look all that elite, but looks are deceiving in this case. The 6930p 14.1" wide notebook PC meets MIL-STD 810F specifications. What does that mean? That means the 6930p is one touch machine. A brushed anodized aluminum exterior casing is combined with a magnesium alloy chassis that makes the 6930p a very durable notebook PC. It also has a shock-resistant HD and spill-resistant keyboard. The optional Illumi-Lite LED backlight should add to a bit more battery life while keeping the unit rugged.
