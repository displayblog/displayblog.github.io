The Olympus PEN E-P2 is available for pre-order at Amazon. It's a bit on the expensive side at US$1,099.99 but it does have some enhancements over the original E-P1: continuous AF tracking mode for both stills and videos, full manual exposure control in movie mode, optional microphone jack adapter, CEC compliance, articulating VF-2 EVF included.
