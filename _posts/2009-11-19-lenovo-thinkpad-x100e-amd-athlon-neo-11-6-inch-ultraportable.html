Lenovo's ThinkPad X100e is a 11.6-inch notebook PC that sports a 1366 x 768 resolution and powered by AMD's Athlon Neo. So it isn't a netbook but an ultraportable notebook PC. RAM can be equipped up to 4GB and a 320GB hard drive. 3G and GPS are optional. There are three USB ports, an Ethernet and a media card reader. The battery options include a 3-cell and a 6-cell. White. Now that's different.
