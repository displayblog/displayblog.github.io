Westinghouse Digital D56QX1 Specifications
Display: 56" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 3840 x 2160
Availability: June 2008
Price: $50,000

Westinghouse Digital showcased its 56" quad HD display, the D56QX1, during InfoComm 2008. Simply put the D56QX1 is like having four 1920 x 1080 displays in one. To be technically correct, it isn't quad HD, it is quad Full HD! If you look at the text on the D56QX1 on the upper left corner, you can see that this 56" monster is nothing like other 56" displays. $50,000 is a LOT to pay, but if you need a single display to sport as many pixels as possible, you'll be hard-pressed to find a LCD with more pixels per inch than the D56QX1.
