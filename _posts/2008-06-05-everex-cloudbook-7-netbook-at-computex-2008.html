Everex Cloudbook Specifications
Display: 7" LTPS TFT LCD with LED Backlight
CPU: 1.2GHz VIA C7-M ULV
Chipset: VIA VX700 Unified Digital Media IGP
GPU: Integrated VIA UniChrome Pro II 3D/2D AGP
Storage: 1.8" 30GB HD
Connectivity: Bluetooth, WiFi
OS: gOS
Dimension: 230 x 171 x 33.5mm
Weight: 970g

Everex showcased its Cloudbook, a 7" netbook that uses a higher performance Low Temperature Poly-Silicon (LTPS) TFT LCD. Everex could have made the entire unit much smaller, but I guess they were trying to keep the keyboard large enough for regular-sized folks. It is also unfortunate that the 7" LCD is shifted to the left and not centered. The LED backlight should sip, rather than gulp, power to maximize your cloud computing time. But the 30GB HDD will negate that advantage a bit: how about a SSD option like those from the Eee PC, Aspire One, M912?

Source: AVING
