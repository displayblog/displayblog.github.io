Atom 330 + Ion: ASUS' Eee PC 1201N is a glossy 12.1-inch netbook sporting a 1366 x 768 resolution, a dual-core 1.6GHz Atom 330 CPU, up to 3GB DDR2 RAM, up to 320GB HDD, a six-cell battery that lasts up to 5 hours and running Windows 7 Premium. The 1201N packs NVIDIA's Ion GPU that means you can play DirectX 10 games and watch 1080p video. It's not too thick either at just 1.3 inches. And at 3.2 pounds, it's quite light. Connectivity options include HDMI, VGA, USB 2.0 (3), Ethernet, headphone & mic, MMC/SD/SDHC slot, WiFi N, Bluetooth 2.1+EDR. All of this can't be cheap. But it is. The 1201N is just US$499 with your choice of black or silver.
