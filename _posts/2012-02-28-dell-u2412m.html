AnandTech: The US$329 Dell U2412M is a 24-inch 16:10 1920x1200 LCD monitor sporting a 6-bit + FRC e-IPS panel. If color is important to you, and it probably is if you're looking for an IPS monitor, I recommend spending a bit more for one with a bona fide 8-bit IPS panel with LUT.
