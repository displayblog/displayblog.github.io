NEC MultiSync LCD39090WQXi Specifications
Display: 30" TFT LCD
Pixel Format: 2560 x 1600
Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
Response Time: 12ms, 6ms (GTG)
Brightness: 350 cd/m2
Viewing Angle: 178/178
Color Gamut: 102% USNTSC
Power Consumption: 150W (operational)
Connectivity: Composite, DVI-D with HDCP, DVI-I, VGA

NEC's LCD3090WQXi is a beautiful LCD monitor. You really have to see the actual picture quality on the 30" LCD monitor to truly appreciate the near perfect image quality. I am currently at InfoComm 2008 being held in Las Vegas and there are many interesting displays being showcased. When I was at the NEC booth, I could not help noticing the brilliant colors on the frog that was being displayed on the LCD3090WQXi. I knew I had to start saving up now if I wanted to afford this beauty.

The pixel format of 2560 x 1600 is normal for a 30" LCD monitor, but what is not normal is the 102% US NTSC color gamut that bring out some incredible colors thanks to the wide color gamut CCFL (WCG-CCFL). The IPS LCD panel technology feature a 1000:1 contrast ratio, 6ms GTG, 350 cd/m2 of brightness and a very good 178/178 viewing angle.
