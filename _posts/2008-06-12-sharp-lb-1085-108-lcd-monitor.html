Sharp LB-1085 Specifications
Display: 108" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 1200:1
Brightness: 400 cd/m2
Viewing Angle: 176/176
Response Time: 6ms (GTG)
Connectivity: HDMI (3), S-Video, DVI, VGA, Audio Out (3.5mm), Composite (2), Component (2)
Dimension: 2572 x 1550 x 204mm
Weight: 195kg (w/o stand)
Availability: To be delivered to the Shinjuku Piccadilly Cinema Complex opening on July 19, 2008
Price: Open (build-to-order product)

You need big? Sharp has got you covered. Sharp's LB-1085 is a 108" LCD monitor! Sharp is targeting the 108" monster for commercial applications, but I don't see why it wouldn't work in your massive living room. Unfortunately, or fortunately, the pixel format is limited to 1920 x 1080. You won't be able to watch more than a single 1080p HD stream, but that might be all you want. You can also consider tiling four together to get to Quad Full HD configuration.

When used for its intended market, we can look forward to massive advertisements at super exclusive retail outlets in the near future. Or actually, at the Shinjuku Piccadilly, Tokyo's premiere multiplex cinema. The LB-1085 will be set up in the center of the third floor main lobby facing the open foyer.


Shinjuku Piccadilly Cinema Complex concept drawing

The LB-1085's LCD panel is manufactured from Sharp's Kameyama Plant No. 2 and 108" is the maximum size that can be manufactured from a single 8th generation (G8) glass substrate with a dimension of 2160 x 2460mm.

Source: Sharp via Akihabara News
