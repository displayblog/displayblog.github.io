DigiTimes: North America will be getting the BlackBerry PlayBook first in the first quarter of 2011 according to Gregory Wade, Regional VP for Asia Pacific at RIM. The rest of the world will need to wait until the second quarter. The PlayBook will support either LTE or WiMAX. Taiwan Mobile (TWM) will be the exclusive provider for the PlayBook in Taiwan.
