Dell Vostro A90 Specifications

Display: 8.9" TFT LCD
Pixel Format: 1024 x 600
Webcam: 0.3MP (optional)
GPU: Intel GMA950
CPU: 1.6GHz Intel Atom N270, 533MHz FSB
Chipset: Intel 945GSE
RAM: Up to 1GB DDR2 533MHz
HDD: Up to 16GB SSD
Connectivity: WiFi BG, Ethernet, Bluetooth, VGA, 3-in-1 Memory Card Reader (SD, SDIO, MMC), Mic-In, Headphone-Out
OS: Windows XP Home Edition
Battery: 4-cell Lithium Ion
Dimensions: 9.13 x 6.77 x 1.07-1.25" (232 x 172 x 27.2 - 31.7mm)
Weight: Starting 2.36lbs (1.07kg)
Price: US$349

Dell's Vostro A90 is a Mini 9 clone but in business attire. But business doesn't mean a lot more expensive, just a bit more expensive. The Vostro A90 is just $349, $100 more than the Mini 9. All the specs are pretty similar to the Mini 9 such as the 8.9" LCD, 1024 x 600 pixel format, slow Intel GPU, Atom CPU, etc. so I don't know what the premium cost is associated with. Ah! I see. It's probably Windows.
