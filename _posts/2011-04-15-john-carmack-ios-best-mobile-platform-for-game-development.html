CrunchGear:

John Carmack, the id Software co-founder who essentially created the modern first-person shooter genre from scratch, has once again said that the iOS platform is the best mobile platform out there when it comes to game development.

I guess it's safe to assume that more great games will be coming to an iPhone, iPod touch, iPad near you.
