Gartner research director George Shiffler via Cult of Mac:

We expect growing consumer enthusiasm for mobile PC alternatives, such as the iPad and other media tablets, to dramatically slow home mobile PC sales, especially in mature markets.

Translation: netbooks, notebooks, laptops, etc. for the home will get kicked in the nuts by the new iPad 2, and other tablets. With one exception: MacBooks.
