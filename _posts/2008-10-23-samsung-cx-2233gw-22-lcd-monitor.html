Samsung CX-2233GW Specifications

Display: 22" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1680 x 1050
Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
Brightness: 300 cd/m2
Response Time: 5ms
Connectivity: DVI-HDCP

Samsung unveiled its CX-2233GW 22" LCD monitor. It is a fairly standard monitor except it is white. The CX-2233GW has a 1680 x 1050 pixel format, a brightness of 300 cd/m2, a contrast ratio of 1000:1 and a fairly fast 5ms response time. The sole connection is a DVI with HDCP. I wonder if Samsung and other major brands will follow Apple's lead and shift toward DisplayPort. Actually it wasn't Apple that led, it was Dell. Do you think DisplayPort will become the next display connection standard?
