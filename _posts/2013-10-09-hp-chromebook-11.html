Google: The HP Chromebook 11 sports an 11.6-inch 1366x768 IPS LCD, charges via USB, and is priced at US$279. I think it'll be great for anyone who lives on the Internet and is looking for a cheap portable computer. Google-colored accents are a nice touch, but I think the black version looks best.
