BenQ nScreen i221 Specifications

Display: 21.5" Wide TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Webcam: 1.3MP
GPU: ATI Radeon X1200
CPU: 1.50GHz Sempron 210U
Chipset: AMD 690+ SB600
RAM: 1GB
HDD: 160GB
Optical: N/A

BenQ's nScreen i221 is a 21.5" all-in-one PC that looks quite nice: simple and elegant. The 21.5" LCD sports a pixel format of 1920 x 1080 and is powered by ATI's Radeon X1200. The 1.5GHz Sempron 210U powers the nScreen i221 but the funny thing about it is that it does not have an optical drive. There are more pictures of the nScreen i221 at Akihabara News.
