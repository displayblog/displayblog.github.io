Novel Quest will be coming out with its Emperor 1510 workstation, a slightly less expensive version of the Emperor 200. The Emperor 200 was US$40,000; the Emperor 1510 will be just $4950. Still it's quite expensive. But for that price you get seat tilting, integrated audio, and LED lighting. Expected launch: December 15. Source: Novel Quest via Born Rich, Gizmodo
