Samsung's SyncMaster Lapfit line of LCD monitors are geared for notebook PCs that require a bit more screen real estate but don't require looking up and down at an external monitor. The LD220Z is a Lapfit with a 22-inch LCD with a 1920 x 1080 resolution and multitouch capabilities. Here are some specs:

  • Resolution: 1920 x 1080
  • Response Time: 5ms
  • Speakers: Yes, 2x 1.5W
  • Connectivity: VGA, HDMI


The Lapfit LCD monitors from Samsung stands at the same height as most notebook PCs so it's easy to look from your notebook screen to the Lapfit.
