Canon: The EOS-1D X replaces both the 1Ds Mark III and 1D Mark IV. Specs: 18 MP full-frame CMOS sensor, dual DIGIC 5+, 12 fps RAW, ISO 51200, 61-point AF, 252-zone metering, 1080/30p, Gigabit Ethernet, 3.2-inch LCD, and much more. Available March 2012 for MSRP US$6800.
