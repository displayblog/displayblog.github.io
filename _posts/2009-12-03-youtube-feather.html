On December 3, 2009 Google announced YouTube Feather, a light (beta) version of YouTube. Loading videos is definitely faster, but some features are gone: rating and commenting. You only see 10 comments. No biggies in my book since most of the time I'm just watching. This is a great move by Google by enhancing the experience of watching movies on YouTube.
