At just 1.74 pounds the XNote LGX30 from LG is very light. The 11.6-inch netbook sports a 1366 x 768 resolution, SSD, webcam, multicard reader a 3-cell battery and is powered by an Atom CPU and Intel's GMA500. Connectivity options include Bluetooth and WiFi GBN. Windows 7 runs on the LGX30. Again this 11.6-inch sporting a decent 1366 x 768 resolution is just 1.74 pounds. Wish I was toting this one around CES 2009 instead of my 17-inch MacBook Pro! Source: FCC (PDF) via Wireless Goodness, Engadget
