HTC G2 / Magic / Sapphire Specifications

Display: 3.2" Touch TFT LCD
Pixel Format: 320 x 480 (portrait), 480 x 320 (landscape)
CPU: 528MHz Qualcomm MSM7201a
Camera: 3.2MP with Auto Focus
RAM: 192MB (ROM: 512MB)
Storage: microSD
Connectivity: WiFi BG, GPS, Bluetooth, mini-USB
Network: HSDPA/WCDMA (900/1200MHz), GSM/GPRS/EDGE (850/900/1800/1900MHz)
OS: Android Cupcake
Battery: 1340mAh (400 minutes WCDMA and 450 minutes GSM talk time)
Dimensions: 113 x 55 x 13.65mm (4.45 x 2.17 x 0.54")
Weight: 118.5g (4.18oz)
Availability: Late April 2009 from Vodafone

The G2 or Magic or Sapphire looks very close to the G1 but without a slide-out QWERTY keyboard. The G2 has five buttons on the lower part of the smartphone and is slimmer and lighter than the G1. The G2 is the first Android phone without a physical keyboard. According to Wired, the on-screen keyboard is good but is not as good as the iPhone or the Pre. That's not good.

Source: Wired, The Boy Genius Report
