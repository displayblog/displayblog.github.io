Price: EST $1,099.99

NEC's MultiSync LCD2490WUXi is a top-grade 24" LCD monitor that incorporates a H-IPS (In-Plane Switching) LCD panel. The LCD2490WUXi features a very good contrast ratio of 800:1, excellent viewing angles of 178/178 and GTG response times of 8ms. The price is on the high side, but you do get a very long warranty of 4 years for parts and labor including the backlight. If you require high levels of color fidelity, you can't go wrong with a LCD monitor using an IPS panel.
