VIZIO VP322 Specifications
Display: 32" Plasma
Pixel Format: 1366 x 768 1024 x 720
Contrast Ratio: 30,000:1 (dynamic)
Lifetime: 60,000 hours
Connectivity: HDMI (3), Component, VGA
Tuner: ATSC, ClearQAM, NTSC
Price: US$599

Update 2008.06.25 12:54PM PST
The pixel format of Vizio's VP322 is 1024 x 720. The XGA-type, but not quite. So, don't go thinking that this plasma TV will show off the new HD channels you've been subscribing to. To properly show 720p content at a 16:9 aspect ratio, you'll need a pixel format of 1280 x 720. Thanks Sam K for the heads up.

Original Post

VIZIO has done it again. The company is busting out its VP322 for just $599. The VP322 32" plasma TV is available in Wal-Mart stores. The specifications are not going to blow your socks off, but at that price, it's not a bad deal. The VP322 does have a cool feature: a dynamic contrast ratio of 30,000:1 that will make the images on the uber-cheap plasma TV not so bad. I'm still hoping there will be a plethora of $499 plasma TVs later this year.
