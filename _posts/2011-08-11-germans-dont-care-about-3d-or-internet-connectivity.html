DisplaySearch:

Results of the recently published DisplaySearch Global TV Replacement Study suggest that German consumers barely show an interest in new technologies like 3D or Internet-TV, despite record-setting sales of new flat screen displays.

Sensible German folks. Dig a little deeper and it might be surprising to TV brands that most of the world don't care for 3D or Internet connectivity. I'll tell you what gets me excited: improvements in picture quality.
