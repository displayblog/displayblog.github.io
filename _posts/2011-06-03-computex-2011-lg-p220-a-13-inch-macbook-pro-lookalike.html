Geeky Gadgets: The LG P220 notebook is very similar looking to a MacBook Pro. There are obvious differences like the display hinge, indicators on the front edge (exactly the place where it is impossible to see at a glance), the trackpad button, the vent on the side, but the overall design utterly lacks in creativity.

LG reportedly incorporated LG Display's Shuriken Display into the P220 but the bezel doesn't look all that thin to me. Compared to a 13.3-inch MacBook Pro the display looks almost identical, except the bottom bezel looks a bit thicker on the P220. And why would you place a webcam at the bottom?
