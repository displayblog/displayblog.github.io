Konka Group, a China-based TV manufacturer with its headquarters in Shenzhen, announced that it broke ground on its LCD module plant located in Kunshan City, Jiangsu Province, China. Konka is investing CNY886 million on the LCD module plant that will have four module production lines and four module TV integration lines with an annual capacity of 2.4 million units. Once the LCD module plant commences operation in Q4'08 it will be the largest in China. The company is building the LCD module plant to reduce FPD TV production costs by about 10% according to Chen Yuehua, president of Konka.
