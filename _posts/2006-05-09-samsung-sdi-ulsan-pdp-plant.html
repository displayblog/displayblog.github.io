On May 15, Samsung SDI will hold a groundbreaking ceremony for its PDP plant in Ulsan, Korea. SDI will invest 730 billion won (~US$770 million) to build this new PDP plant that will be able to produce 3 million 42" panels per year. The new plant will have the largest production capacity. With the additional capacity, Samsung SDI will have a total production capacity of 7.32 million units.
