The REGZA IS04 on KDDI is Toshiba's first Android smartphone. The IS04 sports a large 4-inch LCD packing a 16:9 854x480 pixel format and an image sensor with 12.19 megapixels. Other specs include: 1GHz Snapdragon CPU, 1-Seg TV tuner, micro SDHC slot, WiFi, Bluetooth, WIN HIGH SPEED (9.2Mbps up/5.5Mbps down). And a unique feature for a smartphone as good looking at the IS04: waterproof casing. The only downside is that it will be running Android 2.1. The REGZA IS04 will be available January 2011.
