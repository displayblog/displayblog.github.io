Ozzio seems to be a local Japanese brand. The 50" HDV-50WX31PD features a pixel format of 1366 x 768 and a very bright 1,000 cd/m2. The contrast ratio of 8,000:1 is very good. Overall, the HDV-50WX31PD is a solid offering from falls short of being great. I would like to see the contrast ratio get north of 10,000:1 at the least and the pixel format at 1920 x 1080. But, if you're looking for something that only a few have outside of Japan, Ozzio might be that sleeper brand you've been waiting for.
