Akihabara News: The Hanvon WISEreader is considered to be the world's first color ebook reader incorporating the newly announced Triton color E Ink display. The Triton display is 9.68 inch in size and displays 4096 colors. You can touch it too. The OS of choice is Windows CE 6.0 powered by a Freescale CPU. There's WiFi BG and optional 3G. From what I can see the color isn't all that bad.
