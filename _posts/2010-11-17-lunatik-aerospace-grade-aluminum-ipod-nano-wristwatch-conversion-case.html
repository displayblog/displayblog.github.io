Scott Wilson, founder of Chicago-based MINIMAL, on the LunaTik:

LunaTik is designed and intended to be a premium conversion kit for someone that wants to dedicate their Nano to being a watch and the newest conversation piece with their friends. It is forged from Aerospace Grade Aluminum and then machined via CNC into its final form.

In addition: high-grade silicone rubber straps, solid stainless steel hardware and built in the same factories that make Nixon, Diesel, Vestal, and Nike.

Beautiful, isn't it? The LunaTik will be shipping to KickStarter backers by mid-January 2011. Can't wait.
