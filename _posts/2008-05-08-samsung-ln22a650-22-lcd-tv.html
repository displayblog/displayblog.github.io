Samsung LN22A650

Size: 22.05"
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1680 x 1050
Contrast Ratio: 3000:1
Viewing Angle: 170/160
Response Time: 8ms
Image Processing: DNIe+
Input: S-Video, Composite, HDMI, VGA
Dimension: 22.2" x 17.8" x 8.5" (Width x Height x Depth, without stand)
Weight: 12.8lbs (without stand)
Price: $649.99

Samsung's LN22A650 is a 22" LCD TV. Yes, a 22" LCD TV. The size is rather unique. And there is a good reason for that. You see, the LN22A650, despite the "650" number, uses a LCD monitor panel. Don't believe me? Check out the pixel format: 1680 x 1050. Ever seen that before? I bet you have, on almost all 22" LCD monitors. Now the Touch of Color design is cool, without a doubt. But stick a LCD monitor panel in there that's slow as molasses, do a TOC makeover, add a bit of image processing technology (DNIe+) and some CE input (like HDMI and Composite) and you get a cool LCD TV? No. You get a decent TN+Film (not-so-good viewing angle) based 22" LCD monitor that's way overpriced. If you're going to get this, just get the T220, which is a TOCized 22" LCD monitor with DVI for about $320 (PriceGrabber).
