BTC Zeus 7000 240MA-8FD(P) Deluxe
Size: 24" (61cm)
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1200
Brightness: 450 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio: 1,000:1 (typical), 10,000:1 (dynamic)
Response Time: 6ms (GTG)
LCD Technology: S-PVA
Viewing Angle: 178/178
Color Gamut: 92% NTSC
Input: HDMI with HDCP, DVI-D, VGA, Composite, Component, S-Video
Dimension: 674.4 x 438.2 x 165mm
Weight: 8.6kg
Price: KRW649,000

South Korea-based BTC let loose its Zeus 7000 240MA-8FD(P) Deluxe (what a name!) 24" LCD monitor and it looks pretty well designed on the outside.

The specifications look pretty impressive too, especially the 10,000:1 dynamic contrast ratio. The fact that the Zeus 7000 240MA uses a Super Patterned Vertical Alignment (S-PVA) LCD panel from Samsung suggests that at least the picture quality is going to be very good.

Source: AVING, BTC
