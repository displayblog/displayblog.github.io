B&O just unveiled its stunningly huge BeoVision 4-103. The BeoVision 4-103 is a 103" plasma TV but with a special feature:

... it is the only giant TV to come with a stand that can lift the TV at the touch of a button and also turn and tilt to the optimal viewing position. The screen itself will rest only inches above the floor when the TV is not in use, thus reducing its prominence in the room and concealing all integrated applications.


It's best just to see this massive 103" plasma TV in action, and I mean it physically moving up and down. I'm sure the on-screen performance will be absolutely stunning. The frame is made of high-grade aluminum. And the price? Probably as expensive as the new fuel-efficient car you just bought.

A little update: The price tag on the BeoVision 4-103 is stratospheric. For a wall mounted version it will be just US$93,050. You'll need to return your Tesla Roadster so you can pay $111,805 for the version with the motorized stand.

Source: B&O
