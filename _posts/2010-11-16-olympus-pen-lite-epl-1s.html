Olympus (Japan) → Wired: Olympus made some small changes to the PEN EPL-1 and now offers you its PEN Lite EPL-1s. Maximum ISO is doubled from 3200 to 6400 and overall weight was reduced a bit.

The big change is a new M.ZUIKO DIGITAL 14-42mm II Æ’3.5-5.6 kit lens, which is 25% lighter (112g) and sports a silent, faster, internal-focus AF motor. The display is a 2.7-inch "Hyper Crystal LCD". The EPL-1s is a Japan-only affair for now and will launch on December 4th.
