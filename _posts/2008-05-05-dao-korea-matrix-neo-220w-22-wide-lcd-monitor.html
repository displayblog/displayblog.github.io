Dao Korea Matrix neo-220W

Size: 22"
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1680 x 1050
Brightness: 300 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio: 7,000:1
Response Time: 5ms
Viewing Angle: 160/160
Price: KRW259,000 (US$256.56)

I guess Dao Korea has a thing for The Matrix and its main character Neo! Dao Korea's Matrix neo-220W is a 22" wide LCD monitor with a nice glossy black exterior. The LCD is of the TN+Film variety as is the case with most 22" LCD monitors. The Matrix neo-220W though as a very high contrast ratio of 7,000:1.
