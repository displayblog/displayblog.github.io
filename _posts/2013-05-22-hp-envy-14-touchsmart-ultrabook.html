The Verge: HP continues to liberally borrow design elements from Apple, but the 14-inch HP Envy 14 TouchSmart Ultrabook goes much further when it comes to pixels: 3200x1800 is an option. That's good for a resolution of 262 ppi, which is higher than the Chromebook Pixel (239 ppi), the 13-inch retina MacBook Pro (227 ppi), and the KIRAbook (221 ppi).
