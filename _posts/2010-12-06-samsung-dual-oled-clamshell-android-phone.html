Akihabara News: Samsung has a new clamshell Android 2.2 smartphone with two 3.3-inch Super AMOLED displays: the W899. Unfortunately it is limited to the Chinese market. Each Super AMOLED sports 800x480 PenTile Matrix pixels. Other specs include: 5 megapixel camera, 720p HD video capture, 1GHz processor and both CDMA and GSM basebands. The W899 is available on China Telecom. You can see the W899 working in the launch ceremony video.
