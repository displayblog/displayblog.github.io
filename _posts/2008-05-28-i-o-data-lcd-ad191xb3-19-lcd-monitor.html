Most likely the I-O Data LCD-AD191XB3 will go for much much less than $360 in the US, if it gets here. The LCD-AD191XB3 is a 19" wide LCD monitor with the usual pixel format of 1440 x 900. I think there is an opportunity for all manufacturers of 19" wide LCD monitors to offer an upgraded 1680 x 1050 version, similar to what Viewsonic did with its VX1962WM. There is really nothing special about the LCD-AD191XB3 with ho-hum specifications. The high price is a deal-killer, but I think the price will be quite aggressive at sub-$200 in the US. And at that price, the LCD-AD191XB3 could compete as the entry-level 19" LCD monitor to look at if all you want is a workable 19" wide LCD monitor.
