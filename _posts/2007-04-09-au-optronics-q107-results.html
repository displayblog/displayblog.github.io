AUO announced Q1'07 financial results on April 9. Preliminary consolidated March revenues were NT$29.572 billion and unconsolidated net sales totaled NT$29.566 billion representing an increase of 30.3% and 30.4% M/M, respectively, and 31.7% Y/Y for both.

Q1'07 unaudited consolidated and unconsolidated revenues totaled NT$80.72 billion and NT$80.694 billion, gaining 21.8% Y/Y. Large-area LCD panel shipments in March were 5.89 million units, a gain of 29.6% M/M. Small/medium LCD panel shipments totaled 9.2 million, up 52.8% M/M. In Q1'07 shipments of large-area LCD panels were 15.94 million, gaining 70.0% Q/Q while small/medium LCD panel shipments grew 39.9% Y/Y to 22.13 million.
