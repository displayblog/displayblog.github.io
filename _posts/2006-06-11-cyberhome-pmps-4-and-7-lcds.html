Cyberhome will be using Philips' Nexperia mobile multimedia processors, the PNX0190, which supports Adobe Flash and DVB-T in its portable multimedia players (PMPs) that will sport either a 7" or 8" display and a 4". The device has MPEG2, WMV9, MPEG4 and DivX playback capabilities.
