Hitachi:

  • Size: 4.5 inch (11 cm)
  • Dimension: 55.512x98.688 mm
  • Pixel Format: 1280x720 RGB
  • 2D Brightness: 400 nits
  • 3D Brightness: 470 nits

I don't give a hoot about 3D, but the 1280x720 pixel structure, now that's exciting. It is rumored that Google's next Nexus phone, the Nexus Prime, will sport a Samsung-built Super AMOLED HD display, it too with a 1280x720 pixel format.

1280x720 seems to be the next milestone for smartphone displays.
