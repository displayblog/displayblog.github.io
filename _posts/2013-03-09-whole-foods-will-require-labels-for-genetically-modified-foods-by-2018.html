Andrew Webster, The Verge:

Grocery chain Whole Foods has announced that in five years time it will require labels for all products containing genetically modified organisms (GMO), a first among major American retailers.

Not soon enough. Seven Whole Foods locations in the UK already have this requirement. Kudos to the UK.
