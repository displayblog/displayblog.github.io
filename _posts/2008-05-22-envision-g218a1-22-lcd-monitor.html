Envision G218a1
Size: 22"
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1680 x 1050
Contrast Ratio: 700:1
Response Time: 5ms
Input: VGA, DVI-D with HDCP

Envision's G218a1 is a very affordable 22" LCD monitor. Like most 22" LCD monitors (Lenovo's ThinkVision L220x being one of the few exceptions), the G218a1 features a pixel format of 1680 x 1050. You can use this LCD monitor as a TV to watch content protected HD sources via its HDCP enabled DVI connection. Overall PC World seems to have liked the performance of the G218a1 and gave it a 75 out of 100. If you're looking for a 22" LCD monitor and want to use it as a TV from time to time in a no-frills econo-box style, the G218a1 might be just what you need.

Source: PCWorld
