Adding the functionality of fast app switching and backgrounding (not to mention some of the interface tweaks and tightening that seems to have been done) to the iPad finally makes the tablet feel more like a real alternative to a laptop.

You mean netbook. Other updates include: unified inbox, threaded messaging, folders (20 items per), brightness/orientation control, Game Center, etc.
