Nokia's 8800 mobile phone has been upgraded with some bling, some diamond bling, and 112 of them. Thomas Heyerdahl will craft just 100 of these diamond editions of the Nokia 8800 and each one will be numbered. The 112 diamonds will add up to 0.7 carats. The price? Just $5,933 gets you a VIP pass to be part of a group of just 100 proud owners. Number 1 of the diamond edition will be auctioned off.
