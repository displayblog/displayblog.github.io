Aiptek's PocketCinema V10 is a pico projector based on 3M's miniature projection technology that can generate an amazingly large 50" image. Other cool features of the V10 is its 1GB of internal memory and a 3-in-1 multicard reader (SD, MMC, MS Pro) so you can present JPEG, ASF, MPEG4 and AVI content without having to connect it to anything else. A battery and stereo speakers are included, of course.
