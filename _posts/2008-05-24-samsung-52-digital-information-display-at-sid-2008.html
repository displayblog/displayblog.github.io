Samsung 52" Digital Information Display (DID)

  • Size: 52"
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9
  • Pixel Format: 1920x1080
  • Brightness: 700 cd/m2
  • Contrast Ratio: >2,000:1
  • Viewing Angle: 180/180 (wow)
  • Other: Dual Orientation (landscape, portrait), Narrow Bezel Technology

Samsung showcased a 2x4 video wall composed of eight 52" digital information displays. Considering each 52" LCD has 2.07MPs the entire video wall has a total of 16.59MPs! I would like that in my office. Contrast ratio is an exceptional 2,000:1 and Samsung claims that viewing angle is virtually 180 all around. Wow.
