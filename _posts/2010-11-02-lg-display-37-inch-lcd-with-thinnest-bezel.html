Digital Daily (Korean) → OLED-Display.net → Engadget: LG Display (LGD) showcased its 37-inch LCD with a 2.5mm bezel on the bottom/right with the top/left edges sporting a bezel of just 1.5mm. The world's thinnest bezeled LCD is geared for digital signage applications and will be on display during FPD International 2010 in Japan.
