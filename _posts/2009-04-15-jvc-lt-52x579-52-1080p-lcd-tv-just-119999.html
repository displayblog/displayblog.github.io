JVC LT-52X579 Specifications

Display: 52" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 2000:1 (native)
Brightness: 500 cd/m2
Response Time: 6ms
Viewing Angles: 176/176
Tuner: ATSC, NTSC
Connectivity: USB, Component (3), HDMI with HDCP (3), S-Video, Composite (2)
Dimensions:
49.5 x 32.5 x 4.6" (w/o stand)
49.5 x 34.5 x 13.63" (w/stand)
Weight: 65.3lbs (w/o stand), 74.8lbs (w/stand)
Power Consumption: 130.8W (operational), 0.3W (standby) as measured by Energy Star
Price: MSRP US$2999 ($1199 at Dell)

Dell's Small & Medium Business store has JVC's LT-52X579 for just $1,199.99 after instant savings of $800. The LT-52X579 sports a 52" TFT LCD featuring a pixel format of 1920 x 1080. Brightness is a standard 500 cd/m2; response time is a bit on the slow side at 6ms (I like to see <5ms); and there are three HDCP compliant HDMI connections--one more on such a huge TV would have been better. Sports fans watch out. I don't think this TV has 120Hz so you will see some motion blur. But the bottom line is that you get a very decent 52" LCD TV for just $1199.
