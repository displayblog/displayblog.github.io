Mobile Intelligent Ubiquitous Technology (MIU) Hybrid Dual Portable Computer (HDPC) Specifications

Display: 4.1" TFT LCD and 2.4" Active Matrix OLED (AMOLED)
Pixel Format: 800 x 480 (4.1" TFT LCD), 320 x 240 (2.4" AMOLED)
Connectivity: WiFi (802.11b/g), GPS, HSDPA, USIM, DMB
Availability: South Korea
Price: About US$500

MIU's HDPC sports two displays. On the outside it has a 2.4" Active Matrix OLED (AMOLED) display with a pixel format of 320 x 240 (landscape). When you flip the display open a large 4.1" TFT LCD greats you that feature a pixel format of 800 x 480. You need all of those pixels because the HDPC runs Windows XP. The 800 horizontal pixels aren't optimal for web browsing since you'll be scrolling left and right quite a bit, but for a device this small it's definitely on the better side. You also have a QWERTY keyboard that is revealed. The HDPC sports a ton of features, which I won't go into detail here, but it is Intel Atom powered, has DDR RAM and enough display real estate to challenge the smaller netbooks out there.
