Acer's H233H is a very good-looking monitor. It sports a 23" TN LCD panel leading to slightly limited viewing angles (160/160). Response time is about average at 5ms; brightness at 300 cd/m2 will be bright enough for most; and there is a plethora of connections: VGA, HDMI and DVI. The 40,000:1 dynamic contrast ratio can be ignored. The H233H is a 16:9 aspect ratio LCD monitor with a pixel format of 1920 x 1080 so you can pipe 1080p HD content straight to it and watch the video in its full glory without any scaling. Of course, if you want to watch film such as on Blu-ray you'll still get black bars: on top and bottom. You'll need to get a 21:9 Philips TV for that.
