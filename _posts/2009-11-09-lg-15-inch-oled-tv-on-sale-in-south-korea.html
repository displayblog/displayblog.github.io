LG Electronic's 15-inch OLED TV is on sale in South Korea for KRW3 million (about US$2600). The 15-inch OLED TV is the world's largest and the second only commercially available OLED TV besting Sony's XEL-1 by 4 inches. The price is quite aggressive as the XEL-1 at just 11 inches is still commanding about $2000. Originally slated for sale in December, I guess LGE pulled it in by a month.
