Gigaom: The HP Stream 14 is a US$199 Windows 8.1 3.9-pound notebook. The price is right, but that's about as good as it gets:

  • The 200-nit 14-inch SVA LCD sports a pixel format of 1366x768.
  • RAM is limited to single-channel 2GB DDR3L-1333MHz.
  • Storage is eMMC NAND flash: 32GB or 64GB
  • Powered by an AMD A4 Micro-6400T CPU.
  • Cloud storage: 100GB of OneDrive for two years


My biggest question: Can Windows 8.1 run smoothly?
