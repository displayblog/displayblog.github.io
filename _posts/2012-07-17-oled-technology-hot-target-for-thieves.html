Sam Byford, The Verge:

The eleven people arrested on suspicion of illicitly selling OLED technology from Samsung have now been charged with theft.

Six from LG Display and three from Samsung Display have been charged. OLED display technology is a hot target: Orbotech was charged with stealing from both LG Display and Samsung Electronics.
