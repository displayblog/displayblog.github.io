JVC's Everio GZ-HD620 records in 1080p via its backside illuminated CMOS image sensor. The 1080p footage is recorded unto a 120GB hard drive or a microSDHC card. The GZ-HD620 only weighs 270g, which is very light. For example, Panasonic's latest Micro Four Thirds camera, the GF1, weighs 285g and that's just the body without a lens. Add the 20mm lens and the weight balloons to 448g. The GZ-HD620 is definitely a lightweight. At the maximum bit rate of 24Mbps the 120GB hard drive can record up to 11 hours. Need more time?
