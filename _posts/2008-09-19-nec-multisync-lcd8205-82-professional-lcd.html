NEC MultiSync LCD8205 Specifications

Display: 82" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 5000:1
Brightness: 600 cd/m2
Response Time: 8ms
Viewing Angle: 178/178
Connectivity: DVI, HDMI, Composite, S-Video, Component, RS-232
Other: TileMatrix (4x4)
Availability: December 2008
Price: US$54,999

NEC's LCD8205 is an LCD display geared for the professional markets and will be showcased during the 2008 Digital Signage Expo East. The LCD8205 is huge: 82". It also has a huge price: $54,999. Usually very large TFT LCDs are designed for either landscape use or portrait use. NEC states that you can use the LCD8205 in either orientation. I wonder how they were able to do that since there are some design limitations and long-term stability issues. The LCD8205 has a 1920 x 1080 pixel format and a contrast ratio of 5000:1. With NEC's TileMatrix technology 16 of these gigantic displays can be tiled together in a 4 x 4 format.
