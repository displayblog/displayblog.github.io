Sewell's Minideck USB to DVI, VGA and HDMI Display Adapter makes use of DisplayLink's DL-195 chip that provides up to a resolution of 2048 x 1152, a significant improvement over previous generation USB-to-DVI/VGA adapters that maxed out at 1600 x 1200 or 1680 x 1050. Price is US$99.95. Source: Sewell via Engadget
