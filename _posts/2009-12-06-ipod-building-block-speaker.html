There are many tech gifts, too many actually. But there are few really nifty ones. iGadget Life lists a few budget-friendly ones and the iPod Building Block Speaker caught my eye. It adds a playful design to the ultra-sleek high-tech iconic MP3 player today, Apple's iPod. The iPod Building Block Speaker is priced at just US$14.99 at Amazon. A great stocking stuffer for not a lot of money.

Compatible with iPod mini, iPod touch (1st gen), iPod nano (1st, 2nd, 3rd gen), iPod (3rd, 4th, 5th gen), iPod Classic.
