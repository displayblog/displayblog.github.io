MSI's X600 Pro is very thin at just 2.5 cm thick. That would be a little less than 1-inch thick. The 15.6-inch CULV notebook PC weighs just 2.1 kg or 4.6 pounds. ATI's Mobility Radeon HD 4330 with 512MB is tasked with GPU duties. Other specs include: HDMI, WiFi BGN, WiMAX (optional), Bluetooth, DVD Super-Multi / Blu-ray, Windows 7. Personally, I don't like offset keyboards and the X600 Pro has one. I generally don't need a numeric keypad but some others might. Source: Akihabara News
