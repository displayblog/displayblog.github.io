Toshiba Satellite M300 Romantic Red Specifications:

  • Display: 14.1" Wide TFT LCD
  • Webcam: Face Recognition
  • GPU: ATI Radeon X1250 or Intel GMA X3100
  • CPU: AMD Turion Dual Core or Intel Core 2 Duo
  • RAM: 2GB DDR2 667MHz SDRAM
  • HDD: 250GB
  • Optical: DVD SuperMulti DL with Labelflash
  • External Storage: 9-in-1 Multicard Reader
  • Connectivity: WiFi, USB (Sleep-and-Charge)
  • Battery: 3700mAh or 4000mAh
  • Price: KRW1,100,000

Toshiba: The Toshiba Satellite M300 Romantic Red edition is available in the South Korean market. An interesting feature of the M300 is the facial recognition capability via the webcam. The M300 sports a 14.1" wide TFT LCD and is powered by either an ATI Radeon X1250 or an Intel GMA X3100 GPU. Another unique feature is the Sleep-and-Charge USB where you can charge your mobile devices via the USB port even while the M300 is sleeping. Besides these nifty little features and the fact that the color is "Romantic Red", there isn't anything too special.
