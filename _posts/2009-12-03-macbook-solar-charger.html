Are you often outside in the sun with not-enough battery power on your MacBook? QuickerTek Inc. has a solution for you: Apple Juicz, a solar charger. There is a US$700 27 Watt model and a 55 Watt one that costs $1200. Quite expensive. It takes some time to charge your MacBook from as little as 6 hours to as much as 12. If you absolutely need to recharge your MacBook and you're far far away from a power outlet, maybe the Apple Juicz can help. Source: QuickerTek via The New York Times
