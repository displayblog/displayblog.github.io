Multiple sites are conjecturing that Apple will unveil a 11.6-inch MacBook Air in next week's "Back to the Mac" event. This smaller, lighter MacBook Air is expected to be more affordable, too. The current 13.3-inch MacBook Air starts at $1499.

The pixel format will probably remain the same at 1280x800. But I wonder if the 11.6-inch LCD will be of the IPS variety...
