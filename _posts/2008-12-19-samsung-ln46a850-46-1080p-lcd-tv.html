Samsung LN46A850 Specifications

Display: 45.9" PVA (Patterned Vertical Alignment) TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 50,000:1 (dynamic)
Viewing Angles: 178/178
Response Time: 4ms
Video Processing: DNIe
Frequency: 120Hz
Tuner: ATSC, NTSC
Connectivity: HDMI (4), Composite (2), VGA, Component (2), Anynet, Wiselink Pro
Dimensions:
44.1 x 29.3 x 11.1" (w/stand)
44.1 x 27.3 x 1.9" (w/o stand)
Weight: 55.1lbs (w/stand), 46.1lbs (w/o stand)
Price: MSRP US$2599.99 ($1799.98 at Amazon.com)

Samsung's LN46A850, a 46" LCD TV, has everything. Well almost. The 46" LCD TV sports a 1920 x 1080 pixel format allowing for unscaled 1080p HD input. The four HDMI ports should be plenty for most of you out there: satellite TV, PlayStation 3, Slingbox, Apple TV... connected them all. The PVA (Patterned Vertical Alignment) LCD panel from Samsung has some very good features such as a 50,000:1 dynamic contrast ratio, excellent viewing angles (178/178), a fast response time (4ms), a clean design that's fairly thin at just under 2".

The LN46A850 also has a 120Hz frequency: double that of normal ones. 120Hz reduces motion blur considerably and that means if you're the type that likes this equation: TV = ESPN, then you'll enjoy this Samsung TV. The fast movements will be crystal clear without motion blur.

There is just one thing missing to make this LN46A850 a perfect TV: a LED backlight. But you do save a bit of money without it. So what will you miss? With a LED backlight you get significantly better contrast. But even without a LED backlight the LN46A850 packs in a lot of ooomph. With power comes... a hefy price. The MSRP on this beautiful 46" LCD TV is $2599.99; Amazon.com has it for just $1799.98 and that's with free shipping.

Source: Amazon.com, Samsung
