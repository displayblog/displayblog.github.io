Runco Crystal Series CX-70DHD Specifications
Display: 70" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Connectivity: HDMI with HDCP, Composite, S-Video, Component
Video Processing: DHD Vivix II
Availability: Mid-October 2008
Pricing: MSRP US$34,995

During CEDIA 2008, Runco unveiled a massive LCD TV. The company's Crystal Series CX-70DHD is a 70" behemoth and is Runco's largest LCD TV to date. Runco unveiled its Optical Path Alignment (OPAL) image enhancement technology that allows for great pictures in high ambient light environments. The DHD video processor is on board that uses the Vivix II digital video processing technology that improves all digital and NTSC content to near HD quality. VirtualWide mode converts all 4:3 content into 16:9 while optimizing Anamorphic and Letterbox content too, and without loss of image integrity.
