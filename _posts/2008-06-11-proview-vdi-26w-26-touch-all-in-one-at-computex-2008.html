Proview VDI-26W Specifications
Display: 26" Touch TFT LCD
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1200 (?)
Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
Connectivity: VGA Input/Output, Ethernet (2), USB 2.0 (4), Audio In/Out/Speaker
Other: Wireless Pen

High-tech showcases during Computex 2008 keep popping out. This time it's a rather large 26" all-in-one PC from Proview. It is touch enabled as you can see from the picture and has a very nice slim design. Most of the connections are tucked away via a small box attached to the back while a headphone jack and VGA connector is on the left side of the 26" LCD. I really like the idea of reaching out and touching the LCD for input, drawing, etc. The only limitation to this is the operating system. If only the iPhone's touch UI were implemented on a regular touch-enabled PC...
