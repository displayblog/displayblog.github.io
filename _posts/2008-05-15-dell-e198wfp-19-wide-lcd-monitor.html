Dell E198WFP
Size: 19" Wide
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1440 x 900
Response Time: 5ms
Brightness: 300 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
Viewing Angle: 160/160
Input: VGA, DVI-D
Power Consumption: 38W (operational), 2W (standby/sleep)
Price: $169 (Advance Computer)

I was looking for some ultra-cheap 19" LCD monitors and came upon Dell's E198WFP at Advance Computer for just $169.00. I believe it is brand new. Compared to Gateway's FPD1975W, the E198WFP is has much better specifications for just $20 more (before shipping and handling). The response times are pretty quick at 5ms; the viewing angle is standard at 160/160 for a TN LCD panel; and contrast ratio of 1000:1 is downright great considering the price! If you're not too keen on purchasing something from small shops, Amazon has the E198WFP for $184.95.

Source: Advance Computer
