Dell S2409W Specifications

Display: 24" Wide TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
LCD Technology: TN
Backlight: CCFL
Color Gamut: 72% NTSC
Response Time: 5ms
Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
Brightness: 300 cd/m2
Viewing Angles: 170/160
Connectivity: HDMI with HDCP, DVI with HDCP, VGA

Dell is moving toward 16:9 in a big way by introducing a 24" LCD monitor with a 16:9 aspect ratio. The pixel format typically found in 24" LCD monitors is 1920 x 1200 with a 16:10 aspect ratio, but to get to 16:9 you need to make it a bit shorter (or wider) by lopping off 120 vertical pixels. The result? 1920 x 1080, the same as 1080p LCD TVs. Don't expect great viewing angles because this 24" uses a TN LCD technology, but the viewing angles aren't so bad at 170/160. Other than the 16:9 aspect ratio, there isn't that much to write home about.

Source: Dell via TFT Central
