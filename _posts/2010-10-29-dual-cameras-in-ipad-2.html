Detweiler Fenton → Barron's → MacRumors: OmniVision Technologies will be supplying two image sensors for the next iPad: a rear-facing 5-megapixel and a front-facing VGA (640x480). I'm guessing the next iPad will probably be called iPad 2, which is expected to launch in the first quarter of 2011.
