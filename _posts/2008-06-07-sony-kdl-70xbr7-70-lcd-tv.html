Sony KDL-70XBR7 Specifications
Display: 70" 10-bit LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Refresh Rate: 120Hz Motionflow
Backlight: WCG-CCFL
Video Processing: 10-bit BRAVIA Engine 2 PRO
Contrast Ratio: Advanced Contrast Enhancer (ACE)
Color Gamut: x.v.Color
Input: HDMI (4, 1080/60p, 24p), Component (2, 1080/60p), VGA (1080/60p), Ethernet, RS-232C
Other: DMe, XMB
Availability: Fall 2008


Note: As far as I can tell the XBR 7 and the XBR 8 external design is similar if not identical.

Sony's KDL-70XBR7 is the largest LCD TV you can buy from Sony. The KDL-70XBR7 has everything the XBR 8 series has except for the TRILUMINOS RGB LED backlight, which is a big deal since that provides an enhanced color gamut as well as local dimming capabilities (Advanced Contrast Enhancer - ACE) to improve contrast ratio to insane levels.

But if big is your thing, then the KDL-70XBR7 is it, there is nothing bigger. Of course, the 70" monster also has ACE to improve contrast but is limited to a wide color gamut CCFL (WCG-CCFL).

The KDL-70XBR7 incorporates the most advanced video processing engine: BRAVIA Engine 2 PRO, just like the XBR 8 series. This differentiates it from the smaller 40" KDL-XBR7 that uses the BRAVIA Engine 2 EX. For for these later in the fall.

Source: Sony
