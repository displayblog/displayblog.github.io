The iLiad features USB connectivity, a CF Type-II slot, a SD/MMC slot, a stereo audio output jack (for listening to music?), a 802.11g receiver and a 10/100 Ethernet jack. The LCD is of a bistable variety with technology most likely coming from E-Ink and is 8.1" in size diagonally. The display has touch capability and has 1024 x 768 resolution.
