Akihabara News: The BenQ nReader K60 is a 6-inch SiPix-based ebook reader sporting a 800x600 resolution, will be available in Asia starting January 2010. The nReader supports a variety of formats including ePub, PDF, TXT and HTML. Like other ebook readers the nReader can playback audio as well as pictures.
