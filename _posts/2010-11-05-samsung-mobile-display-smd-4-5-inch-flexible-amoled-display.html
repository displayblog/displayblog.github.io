Samsung Mobile Display Blog (Korean): Samsung Mobile Display (SMD) will be unveiling a 4.5-inch flexible AMOLED display at FPD International 2010. The pixel format is 800x480 with a folding radius of just 1cm, which is considerably less than the typical 5cm.
