MobileCrunch: Here are the quick stats on the Sharp Lynx 3D SH-03C: 3.8-inch TFT LCD, 800x480, 1GHz Snapdragon, 9.6 megapixel camera, 720p video recording, 1-Seg TV tuner, e-wallet, Android 2.1 with 2.2 update in early 2011.

You don't need 3D glasses to view 3D on the Lynx 3D SH-03C so that's convenient. The 3D is based on a special type of parallax barrier that controls the liquid crystal to separate images causing the different images to be seen by the left and right eyes. The video doesn't do a good-enough job showing off the 3D on the Lynx 3D SH-03C but from my experiences looking at parallax barrier-based 3D I don't think the 3D experience will be compelling.
