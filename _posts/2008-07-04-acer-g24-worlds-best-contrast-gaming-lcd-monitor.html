Acer G24 Specifications

Display: 24" Wide TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1200
Contrast Ratio: 50,000:1 (dynamic)
Brightness: 400 cd/m2
Response Time: 2ms
Connectivity: DVI, HDMI

Acer's G24 24" LCD monitor has a lot going for it. It has a super fast 2ms response time and has a very bright 400 cd/m2. The pixel format is a standard 1920 x 1200 with both DVI and HDMI ports. But what puts the G24 over the top is its contrast ratio. The dynamic contrast ratio is rated at 50,000:1!!! That is as good as some really high-end LCD TVs! I'm sure gaming on this super fast and super contrasty 24" LCD monitor will be something else!
