Sony BRAVIA KDL-37M4000 Specifications

Need a 37" LCD TV? But don't want to spend a bunch of money? Beach Camera has the KDL-37M4000 from Sony for just $749! The 37" LCD TV from Sony sports a 1366 x 768 pixel format, a contrast ratio of 2500:1 (good) and with two HDMI ports. Isn't 1366 x 768 outdated? I say no. Most people can't tell the difference between 1366 x 768 and 1920 x 1080 at the distance they are suppose to watch TV. You can see the difference if you get close enough. So if you're intending to use this as a monitor, then you would notice.

The two HDMI ports should allow for you to hook up a gaming console and a cable box (or satellite). The VGA connection can come in handy if you're planning on connecting a PC to the KDL-37M4000. The MSRP on the KDL-37M4000 is US$1199 so the price you get from Beach Camera at the moment for just $749 is a great deal on a 37" LCD TV, especially from Sony.
