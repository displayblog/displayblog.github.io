Akihabara News: The Gigabyte BookTop T1125 is a 11.6-inch convertible notebook PC that can also be docked and used as a desktop. The T1125 can be equipped with either an i3 or i5 CPU with other specs including: 2GB RAM, HDMI, NVIDIA Optimus, USB 3.0. The three-in-one (tablet, notebook, desktop) runs Windows 7. The BookTop T1125 will be priced around US$1300 and is expected to be available in Taiwan on November 20th.
