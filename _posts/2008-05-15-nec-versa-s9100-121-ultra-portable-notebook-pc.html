NEC Versa S9100
Display Size: 12.1" Wide
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1280 x 800
Backlight: LED
GPU: Intel GMA X3100
Wireless: 802.11b/g/n
Weight: 1.25kg (about 3lbs)
Battery: 6-cell Lithium Ion rated at 6 hours.
CPU: 1.2GHz LV Intel Core 2 Duo U7600
RAM: 2GB
HDD: 160GB 5400RPM Shock-resistant
Price: About US$3500

NEC's Versa S9100 features a magnesium alloy chassis that can withstand 300kg of force to prevent damage to the LCD. The HDD is protected by a "Free Fall Sensor" that detects when it is being dropped, bumped or excessively vibrated and parks the HDD heads. This little 12.1" ultra-portable notebook PC packs an internal DL DVD burner and comes standard with 2GB of RAM. There is also a fingerprint biometric sensor and a Trusted Platform Module (TPM) security chip for protecting your data on the S9100.

The LED backlight allows the S9100 to have a super-thin LCD module and will help in lowering power consumption. Also LED, due to it being a solid state lighting device, is much more durable than CCFL. The 6-hour rating of the Lithium Ion battery is excellent and I hope in real life it will go on for at least 5 hours.

Source: PC World New Zealand, TechWorld
