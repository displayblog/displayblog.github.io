VIZIO's M-Series 4K Ultra HD TVs start at 43 inches and a penny less than US$600. Here are some tech specs:

  • Backlight: Full Array LED, 28 Local Dimming Zones
  • Refresh Rate: 120Hz
  • Viewing Angle: 178/178
  • Wireless Connectivity: 802.11ac
  • Wired Connectivity: 5x HDMI (4x UHD 30Hz, 1x UHD 60Hz), 1x Component, 1x Composite, 1x Ethernet, 1x USB

VIZIO's decision to use a full array LED backlight is impressive considering the $600 price point for a 4K TV.
