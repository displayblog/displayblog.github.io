There is an interesting rumor floating around: that Apple will introduce an iPhone Nano at MacWorld 2009. The iPhone Nano would follow the tradition of the original iPod where a smaller and slightly cheaper version is introduced with a Nano suffix. The iPhone Nano, as the picture (mock up probably, via MacRumors) shows, is much smaller than the iPhone and will probably fit those who have smaller hands quite a bit better than the largish iPhone. If in fact the iPhone Nano does get introduced next week, it wouldn't be surprising to see an iPod Touch Nano in the near future as well since it would use the same chassis. Speaking of chassis, will we see an unibody aluminum iPhone?

Source: MacRumors, XSKN
