Samsung R710 Specifications

Display: 17" Wide TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1200
GPU: 512MB NVIDIA GeForce 9600M GT
CPU: 2.53GHz Intel Core 2 Duo P9500, 6MB L2 Cache, 1066MHz FSB
RAM: 4GB DDR3 1066MHz

Samsung seems to be unifying its design philosophy. Case in point: its new Eclipse R710 notebook PC. The R710 is a handsome notebook PC as it is but with the Touch of Color design theme applied to it, the R710 gets a bit of flare. The specifications are very impressive and feature a very fast CPU as well as GPU and has a 17" wide TFT LCD sporting a pixel format of 1920 x 1200. Add to all of this a whopping 4GB of super-fast DDR3 1006MHz RAM and you've got yourself a powerhouse!

The only thing though... I like to be right in the center of a computer display when I work or play. Being forced to the left of center to type is something I'd rather not experience. The R710 forces me to do that and in turn I am forced to abandon the R710 as a possibility for me.
