What is holding up the white iPhone 4? The original batch of white iPhone 4s were shipped to Apple headquarters with the button and the faceplate having different shades of white.

In John Sculley: Steve Jobs' Strategy that was posted yesterday, two of the many secrets to Jobs' success is perfectionism and sweating the details. So it isn't surprising that differing shades of white would need to be corrected, and perfected.
