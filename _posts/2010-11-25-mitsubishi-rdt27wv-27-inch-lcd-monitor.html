Akihabara News: The Mitsubishi RDT27WV is a 27-inch LED-backlit LCD monitor sporting a pixel format of 1920x1080, viewing angles of 178/178, brightness of 300 cd/m2, response time of 6.5ms, and a lot of connectivity options: HDMI (2), D5, DVI-D, and VGA. Priced at ¥52,800 the RDT27WV will be available on November 30th. At 27 inches I expected a pixel format of 2560x1440.
