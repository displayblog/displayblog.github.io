LG's Flatron W2363V is a 23-inch 1080p LCD monitor sporting a 1920 x 1080 resolution and featuring a 1000:1 contrast ratio, 300 cd/m2 of brightness, and a very fast 2ms GTG (Gray-to-Gray) response time. Connectivity options include HDMI (2), VGA, DVI-D and component video. There are two unfortunate things with the W2363V: the LCD is of the TN variety that means limited viewing angles (170/160), significant color shifts when viewing at angles. The other is a massively distracting sound bar of sorts underneath the LCD. I would think a sound bar should emit sound and let the LCD be in control of emitting light.

Source: Akihabara News, LG UK
