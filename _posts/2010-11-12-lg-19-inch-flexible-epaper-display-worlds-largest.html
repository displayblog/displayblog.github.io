Akihabara News: LG claims its 19-inch flexible e-paper display is the world's largest. The black and white e-paper is just 0.6-mm thick and uses stainless steel as the substrate. The pixel format is quite impressive at 2560x1600 resulting in a resolution of 163 PPI. Gray levels are limited to 16 and the contrast ratio is 7:1.
