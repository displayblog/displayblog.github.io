The Micron V100 LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) pico projector pumps out five lumens of light in a 320x240 pixel format. Power consumption is rated at just 1.5 watts for the entire module that includes all display processing to project images. The tiny V100 measures just 33x31x12 mm. Production is slated for end of 2010 with the cost expected to be less than US$30. Source: AV Watch (Google translated from Japanese to English)
