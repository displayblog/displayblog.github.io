Apple's iPhone will debut in South Korea on November 28, 2009 on Korea Telecom (KT). The price for a 32GB iPhone 3GS tops out at KRW396,000 (about US$340) with a KRW45,000 ($39) per month plan. iPhone prices depend on the monthly plan. For instance, the i-Premium plan costs KRW95,000 ($82) per month but with that the cost of the 32GB iPhone 3GS drops to KRW132,000 ($114). My gut feeling is that Apple and KT will generate considerable revenues from the iPhone in South Korea. Source: KT (Korean) via Yahoo, Engadget
