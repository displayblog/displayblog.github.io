The Verge: The Acer Iconia Tab A700 is a 10.1-inch Ice Cream Sandwich tablet with a 1920x1200 pixel format, like the Lenovo LePad K2010 / IdeaTab K2. Whether the LCD is of the IPS variety is unknown. The top-of-the-line ICS tablets seem to be converging toward 1920x1200 on a 10.1-inch LCD.

Update: The display is reported as being "IPS-quality", which means it might be Samsung's PLS.
