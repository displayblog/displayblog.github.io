Jeff Somogyi, Media Editor, dealnews.com:

10. They'll Also Want a Laptop, Too

True, they certainly will. I disagree with eight points that Somogyi makes, but #10 is compelling. For most students, the iPad won't work as a complete replacement for a full-blown computer like the MacBook Air. Instead, the iPad would work as a good add-on. But with many families facing financial uncertainty, certainty that tuitions will increase, and the desire to provide the right tools, I agree with the general conclusion that the iPad is not a necessity for students. If you had to go with only a single device, I would recommend one of the two MacBook Airs instead.
