MiNEW, a Korean brand, announced its N20U, a 10.1-inch netbook sporting a 1024 x 576 resolution, 1GB RAM, WiFi BGN and powered by a 1.6GHz Atom CPU. Storage options include either a 16GB or 32GB SSD. I like the simple angular design and the red accents. Source: Akihabara News
