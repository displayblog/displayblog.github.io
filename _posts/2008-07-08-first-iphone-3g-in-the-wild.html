iPhonePortugal has the first iPhone 3G. Upon looking carefully at the innards, it looks like the new slim power adapter is not included, probably because of the higher power requirements (220W). I am not planning to upgrade my iPhone, but I wouldn't mind purchasing the tiny power adapter for travel purposes.

An evolutionary step toward making the iPhone 3G customer even more happy would be to develop and include an in-ear stereo headset with mic. The regular for-iPod in-ear stereo headset is quite an improvement over the regular one. So I could imagine how much better listening and talking on an in-ear stereo headset with mic might be!
