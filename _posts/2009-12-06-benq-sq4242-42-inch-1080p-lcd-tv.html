BenQ's SQ4242 is a 42-inch 1080p LCD TV with the following specs:

  • Contrast Ratio: 5000:1
  • Brightness: 500 cd/m2
  • Response Time: 5.5ms
  • Format Support: 1080/24p
  • Connectivity: HDMI (3), Component, Composite, S-Video, VGA, DVI


The design is clean except for the unnecessary red line. The only thing that concerns me is I don't know if I can trust the BenQ brand for a TV. Availability and price are unknown at this time. Source: PRAD
