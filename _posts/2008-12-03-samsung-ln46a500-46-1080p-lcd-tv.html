Samsung LN46A500 Specifications

Display: 45.9" PVA TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 20,000:1 (dynamic)
Viewing Angle: 178/178
Response Time: 5ms
Video Processing: DNIe
Frequency: 60Hz
Connectivity: HDMI (2), Composite, VGA, Component (2)
Tuner: ATSC, NTSC
Dimensions:
43.5 x 30.2 x 12.8" (w/stand)
43.5 x 27.8 x 4.1" (w/o stand)
Weight: 76.1lbs (w/stand), 64.4lbs (w/o stand)

Samsung's LN46A500 is a 46" LCD TV with 1080p capability. It sports a 1920 x 1080 pixel format with a dynamic contrast ratio of 20,000:1. Viewing angles are excellent at 178/178 but you will see some contrast fall off as soon as you shift away from dead center. So make sure you're the one sitting in the middle! The 5ms response time is relative fast but without 120Hz the LN46A500 won't be the best choice for those who watch a lot of sports or movies with a lot of fast moving stuff. Motion blur will be evident. Another limitation is that there are only two HDMI ports, one short of average. If you need to connect more than a cable box and a Blu-ray drive, look elsewhere. The LN46A500 is a budget 46" LCD TV that will get you to 1080p. If you didn't like Samsung's Touch of Color then you'll like the simple black theme here on the LN46A500. I prefer simplicity and black is my color when it comes to LCD TVs.
