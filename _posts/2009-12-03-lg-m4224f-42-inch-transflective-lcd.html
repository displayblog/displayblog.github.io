The M4224F from LG is geared for digital signage applications. The 42-inch LCD makes use of an IPS transflective "High Performance Public Display Panel" that can easily be seen in sunlight. The M4224F sports a brightness of 400 cd/m2 with reflected light and 700 cd/m2 without. The digital signage display from LG can be used 24/7. Priced at US$2,390 the M4224F will be available in January 2010. Source: PRAD
