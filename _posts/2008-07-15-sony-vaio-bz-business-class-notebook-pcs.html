Sony VAIO BZ Specifications

Display: 15.4" Wide TFT LCD with LED Backlight
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Chipset: Centrino 2
RAM: Up to 4GB RAM
HDD: 320GB (Shock Protected)
Optical: DVD+R
Storage: Multicard Reader
Connectivity: USB (3), FireWire
Other: Magnesium Alloy Case, FreshStart (no crapware), Energy Star 4.0, EPEAT Silver, Spill Resistant Keyboard
Weight: 6lbs
Availability: Now
Price: Starts at around $1000

[caption id="attachment_1896" align="aligncenter" width="494" caption="Sony VAIO BZ Business Notebook PC"][/caption]

Sony's new VAIO BZ buxiness-class notebook PCs look very classy for the intended market segment. The design isn't as rugged-looking as those from Lenovo. And thankfully, the BZ notebook PCs aren't completely surrounded by glare materials. I think a lot of regular customers who are sick and tired of notebook PCs that reflect everything might get interested in a normal non-glare notebook.

The keyboard is spill resistant for all those times when we're trying to gulf down lunch while in front of the computer. And just in case you drop your BZ notebook while taking it out of the computer bag for a security check, don't worry, the HDD should be okay since it is shock protected.

The 15.4" LCD is a standard size but with a LED backlight, which is better for the environment (no mercury). The LED backlight also makes the enclosure thinner and saves a bit of energy. The price of just $1000 sounds very tempting.
