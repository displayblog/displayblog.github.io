A briefcase with a display! Not really. It's Shuttle's X50, an all-in-one PC. With a handle so you can move it around. The X50 sports a 15.6" wide TFT LCD with touch capabilities. You'll get to touch 1366 x 768 pixels and if you want to video chat, there's a 1.3MP webcam with a built-in microphone. The X50 is not the most powerful all-in-one with an Intel Atom powering the unit. There is just 1GB of RAM and a smallish 160GB HDD. The X50 does have something that most all-in-ones, even regular PCs, don't: five USB ports. The X50 is quite cute and has a very good price to boot: just US$500.
