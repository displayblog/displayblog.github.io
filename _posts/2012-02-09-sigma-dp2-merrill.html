DPReview: The Sigma DP2 Merrill sports a simple design, which I like. The specs include a 15.4 megapixel Foveon X3 APS-C CMOS image sensor with 30mm f2.8 lens. The only area where it falls short is video capture: 640x480 at 30fps.
