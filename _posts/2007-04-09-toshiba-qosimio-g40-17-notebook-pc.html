Toshiba's Qosimio G40 is a 17" notebook PC sporting a pixel format of 1920 x 1200. Introduced in May 2007, the G40 is geared toward the user who requires high performance. The huge 17" TFT LCD is driven by NVIDIA's GeForce 8600 GT GPU. The MSRP is US$2999. Performance is definitely not cheap.
