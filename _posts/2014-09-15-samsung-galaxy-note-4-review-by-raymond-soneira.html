An OLED display is the most color accurate display on a smartphone. Now that's something; OLED has come a long way from the days of overly contrasty and over saturated colors.

The 5.7-inch OLED display sports a pixel format of 2560x1440 good for a resolution of 515 pixels per inch. Compare that to the just announced iPhone 6 Plus: 5.5 inches, 1920x1080, 401 ppi. The iPhone 6 Plus doesn't even come close. It's not about specs? Sure it is, especially when you can see the difference.

There's a lot to like about the OLED display in the Samsung Galaxy Note 4. And that stylus is tempting too.
