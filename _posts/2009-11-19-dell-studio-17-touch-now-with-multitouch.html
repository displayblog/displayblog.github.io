Dell's Studio 17 Touch is an updated Studio 17 equipped with multitouch. The 17.3-inch LCD sports a 1600 x 900 resolution. Specs include: 2.1GHz Dual Core Pentium or up to Core i7, up to 8GB of RAM, up to a 1TB hard disk, integrated Intel or discrete ATI GPU, and an optional Blu-ray. Windows 7 is the OS. Price starts at US$899 and ships December 4.
