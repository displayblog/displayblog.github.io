Boy Genius Report → MacRumors: Sprint will be launching the ZTE Peel on November 14th. This is big news. Why? The Peel is a case for the iPod touch that gives it 3G. For US$29.99 per month for 1GB of data without a contract you can say adios to AT&T and to the insanely expensive and required voice + data plans for the iPhone. Everything is data so I don't know why we are being forced to pay for voice. I'd rather Skype and save a bunch of money. The Peel also has a five-device mobile hotspot capability.
