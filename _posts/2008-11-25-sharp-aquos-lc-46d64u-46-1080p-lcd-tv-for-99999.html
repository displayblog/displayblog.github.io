Sharp's AQUOS LC-46D64U is a 46" LCD TV that is 1080p capable. The LC-46D64U has a pixel format of 1920 x 1080 so 1080p content is displayed without any scaling. Scaling, no matter how good, degrades the quality of the HD video to some extent. The static contrast ratio of 2000:1 is very good while the relatively fast response time of 4ms should allow you to see some action without too much motion blur. For keen eyes motion blur on the LC-46D64U will be seen since it does not have 120Hz frequency or other technologies to minimize it. That said, the 46" LCD TV comes with a decent amount of connections, very good viewing angles and an extremely aggressive price at just $999.99: get it now while it lasts at Newegg.com.
