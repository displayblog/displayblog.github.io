Sonic Chair Version 3 Specifications

Display: 20" iMac, touch enabled
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1680 x 1050
GPU: ATI Radeon HD 2400 XT
CPU: 2.0GHz Intel Core 2 Duo
RAM: 1GB
HDD: 250GB
Optical: 8x SuperDrive
OS: OS X 10.5

Sonic Chair: The Sonic Chair... is simply a cool chair. The Version 3 adds another cool gadget: Apple's 20" iMac. But with a twist. The 20" iMac is touch-enabled. The whole idea is really cool except: how are you suppose to type? Maybe the iMac tilts (almost flat) so you can type directly on the display? Sounds and looks (in my mind) a bit awkward. Sonic Chair should have incorporated the "laptop sidetable" from Version 4 into this so you can have a table for your wireless aluminum keyboard. The Sonic Chair Version 3 is still quite cool.
