Brian Chen, Wired:

If you ever wanted a backup battery that plugs straight into your MacBook, now is your last chance to get one.

Appled sued HyperMac for violating the MagSafe design. So there will be no more MacBook charging cables coming with HyperMac's external battery. You've got until midnight November 2nd to get yours. via AppleInsider
