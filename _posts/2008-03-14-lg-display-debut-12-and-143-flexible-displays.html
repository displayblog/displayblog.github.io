LGD is expected to volume manufacture these flexible displays later in 2008. The flexible displays make use of an ultra-thin steel foil on a plastic substrate that allows them to be flexible.â€¨

Like the displays in Sony's ebook reader and Amazon's Kindle, the flexible displays are easy to read text off of. LGD stated that it has a wide viewing angle, but I'm not sure if you want to read your ebook off axis. Maybe some do, but I don't read normal books that way and don't see a reason why I would want to do that with an ebook. Of course, low power consumption is part of the equation since these displays make use of bi-stable technology that consumes no power when the display is not being updated.
