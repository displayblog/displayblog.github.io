Samsung S3

Display: 1.8" TFT LCD
Video: MPEG4
Audio: MP3, WAM, OGG
Capacity: 2GB, 4GB, 8GB
Color: White, Red, Green, Blue, Black
Availability: June 2008


Image courtesy: Akihabara News

Samsung announced its S3 on April 30, 2008. The cute little S3 portable media player from Samsung has a lot of functions packed into it. The dimensions of the S3 is 95mm x 44mm x 9.8mm and only weighs 53g. Some of the features include a FM radio (and recording), a text viewer, photo viewer, games, a screen savor and something called dynamic LED lighting. The LED lighting effect is linked rhythmically to the on-screen images and provides for a fusion of listening to and looking at music. Samsung's Digital Natural Sound ending (DNSe 2.0) makes the audio sound good.

These little players shouldn't be called digital audio players (DAPs) because they do so much more than play digital audio. I prefer to call these portable media players because one they are portable and two they play all sorts of media.
