Samsung is pushing out Android 2.2 (Froyo) updates to its Galaxy S smartphones. The Froyo update is available via Kies, an in-house software upgrade program. Now you can load up Flash Player 10.1 and record videos in MP4. The 4-inch Super AMOLED display should make both experiences quite nice as long as you're okay with overexcited colors (read Tim Bray: Samsung Galaxy Tab Review). Source: Samsung
