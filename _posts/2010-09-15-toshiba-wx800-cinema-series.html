Toshiba's LED backlight toting WX800 Cinema Series comes in two sizes: 46-inch 46WX800 and 55-inch 55WX800. The specs: 240Hz, WiFi BGN, 1920x1080, 3D Resolution+. Finally, another brand besides LG realizing thinner bezels are what we want. Nothing is perfect: the distracting green light, bottom aluminum-colored strip and the weird angular neck. Both are available now with the 46WX800 going for SRP of US$2599.99 and the 55WX800 priced at $3299.99. Sexy but a bit pricey. Source: Toshiba
