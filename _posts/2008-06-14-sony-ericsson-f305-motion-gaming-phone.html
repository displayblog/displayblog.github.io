Sony Ericcson F305 Specifications:

  • Display: 2.0" TFT LCD
  • Pixel Format: 176x220
  • Color: 262K
  • Connectivity: GSM, EDGE, Bluetooth 2.0 with A2DP
  • Storage: 10MB internal, MS Micro
  • Camera: 2MP fixed focus, flash
  • Audio Support: eAAC+
  • Dimension: 96 x 46 x 14.6mm
  • Weight: 97.5g
  • Availability: Q3'08

Unofficial Sony Ericsson Blog via Engadget: The Sony Ericsson F305 is a normal mobile phone but with a special talent for games. The F305 has an accelerometer that enables Wii-like gameplay, and that's quite special indeed. But, I'm a bit confused. How are you supposed to watch the bowling game when you're doing the bowling motion? Huh. You can compete with a friend on some games via a Bluetooth connection.

The display is a rather anemic 2.0" TFT LCD with a 176 x 220 pixel format. I don't know how you're suppose to play games on that small screen with the lack of pixels, but I'll reserve judgment until I get to play with it. My initial opinion is that the LCD is a bit on the smallish pixel-lacking side.
