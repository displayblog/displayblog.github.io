Garmin Nuvi 650 Specifications

Display: 4.3" Wide TFT LCD with Touch
Pixel Format: 480 x 272
Battery: Up to 7 hours
Dimensions: 4.9 x 2.9 x 0.9" (12.4 x 7.4 x 2.3cm)
Weight: 6.2oz (176g)

Garmin's Nuvi 650 portable GPS navigator is going for just $199.99 at Amazon.com. The original MSRP of the Nuvi 650 was $749.99! The Nuvi 650 has a 4.3" TFT LCD with a pixel format of 480 x 272. Not the highest resolution in the world, but if you're used to a Sony PSP, this should fit right in. There are a lot of interesting features for the Nuvi 650 including: 3D map view, auto reroute, route avoidance, FM traffic compatible, MP3 player, audio book player, picture viewer, world travel clock, touch capability, etc.

Source: Garmin, Amazon.com
