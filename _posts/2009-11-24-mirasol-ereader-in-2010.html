According to Qualcomm a Mirasol-based ereader will launch in the fall of 2010. The 5.7-inch Mirasol display will sport a 1024 x 768 resolution. Qualcomm's Mirasol display consumes very little energy thanks to its reflective design but can update much more quickly compared to displays with E Ink technology. The Mirasol-based ereader be able to last a long time for reading but you could also do things like watch movies at 30fps, surf the Internet, etc. Source: E-Reader-info.com
