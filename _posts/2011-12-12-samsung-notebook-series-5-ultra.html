via The Verge. Samsung.co.kr: The Notebook Series 5 Ultra comes in two sizes: 13-inch and 14-inch with both sporting some sort of anti-reflective technology. Samsung wants to distance its "high performance ultrabooks" from regular ones with: big HDD storage options (up to 500GB on the 13-inch and 1TB on the 14-inch), an integrated optical drive (14-inch), up to 8GB of RAM, and a two-second wake from sleep.
