Vizio VOJ370 Specifications

Display: 37" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 6500:1 (dynamic)
Response Time: 6.5ms
Viewing Angle: 178/178
Brightness: 500 cd/m2
Connectivity: HDMI (3), Component, VGA, Composite, S-Video
Tuner: NTSC, ATSC, Clear QAM
Price: US$849

Vizio announced that it is shipping its VOJ370F 37" 1080p LCD TV on October 21, 2008. The price is a respectable US$849, but you'll probably see street prices below $800 very soon. The VOJ370F sports a decent dynamic contrast ratio of 6500:1, a response time of 6.5ms and a fairly bright 500 cd/m2. What is really interesting is the color of the 37" 1080p LCD TV: Java. Yes, it seems brown is in. Brown isn't my color and is especially not my color when it comes to high-tech good. I want my high-tech electronics to look, well, high-tech. And the color brown conjures up more earthly feelings. Maybe that's what Vizio is going for: earth-friendly high-tech electronics. I personally don't like it. Keep it simple: black or silver. White would be acceptable too.
