Toshiba A300 Specifications:

  • Display: 15.4" TruBrite TFT LCD
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:10
  • Pixel Format: 1280 x 800
  • CPU: 2.1GHz Turion Ultra ZM TL-80
  • Memory: 2GB DDR2 800MHz SDRAM (max. 4GB)
  • HD: 320GB 5400RPM
  • Optical: DVD SuperMulti (+/-R DL) with Labelflash
  • Webcam: 1.3MP
  • Connectivity: HDMI, VGA, eSATA, USB 2.0 (3), 10/100 Ethernet, Modem, 5-in-1 Media Reader, Audio In/Out, FireWire, ExpressCard/34/54
  • Battery: 6-cell 4000mAh Lithium Ion

NotebookReview → Engadget: Kevin O'Brien over at NotebookReview was able to sneak install wPrime on the Toshiba A300 notebook PC at Computex 2008 and test its metal. Toshiba's A300 is a 15.4" TFT LCD sporting a 1280x800 pixel format. The benchmark results? wPrime 32M time of 38.437 seconds, a bit slower than the A305D (2.2GHz Turion X2 TL-64). The fastest one on the list at NotebookReview is Toshiba's own A305 with a 2.1GHz Core 2 Duo T8100 with a 36.44 second wPrime 32M time.
