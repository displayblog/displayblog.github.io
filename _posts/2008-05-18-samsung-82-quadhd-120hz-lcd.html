Samsung 82" QuadHD
Size: 82"
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 3840 x 2160 (that's 1920 x 1080 times four)
Frequency: 120Hz
Backlight: LED

Samsung's 82" QuadHD 120Hz LCD is simply wonderful. What more can you ask for? Maybe 240Hz? It has a LED backlight, a 120Hz frequency, and a QUAD HD (that would be four 1080p LCDs in one!) pixel format. You could possibly ask for a RGB LED backlight (TRILUMINOS) like the one Sony has on its 70" KDL-70XBR3. Samsung is showcasing this show stopper at SID 2008 in LA. I hope I can take some good pictures of this when I'm down there. I wonder what kind of GPU is required to run this thing.
