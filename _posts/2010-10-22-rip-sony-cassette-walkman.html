Engadget is reporting that Sony has now stopped production of cassette Walkmans in Japan. Once inventory in Japanese channels run out there will be no more cassette Walkmans. China-made cassette Walkmans are still a go, to some extent.

I lived with my WM-F102 during my junior high school days back in the mid-80's. I moved around a lot when I was younger so music was my connection to what I had to leave behind. When I left Korea for the second time the WM-F102 and Lee Moon Sae's fourth album, which I recorded off of my LP, kept me connected.
