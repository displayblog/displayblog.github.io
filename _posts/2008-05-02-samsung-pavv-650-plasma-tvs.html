Samsung PAVV 650

Size: 50", 58"
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 1,000,000:1
Viewing Angle: >175/175
Input: HDMI (4), USB (1), Component (2), Composite
Price:

  • 50": KRW3,000,000 (about US$2,963 based on KRW1012.3/US$)
  • 58": KRW4,900,000 (about US$4,840)


More pictures at AVING

There isn't much information about the PAVV 650 plasma TVs, but one thing is for sure: with a contrast ratio of 1,000,000:1, pictures will look incredible! The price for the 50" unit looks pretty good too. If the previous generation PAVV 550 plasma TVs are any indication, the PAVV 650 series should get a pixel format of 1920 x 1080 and viewing angles of greater than 175.
