The Verge: The IP55-rated ASUS ZenWatch sports a 1.6-inch OLED display with 320x320 pixels protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 3, a stainless steel body, and a nicely stitched tan leather strap. Unfortunately the bezels top and bottom are too thick.

The ZenWatch running Android Wear has a few notable features: it measures your relaxation level (heart rate monitor), it finds your smartphone when you double tap the display, and it can automatically unlock your smartphone. Priced at €199 (about US$260).
