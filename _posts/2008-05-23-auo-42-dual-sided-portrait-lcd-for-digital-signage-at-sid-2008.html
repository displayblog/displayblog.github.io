AUO 42" Dual-Sided LCD
Size: 42"
Aspect Ratio: 9:16
Pixel Format: 768 x 1366
LCD Technology: Advanced Multidomain Vertical Alignment (AMVA)
Color Saturation: 72% NTSC
Viewing Angle: 178/178
Brightness: 550 cd/m2 (each side)
Contrast Ratio: 1,500:1 (dark room)
Response Time: 8ms
Backlight: Portrait direction CCFL
Power Consumption: 350W
Dimension: 1106 x 580 x 57mm
Weight: 15kg



AU Optronics (AUO) showcased a 42" dual-sided portrait LCD for digital signage applications during SID 2008. Possible applications might be general advertising at shopping malls and retail stores, information displays in the transportation industry such as in airports and train stations, menu boards at restaurants, and information displays at hotels. The 42" dual-sided LCD sports AUO's AMVA LCD technology that provides very good front screen performance such as 178/178 viewing angles, a brightness of 550 cd/m2 and a contrast ratio of 1,500:1. In this portrait LCD the CCFLs are arranged horizontally.
