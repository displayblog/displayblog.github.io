Palit GeForce GTS 250 2GB Specifications

Core: 745MHz
Shader: 1848MHz
VRAM: 2GB GDDR3 2200GHz
Connectivity: VGA, DVI (Dual-Link), HDMI

The more graphics intensive the game, the more memory you'll need. That's the moto behind Palit's GeForce GTS 250 with 2GB of GDDR3, in my opinion. TweakTown thoroughly benchmarked the 2GB GPU from Palit. Crysis Warhead ran at a minimum 14FPS and an average 23FPS in Vista at 1920 x 1200.

In terms of power consumption the Palit consumed 303W under load and 206W idling. The bottom line from Palit: "While the 2GB of RAM might not be that helpful, the overall performance, onboard HDMI and general design of the card is simply great. And if you're still set on having 2GB of RAM, you really can't go wrong." Overall rating? 88%.Â
