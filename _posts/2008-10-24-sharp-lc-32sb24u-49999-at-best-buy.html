Sharp LC-32SB24U Specifications

Display: 32" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1366 x 768
Contrast Ratio: 1500:1 (static), 7500:1 (dynamic)
Brightness: 450 cd/m2
Connectivity: HDMI (2), S-Video, Component (2), Composite (2), VGA

Sharp's LC-32SB24U is going for just $499.99 at Best Buy. The LC-32SB24U is a 32" LCD TV with a pixel format of 1366 x 768. I have had a change of heart when it comes to high-tech goods. For most consumers "good enough" really is good enough. And there are some studies to back it up. For instance, most people cannot tell the difference between a LCD TV that has 1920 x 1080 or 1366 x 768 from normal TV viewing distances. So the Blu-ray version of Transformers will look just as good on a 32" with 1920 x 1080 pixels as on a 32" with 1366 x 768. Granted higher-end models come with other advanced features such as 120Hz, a LED backlight, motion interpolation, etc. but you'll need to spend considerably more. If your budget is tight but want to get rid of your CRT TV for a brand new LCD TV go for what looks good to you and forget about most of the techie jargon (including the ones written here!).
