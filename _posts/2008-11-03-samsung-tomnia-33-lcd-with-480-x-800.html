Samsung T*OMNIA Specifications

Display: 3.3" Touch TFT LCD
Pixel Format: 480 x 800 (portrait)
Camera: 5MP with Auto Focus, Flash, Image Stabilization
Video: DivX, XviD, MP4, MOV, SKM, K3G, 3GP, 3GP2
Connectivity: DMB, A-GPS, WiFi, Bluetooth 2.0+EDR, USB 2.0, HSDPA (7.2Mbps), Quad Band CDMA (850, 900, 1800, 1900MHz)

Samsung's T*OMNIA is an improved version of the original Omnia. The improvements include a much higher resolution display and the addition of DMB capabilities. The TFT LCD is slightly bigger at 3.3" versus 3.22" while quadrupling the pixel format to 480 x 800 from 240 x 400. I am sure all those pixels will look wonderful! DMB will allow you to watch mobile TV on the T*OMNIA. It seems the T*OMNIA will be (officially) limited to those lucky folks in South Korea.

Source: Akihabara News
