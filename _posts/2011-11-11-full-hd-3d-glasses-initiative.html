Full HD 3D Glasses Initiative:

Standardizing active shutter 3D eyewear technology is essential in order to help grow 3D industry.

The FH3GI was formed by Panasonic, Samsung, Sony, and X6D and pushes active shutter 3D technology. Philips, Sharp, TCL, and Toshiba have expressed support.

Two big problems. First, if the 3D industry grows the future will be glasses-free. Second, active shutter can cause seizures.
