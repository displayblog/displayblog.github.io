Response Time: 5ms

31.7 x 23.0 x 9.9" with stand

Price: US$1,299.99

Samsung followed up with its excellent LN32A550 32" 1080p LCD TV with the LN32A650. There are two main differences. The LN32A650 has Samsung's Touch of Color design theme and the video processing is done by the company's proprietary DNIe Pro. The LN32A550 has the DNIe+ engine working hard to generate great front screen performance. DNIe stands for Digital Natural Image enhancement and the Pro version is Samsung's third generation engine that optimizes motion display, natural color and improves contrast, brightness and detail.

With the added design and better video processing engine while keeping the excellent 15,000:1 contrast ratio, fast 5ms response time and great viewing angle, the LN32A650, though slightly more expensive than the LN32A550, should provide some excellent performance.
