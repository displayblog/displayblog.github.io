iSuppli announced wordwide PC shipment results for the third quarter and HP is on top with 19.9% market share growing 7% Y/Y to 15.9 million units. Acer overtook Dell for the #2 spot growing 16.6% Y/Y to 10.7 million unit shipments and grabbing a 13.4% market share. Dropping to #3 was Dell with shipments declining 5.9% Y/Y to 10.3 million units representing a 12.9% market share. At #4 was Lenovo followed by Toshiba at #5. Source: iSuppli via DIGITIMES
