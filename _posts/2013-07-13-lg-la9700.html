LG: Samsung announced its 4K TVs, now it's LG's turn. The LA9700 4K LCD TV series come in two sizes at 55 and 65 inches, priced US$6999 and $7999 respectively. The LG 4K LCD TVs are more expensive than Samsung's equivalents, which doesn't seem like a smart move considering Samsung has the stronger brand in markets that would consider purchasing 4K TVs.
