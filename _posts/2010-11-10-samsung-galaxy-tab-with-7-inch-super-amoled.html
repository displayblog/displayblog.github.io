Akihabara News: Samsung will be showcasing its Galaxy Tab sporting a 7-inch Super AMOLED. The just-released Galaxy Tab sports a very decent LCD. The difference is not just display technology but pixel format too.

The LCD packs 1024x600 pixels while the Super AMOLED features 1200x600. I'm not sure if this is an apples-to-apples comparison as the Super AMOLED will most likely make use of PenTile Matrix technology that is based on a 2x2 sub-pixel structure, different from the 3x1 structure of typical LCDs.
