LG.Philips LCD will be showing off its 14.1" flexible ebook display at SID 2006 in San Francisco that starts June 4. Exhibits will start on June 6. Viewing angles are suppose to be extremely good at 180 degrees all around. With a pixel format of 1280 x 800, the display is a wide 16:10. It uses a reflective technology and makes use of natural light (outdoors & indoor ambient light) to improve readability and power efficiency. Key technologies employed are from e-Ink and uses a metal foil substrate.
