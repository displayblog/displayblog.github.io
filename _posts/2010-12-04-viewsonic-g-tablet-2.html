Laptop:

The 10.1-inch 1024 x 600 screen on the G Tablet has sharp images but colors seem muted. Viewing angles are solid at 90 degrees to the left or right when holding the device perpendicular to the ground or a table. However, when the slate is resting flat on its back, you must be right on top of it or images wash out completely. [...]

The most important component on a tablet like this is the display. And one of the most important features of the display are wide viewing angles.

Viewsonic chose to replace the stock Android interface with a Tap 'n Tap software. The UI looks friendly on the surface, but its sluggish performance and lame preloaded apps drag the entire device down.

A not-so-great display + a not-so-great UI = a not-so-great experience.
