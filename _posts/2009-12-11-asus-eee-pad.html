Asustek is rumored to be developing an ASUS-branded Eee Pad, a tablet of course. The size ranges from 4 to 7 inches and to be commercialized in 2010. The amusing part is that the Eee Pad was supposedly inspired by rumors of Apple's tablet.

The fabled Apple Tablet is affecting everyone, to the extent that Apple has almost single-handedly created a product category without doing anything.


That's Charlie Sorrel in his article on Wired, "Asus Eee Pad 'Inspired by Rumors". I'm afraid the Eee Pad will be the netbook version of a real tablet from the likes of Microsoft with its Courier and the oft-fabled tablet from Apple. Source: DIGITIMES via Akihabara News, Wired
