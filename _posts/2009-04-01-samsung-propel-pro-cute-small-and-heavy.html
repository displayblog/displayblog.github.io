Samsung Propel Pro Specifications

Display: 2.55" TFT LCD
Pixel Format: 320 x 320
Camera: 3MP with Auto Focus
Network: GSM (850, 900, 1800, 1900MHz), UMTS (850, 1900MHz)
Audio: MP3, AAC, AAC+, WMA, WAV
Connectivity: WiFi BG, Bluetooth, USB
Storage: microSD, microSDHC
Other: QWERTY Keyboard, Metal Chassis
OS: Windows Mobile 6.1
Battery: Lithium Ion 1440mAh (6.5 hours talk, 288 hours standby)
Dimensions: 3.9 x 2.4 x 0.6" (99 x 61 x 15mm)
Weight: 4.83oz (137g)
Price: US$149

Photo Courtesy: Engadget

Bottomline: Samsung's Propel Pro is cute, small, and heavy smartphone. As you can see from the picture above the Propel Pro is quite small. For people who have smaller-than-average hands, the Propel Pro should fit quite nicely. After holding a credit card to see how it fits, the Propel Pro seems to be about the size of a credit card. Some might argue but I think the smallish smartphone is quite cute. But because Samsung used some heavy metal materials to make it sturdy and solid, the little Propel Pro but the folks at Engadget thinks: "The heft on the other hand is a bit much..."

The Propel Pro is also interesting in that it has a square screen much like the old Palms used o have. The 2.55" TFT LCD sports a 320 x 320 pixel format and can display 65,536 colors. The 3MP can take video too and has an auto focus feature.
