Lumileds' LUXEON Flash LED technology demonstrated a 1.0 lux sec at 3 meter performance, besting a Xenon solution that performs at only 0.67 lux sec. With this, mobile phone flashes will be more bright and allow users to take better pictures in dim-light situations. The LUXEON Flash LEDs are available in surface-mount or as a moedule with a lens.
