Samsung SyncMaster 70 Series Specifications

Display: 20" and 23"
Contrast Ratio: 50,000:1 (dynamic)
Response Time: 2ms
Power Consumption: 33% less than comparable LCD monitors

Image Courtesy: Akihabara News

Samsung just announced its SyncMaster 70 Series LCD monitors.Â  These monitors are green in that they consume 33% less energy. You get a choice of two sizes: 20" and 23". Dynamic contrast ratio is rated at 50,000:1. Don't get too excited about that big number because it just means when you're watching a movie that the contrast ratio will be a bit better than regular monitors. Response time is a very fast 2ms. So gamers should have little problems with lag.

Price is an important factor in any purchase decision and unfortunately there is no information at this time. Purchasing electronics that consume less energy without any decrease in performance is definitely the smart thing to do.
