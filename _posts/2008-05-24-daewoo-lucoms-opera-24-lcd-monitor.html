Daewoo Lucoms Opera
Size: 24"
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1200
Contrast Ratio: 5,000:1 (dynamic)

Daewoo hasn't been a force in the displays market in quite a long while. Well, it seems Daewoo is coming back out punching. The company released its Opera LCD monitor in the Korean market as of May 23, 2008.

The Opera is a 24" LCD monitor with a pixel format of 1920 x 1200. The overall look is simple and nice but it does seem to be a bit porky. The dynamic contrast ratio of 5,000:1 should bring about some excellent images. There isn't much more information about this product, even on its Korean website.
