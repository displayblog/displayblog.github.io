The official specs:

  • 3.7-inch AMOLED
  • 1GHz Snapdragon CPU
  • Digital Compass, GPS, Light & Proximity Sensor
  • 5MP Camera with LED Flash
  • 3.5-mm Headphone Jack
  • Multi-colored LED Under Trackball
  • Two Mics: One on bottom, one on back, for Noise Cancellation
  • Android 2.1
  • HTC Branded
  • Two-Line Custom Engrave Option
  • Price: US$529 Unlocked, $179 with 2-Year Contract


The Nexus One is also headed for Verizon and Vodafone in Spring 2010. Source: Engadget
