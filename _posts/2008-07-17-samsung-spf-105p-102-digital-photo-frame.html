Samsung SPF-105P Specifications

  • Display: 10.2" Wide TFT LCD
  • Pixel Format: 1024x600
  • Brightness: 300 cd/m2
  • Contrast Ratio: 500:1
  • Storage: 1GB (Internal)
  • Connectivity: USB (UbiSync)
  • Other: Samsung Starlight Touch Button Control
  • Battery: 1 Hour
  • Power Consumption: 14W (Max), Auto On/Off Function
  • Dimensions: 11.37 x 7.81 x 1.8"
  • Weight: 2.18lbs

Samsung: The Samsung SPF-105P digital photo frame sports a minimalist clean design. On the bezel is Samsung's Starlight touch button control while a single USB connection connects the 10.2" digital photo frame to the computer. The USB is special and uses the UbiSync technology to allow it to be used as a secondary display. The internal 1GB of memory should allow for plenty of pictures to be stored and displayed, which will be on a 10.2" wide TFT LCD with a pixel format of 1024x600.
