Sony's 46" KLV-46S200A LCD TV is priced at Dh13,999 (~US$3800) and is available at all Jumbo Electronics outlets in the United Arab Emirates (UAE).

The KLV-46S200A has a High Definition Multimedia Interface (HDMI), a VGA input (for connecting a PC), a S-Video, Composite and Component video inputs. The BRAVIA Engine incorporated in the KLV-46S200A enhances contrast, reduces noise and sharpens pictures and colors.

The TFT LCD panel itself has a 178-degree viewing angle, 16:9 aspect ratio, 500 cd/m2 of brightness, 8ms response time, and 1300:1 contrast ratio. The one negative about this large 46" LCD TV is the limited resolution of 1366 x 768. Why couldn't Sony use a 1920 x 1080 resolution? 1336 x 768 is way too low for something this big.
