Who comes up with these model names for NEC? The EA222WMe-BK is a 22-inch LCD monitor sporting a LED backlight, 250 cd/m2 of brightness, and consumes a lot less then typical monitors. Standard power consumption is 23W: ECO-Mode 1 consumes 17W and ECO-Mode 2 consumes 13W. The dynamic contrast ratio is rated at 30,000:1 but that doesn't mean anything. Source: Akihabara News
