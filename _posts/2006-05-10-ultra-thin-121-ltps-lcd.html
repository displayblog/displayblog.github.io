Slightly old news, but according to Akihabara News, Toshiba-Matsushita Display (TMD) announced the world's thinnest 12.1" LCD display that is 2.9mm thick. The 12.1" LTPS LCD also incorporates a LED BLU that has a brightness of 300nits (cd/m2) and has wide 1280*800 pixel format.
