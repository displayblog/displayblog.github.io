FIFA in collaboration with Sony will deliver 3D images from up to 25 matches during the 2010 World Cup in 3D. FIFA will also host the "International FIFA Fan Fest" public viewing events in Berlin, London, Mexico City, Paris, Rio De Janeiro, Rome and Sydney where Sony's commercial displays will show promotional highlight trailers of the FIFA World Cup in 3D. Time to buy a 3D TV. Source: FIFA via Akihabara News
