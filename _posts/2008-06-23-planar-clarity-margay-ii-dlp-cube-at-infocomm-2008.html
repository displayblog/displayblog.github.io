Planar Clarity Margay II DLP Cube Specifications
Display: 50" DLP Rear Projection 2x4
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080 (each), 3840 x 4320 (total)

Here is another beautiful example of a DLP cube that I saw at InfoComm 2008. A total of 8 DLP rear projection displays are put together in a 2x4 structure that gives you a total pixel format of 3840 x 4320. The borders are small enough that it doesn't distract you from the message. Also note that all 8 1080p DLP displays are working in tandem to generate a single massively cool image.
