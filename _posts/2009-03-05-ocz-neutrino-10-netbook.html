OCZ is jumping into the crowded netbook bandwagon wit its Neutrino sporting a 10" LCD with a 1024 x 600 pixel format. The Neutrino will be powered by Intel's Atom CPU running at 1.6GHz and will have the option of a 250GB OCZ SSD. There will be two versions: DIY and maxed-out editions. The DIY will be competitively priced and the maxed-out edition will max out your credit card. As you can see you have a wide variety of OS choices. Engadget does seem to like the keyboard: "... the keyboard was one of the nicest we've touched on a netbook...".
