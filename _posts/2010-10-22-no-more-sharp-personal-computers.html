Sharp announced on October 21st that it will no longer manufacture and sell personal computers. Production of Sharp-branded notebooks and desktops will cease very soon. Sharp will focus on post-PC gadgets, like its Galapagos tablet. Read Engadget: Sharp Galapagos Hands On and Sharp Galapagos. Source: Nikkei
