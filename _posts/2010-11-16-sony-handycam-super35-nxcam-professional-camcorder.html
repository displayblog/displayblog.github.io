Akihabara News: A professional camcorder with an E-Mount, which is used on the NEX. That means all the E-Mount lenses cam be used on the NXCAM professional camcorder, which is currently under development. You can use a dizzying array of lenses with all the lens adapters available on the market today, including those branded Leica, Voigtlander, etc. Video is recorded in AVCHD or in MPEG4 AVC/H.264 at 1080/24p/30p/60p.
