Samsung announced on January 5, 2009 that it will be unveiling the world's thinnest LCD TV with a LED backlight at CES 2009: the LUXIA LED TV. The ultra-thin LCD TV will be just 6.5mm thick with sizes ranging from 40" to 55". 6.5mm... that is REALLY thin. Other features will include the company's 240Hz MotionPlus technology making sure that motion blur is completely obliterated. Looking forward to taking a look at the LUXIA LED TV at CES 2009.

Source: Akihabara News
