Akihabara News: The Samsung C23A750X is a 23-inch UWB (Ultra Wide Band) wireless monitor with a 1920x1080 pixel format. For the C23A750X to work you'll need a computer with UWB, but if you are the lucky few that do have one all you need to do is plug the 23-inch UWB monitor in and wirelessly connect. The C23A750X will be available in Korea at around KRW590,000 (about US$540).
