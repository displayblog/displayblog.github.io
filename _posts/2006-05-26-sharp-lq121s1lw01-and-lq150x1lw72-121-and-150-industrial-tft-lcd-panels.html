Sharp, a Japan based LCD supplier, developed two industrial TFT LCD panels targeted to the industrial market using its Advanced Super View (ASV) technology that is known for brightness and color uniformity and all angles. These two panels will be drop-in replacements for existing panels. Let's look at the specifications:

12.1" LQ121S1LW01
800 x 600
250 cd/m2 using two CCFLs
262,144 colors
800:1 CR
29 ms (rise response time)

15.0" LQ150X1LW72
1024 x 768
350 cd/m2 using four CCFLs
16 million colors (8-bit)
600:1 CR
25 ms
