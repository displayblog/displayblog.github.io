DigiTimes: The second quarter isn't over yet but estimates are flying. TPK Holdings is expected to ship more than three million touch modules for the iPad 2 in the month of June. TPK will manufacture about 1.1 million of those in-house and outsource two million to Sintek Photronics and Cando. Wintek will also supply about one million in June.

Only 4.5 to 5 million iPad 2s were shipped in the first quarter. For the second quarter TPK is expected to ship more than 6.3 million touch modules. Combined with Wintek
total shipments of touch modules in the second quarter could be more than nine million. For the first half of 2011, estimated shipments of the iPad 2 are around 13.5 to 14 million units.
