Sharp AQUOS LC-32GP2U Specifications

Display: 32" ASV TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 2000:1 (static), 10000:1 (dynamic)
Brightness: 450 cd/m2
Connectivity: HDMI (3), Component (2), Composite (3), VGA
Power Consumption: Energy Star
Dimensions:
30-61/64 x 23-7/8 x 11-45/64" (w/stand)
30-61/64 x 21-1/32 x 3-19/32" (w/o stand)
Price: MSRP US$1049.99

Sharp's AQUOS LC-32GP2U is the second generation 32" LCD TV sporting a 1920 x 1080 pixel format. The LC-32GP2U has a very good 2000:1 static contrast ratio, a brightness of 450 cd/m2 and a decent number of HDMI ports (three). There is already a third generation LC-32GP3U but the price sweet spot is on the LC-32GP2U with a $749.99 price at Best Buy. Now, bear in mind that the LC-32GP2U does not have a 120Hz frequency, which means you'll experience some motion blur when watching fast moving objects like sports, animation and when playing games. The $749.99 price is a good price for a 1080p-capable 32" LCD TV if you don't mind the motion blur. Of course, it's a tossup as to whether your eyes are good enough to even see the additional pixels over a 720p set.

Source: Best Buy
