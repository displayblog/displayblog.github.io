The Kodak Easyshare V610 has one of the largest LCDs for a compact digital camera. The LCD is 2.8" and is one of the largest I've heard of. Of course there are larger one. I believe Samsung has a digicam (the Pro815 to be exact) that holds the world record for largest TFT LCD screen on a digital still camera: 3.5". But the body on the Pro815 is much larger than Kodak's V610. The 2.8" TFT LCD on the V610 has 230,000 total pixels--on the high side for a compact digital camera.


Source: Digital Photography Journal, Kodak Easyshare V610

As you can see from the picture, the 2.8" TFT LCD screen takes up much of the V610's back. More screen real estate the better. Although I am not for the complete removal of physical buttons as they provide much better tactile feedback than a touchscreen.

Source: DPReview
