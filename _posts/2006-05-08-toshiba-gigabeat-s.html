The Gigabeat S sports a 2.4" TFT LCD with 320*240 pixel format (the same pixel format that the iPod video has) and displays video as well as other multimedia (pictures, MP3 music info, etc.). The price will be $300 for the 30GB and $400 for the 60GB. In terms of music, the Gigabeat S supports WMA Lossless, WMA, MP3, WAV. The battery life is claimed to be 12 hours and a FM Tuner is built-in with a USB 2.0 interface.
