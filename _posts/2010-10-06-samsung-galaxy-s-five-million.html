Samsung has announced that it has sold five million Galaxy S smartphones. Two million plus have been sold in the US, one million plus in Korea and the rest, well, in the rest of the world. Japan will be getting the Galaxy S in late October and the Galaxy Tab in late November. Source: Korea Newswire
