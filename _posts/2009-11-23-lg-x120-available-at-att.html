LG's X120, a 10.1-inch netbook, can be yours for US$179.99 with a two-year $60/mo contract with AT&T. You can get the 2.8-pound matte white netbook at RadioShack.com or brick-and-mortar The Shack stores. FYI, the two-year contract will cost you $1440, plus taxes. Source: PR Newswire via Engadget
