Part of NEC's MultiSync PA Series, the MultiSync PA241W 24-inch LCD monitor is geared for professional graphics applications. The PA241W uses a 10-bit IPS (In-Plane Switching) panel sporting a 14-bit lookup table (LUT) increasing the number of possible colors to more than one billion. The color gamut is rated at 102% NTSC and 99.3% Adobe RGB. Other specs include:

  • Pixel Format: 1920 x 1200 (RGB stripe sub-pixel)
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:10
  • Brightness: 360 cd/m2
  • Connectivity: 10-bit DisplayPort, DVI-D with HDCP, USB Hub
  • Adjustability: Tilt, Swivel, Height, Pivot
  • Price: US$1079 with 4-Year Parts and Labor Warranty Including Backlight
