Samsung PN50A650

Size: 49.96"
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 1,000,000:1 (dynamic)
Viewing Angle: 175/175
Picture Processing: DNIe Pro
Input: HDMI (4), Composite (2), S-Video (1), VGA (1), Component (2)
Tuner: ATSC, NTSC, Clear QAM
Dimension: 48.8" x 30.1" x 3.8" (without stand)
Weight: 86.4lbs (without stand)
Price: $2599.99

Samsung's PN50A650 incorporates the company's new Touch of Color design that adds a dash of red into the a glossy black finish. Aside from the nice design there is nothing much else, except for the incredible 1,000,000:1 contrast ratio. The last time I saw this kind of specification for contrast ratio was for Sony's XEL-1 OLED TV.

Source: Samsung
