ASUS Eee Top Specifications

Display: 15.6" Touch TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1366 x 768
Webcam: 1.3MP
GPU: Intel GMA 950
CPU: 1.6GHz Intel Atom N270
RAM: 1GB
HDD: 160GB 5400RPM
Optical: N/A
Connectivity: WiFi BGN, USB (6), Multi Card Reader (SD, MMC, MS), Gigabit Ethernet, Mic-In, Line-In, Headphone-Out
OS: Windows XP Home
Dimensions: 4cm thick
Weight: 4.3kg
Price: US$599 at Amazon

ASUS' Eee Top is an all-in-one PC sporting a touch-enabled 15.6" TFT LCD. The LCD features a pixel format of 1366 x 768, giving it an aspect ratio of 16:9. The 1.6GHz Intel Atom powers the Top and that may make a tad slow if you want to do more than check email, surf the net and IM. Don't expect to be watching flicker-free 720p HD on the Top as it will probably overwhelm the GPU (Intel GMA 950) and the CPU. ASUS did a decent job with the design--it is fairly clean with only the required buttons on the front. I do wish ASUS would redesign the logo to make it just a tad bit more upscale. Unboxing video after the jump.

The touch capability, a resistive type, allows you to use your finger or a stylus but only one at a time--no multitouching here. As you can see from the specifications above, the OS is Windows XP Home. The Tablet PC version would have been a better choice in my opinion. The news today is that the ASUS Eee Top is available for pre-order on Amazon for US$599.
