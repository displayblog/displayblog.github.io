IDC: Preliminary results for U.S. PC vendor unit shipments for the second quarter show:

  1. HP: 26.3%, -0.6% Y/Y
  2. Dell: 22.2%, -10.2%
  3. Apple: 10.7%, +14.7%
  4. Toshiba: 9.1%, +3.7%
  5. Acer: 8.5%, -25.4%

  • Other: 23.3%, -2.0%


Overall PC shipments in the U.S. dropped 4.2% Y/Y to 17.857 million units in the second quarter. Only Apple posted double-digit Y/Y growth and is the #3 PC brand in the U.S. Note: IDC does not include media tablets such as the iPad in the PC category.
