People with jailbroken iPhones who have not changed the default root password be careful. There is a new worm that hijacks your iPhone and tries to steal user information from a particular Dutch bank. The worm also appears capable of creating iPhone-based botnets. I strongly suggest changing the default root password if you have jailbroken your iPhone. Many believe more viruses and worms will target the iPhone in the future. Source: News Factor
