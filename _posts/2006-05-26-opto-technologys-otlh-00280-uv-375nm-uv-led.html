Opto Technology introduced a 375nm peak wavelength UV LED array as part of its Shark product line on May 25. The UV LED array use 50 die in a TO-66 package and radiant flux of 254mW when driven with a 300mA maximum peak current. Half-max viewing angle is +54 degrees and the emitters are priced at $133.67 each in quantities of 100.
