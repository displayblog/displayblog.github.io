LG's X300 is a really thin and light fanless netbook: 17.6-mm (0.69 inch) thick and weighs 970g (2.14 pounds). The 11.6-inch netbook sports a 2.0GHz Intel Atom Z550 CPU, 2GB RAM, SSD, 1.3MP webcam, built-in 3G modem, WiFi, 2-cell battery good for 7 hours.
