Zune HD. Now that would be cool. Especially if the LCD (or OLED?) had a 800 x 480 pixel format. Or maybe even a 1280 x 720 (!!!) for true HD capability. There are some that define HD as a viewing experience (a Zune HD with 800 x 480 would probably give you that HD-like viewing experience) but I like to define it simply: 1280 x 720 pixel format or 1920 x 1080 updating video progressively at 30fps or faster. So is the Zune HD for real?
