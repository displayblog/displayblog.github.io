Philips 42PF9831D

Size: 42"
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1366 x 768
Brightness: 550 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio: 4500:1 (dynamic)
Viewing Angle: 176/176

Philips has an interesting feature called Ambilight where light matching the majority of the screens color flash behind the TV giving the viewer a mood that matches what he/she is watching. The Ambilight in the 42PF9831D projects to all four sides of the screen, each side working independently. This probably uses a LG.Philips LCD 42" LCD TV module. The dynamic contrast ratio of 4500:1 should generate some impressive pictures.

Source: T3, Amazon UK
