AOC 2216Vw Specifications

Display: 21.6" TN (Twisted Nematic) TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1680 x 1050
Brightness: 300 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio: 3000:1 (Dynamic)
Response Time: 5ms
Viewing Angles: 160/160
Connectivity: DVI-D with HDCP, VGA
Power Consumption: 49W (Max., operational), Energy Star compliant
Dimensions: 19.9 x 14.3 x 8.3", 505.8 x 357.6 x 210mm
Weight: 11.4lbs, 5.2kg
Price: $174.07 at Amazon.com

AOC's 2216Vw is a 22" LCD monitor sporting a 1680 x 1050 pixel format. The design is simple and to-the-point. The controls are in the middle, where I like them. The reason being that when the buttons are on the side, pushing the buttons can tend to shift the monitor if it is on a smooth surface, which is often the case. The response time is a respectable 5ms and brightness is very good at 300 cd/m2. Viewing angles are limited to 160/160 but if you're the only one looking at it then there's no problem. Of course if you are looking for a monitor that has good color fidelity, I recommend a monitor that uses either a PVA or IPS LCD panel. The 2216Vw has both DVI-D with HDCP and VGA connections.

Source: AOC North America, Amazon.com
