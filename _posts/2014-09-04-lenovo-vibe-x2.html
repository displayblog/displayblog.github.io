Luke Edwards, Pocket-lint:

The handset also features a 5-inch Full HD display, 32GB storage and that tri-layered design meaning multiple colour options. There's a 13-megapixel camera on the rear with auto-focus and a 5-megapixel front facing selfie camera that uses a wide angle lens.

I like the blocky design of the Vibe X2. The 5-inch IPS LCD with 1920x1080 pixels make the Vibe X2 a mid-range smartphone. Many smartphone brands are focusing on improving the selfie experience by adding more pixels and a wide angle lens to the front-facing camera; I expect the photo and video qualities of selfies on Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube to improve quite a bit in the near future.
