Nokia: Guess the rumors were right. The Nokia X line of Android smartphones come in X, X+, and XL varieties. Nokia is targeting the low end smartphone market with the €89 X, €99 X+, and the €109 XL. The X and X+ sport 4-inch 800x480 LCDs, and the XL goes up a notch in size at 5 inches but with the same pixel format.
