AV Watch: Sony PlayView is a PS3 service that pumps high-resolution images and now supports 4K2K* (4096x2160). Sony will use PlayView to launch PLUP SERIES, a 4K2K digital visual magazine. The first PLUP SERIES magazine will be iQueen, available on August 9 for ¥2,100.

* 4K2K can also represent a pixel format of 3840x2160.
