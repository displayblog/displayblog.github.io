The BenQ DV S11 captures Full HD 1080/30p video using a five megapixel image sensor. 720p HD video capture is also possible. There is a 230K-pixel 3.5-inch touch-enabled LCD to view them on, but if that's too small you can use the built-in pico projector for a much larger 50-inch screen. You also have the option to output video via HDMI. Source: Gayakuman
