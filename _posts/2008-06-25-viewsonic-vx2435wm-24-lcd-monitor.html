Viewsonic VX2435wm Specifications

Display: 24" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1200
Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
Viewing Angle: 176/176
Response Time: 8ms (GTG), 20ms (Off-On-Off)
Brightness: 500 cd/m2
Lifetime: 40,000 hours
Connectivity: VGA, HDMI with HDCP, Component, Composite, S-Video
Power Consumption: 90W
Dimension: 22.5" x 20.1" x 9.6" (with stand), 22.5" x 16.5" x 3.6" (without stand)
Weight: 17.6lbs (with stand), 14.7lbs (without stand)
Other: Energy Star compliant

Viewsonic's VX2435wm is targeted at folks who like to use their LCD monitors for work as well as entertainment. Look at the plethora of connections on the 24" LCD monitor and you can see Viewsonic is serious about targeting this market segment: there are five video inputs. This is similar to what Dell has been doing with its UltraSharp LCD monitors. You can easily connect a PlayStation 3 via the HDMI port, a PC via the VGA port and other video players such as a Nintendo Wii via one of the rest.

The 1000:1 contrast ratio isn't too shabby but it could be better. The 500 cd/m2 of brightness is at the top end of the spectrum and most likely you'll need to dial down the brightness a bit if you don't want to fry your eyes. The GTG response times of 8ms is fairly standard, but if you're a hardcore gamer, you'd want something a bit faster. But all in all the VX2435wm has some very decent specs and should satisfy most.

Source: Viewsonic
