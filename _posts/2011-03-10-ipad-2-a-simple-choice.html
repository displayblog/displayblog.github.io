Cult of Mac:

An astonishing 82% of consumers plan to buy an iPad despite the dozens of competing tablets due this year, a ChangeWave survey found.

There are not dozens of iPad alternatives. And if there were, we would buy iPads because there are so many alternatives without clear distinctions from one another.
