What is it? 11.6-inch notebook PC with a 1366 x 768 resolution that lasts about 7 hours. It weighs just 3.5 pounds and a bit thicker than an inch. The P3110 sports a 1.3 GHz Pentium SU4100 and the lower-end P3010 features an 1.6 GHz Athlon Neo MV-40.

The Good & Bad: The 7 hours of battery life is certainly a positive. For a 11.6-incher it is a bit on the porky side at thicker than an inch and weighing 3.5 pounds. CPU power seems to be a bit lacking too with the top end sporting a 1.3 GHz Pentium SU4100; it has two cores though.
