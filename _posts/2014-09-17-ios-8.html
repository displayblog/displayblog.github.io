As of September 17, 2014 (today) iOS 8 is available. A couple of caveats:

  • TOO BIG: The iOS 8 update might be too big if you are attempting to update over the air using a WiFi connection. The solution is to connect your iPod, iPhone, or iPad to your computer and update using iTunes.


  • iCLOUD DRIVE: Do not upgrade to iCloud Drive during the setup process. At the moment iCloud Drive is not compatible with OS X 10.9 Mavericks, the latest operating system from Apple. Many apps will not work with iCloud Drive. Syncing with your Mac will not work, unless your Mac is updated to OS X 10.10 Yosemite, which will be released later this year (source: 9to5 Mac).
