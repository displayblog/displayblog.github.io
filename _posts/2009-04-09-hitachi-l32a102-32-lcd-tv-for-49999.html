Hitachi L32A102 Specifications

Display: 32" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1366 x 768
Brightness: 500 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
Connectivity: HDMI (2), Component, S-Video (2), Composite (2), VGA, RF
Speakers: 8W+8W
Dimensions: 32.06 x 21.19 x 3.5" (w/o stand)
Price: $499.99 at CostCo

My friend, who I will refer to as K, asked me: "Do you have any recommendations for an LCD TV cheap cheap?" K also mentioned that she's not afraid of off brands. So I went off to hunt for some cheap cheap LCD TVs. I usually don't like to recommend brands that I wouldn't purchase myself. After rummaging around the Internet a while I decided to go to CostCo. There I found what I think is a great buy!

Hitachi is known for its quality. And unfortunately it's high price. But this L32A102 model seems to be the contrary. It has all the standard features of a 32" LCD TV such as a 1366 x 768 pixel format, a very bright 500 cd/m2, a good number of ports and it's a solid brand. All for just $499.99. Shipping and handling is included. This might not be the cheapest 32" around but I say it's a great deal.

Source: CostCo
