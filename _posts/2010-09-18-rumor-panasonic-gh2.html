The Panasonic GH2, the successor to the GH1, is rumored to have:

  • 16MP versus the 12MP in the GH1
  • ISO up to 12800
  • Faster autofocus with 14-140mm
  • 5fps bursts
  • 1080/60i HD video recording in AVCHD at 24Mbps


Expect an official GH2 announced at Photokina. Source: 4/3 Rumors
