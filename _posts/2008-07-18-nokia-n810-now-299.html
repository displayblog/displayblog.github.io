Nokia N810 Specifications

Display: 4.13" Wide Touch TFT LCD
Pixel Format: 800 x 480
Colors: 65K
CPU: 400MHz TI HS OMAP 2420
Memory: 128MB DDR RAM, 256MB Flash, 2GB Internal Memory
Storage: miniSD and microSD (with extender) up to 8GB
Battery: Nokia Battery BP-4L, 10 hours (Music playback), 14 days (Standby), 4 hours (Internet browsing on WiFi)
Connectivity: WiFi (802.11b/g), WiMAX (on WiMAX version), Bluetooth 2.0 + EDR, USB 2.0, 3.5mm Stereo Headphone Plug
OS: maemo Linux OS2008

The Nokia N810 Internet tablet is going for just $299.99, down a significant $90. It is not only at CompUSA, Amazon and other places have the N810 at the much lower price. Maybe this is an indication that a new version is around the corner. The N810 sports a fairly large 4.13" TFT LCD with a pixel format of 800 x 480. It just misses the 1024 x 600 required to surf comfortably. Maybe the next version will eschew some buttons, make the LCD a bit bigger and get to that surf-in-comfort 1024 x 600 pixels. Maybe.
