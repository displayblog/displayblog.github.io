Samsung 2433BW Specifications

Display: 24" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1200
Response Time: 5ms
Contrast Ratio: 1000:1 (static), 20000:1 (dynamic)
Brightness: 300 cd/m2
Viewing Angles: 170/160
Connectivity: DVI-D, VGA
Power Consumption: 50W (operational), <1W (standby)
Dimensions: 22.4 x 17.9 x 8.6"
Weight: 11.7lbs
Price: MSRP US$409.99 ($279.95 at PC Connection after $50 rebate)

Samsung's 2433BW is a 24" LCD monitor with a typical aspect ratio of 16:10 meaning that it has a pixel format of 1920 x 1200 (instead of 1920 x 1080 found in most LCD TVs). The 2433BW has a relatively fast response time of 5ms so casual gaming won't pose much trouble. But don't try to watch too much HD on the 2433BW since it does not have any circuitry to reduce motion blur. You wouldn't expect that on a LCD monitor but since there are so many LCD monitors posing as LCD TVs that I needed to make it clear. The DVI-D connection should give you a sharp digital image without any flicker (the VGA connection can be less sharp). The viewing angle of 170/160 is due to the fact that Samsung used a TN LCD type in the 2433BW. Don't worry since you'll probably sitting right in front of the monitor. If you do have to have good color fidelity at different angles, the 2433BW is not the one for you (find LCD monitors with IPS or PVA LCDs in them). The static contrast ratio of 1000:1 is very good. The kicker? Just $279.95 at PC Connection!
