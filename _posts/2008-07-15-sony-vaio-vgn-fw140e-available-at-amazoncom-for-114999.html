Sony's just-announced FW series 16.4" notebook PC is available for pre-order at Amazon.com. The price is a very reasonable $1149.99 and in some areas that means no shipping and no taxes. The VGN-FW140E that is available has the following specifications:

  • 2.26GHz Intel Core 2 Duo P8400, 3MB L2 Cache
  • 16.4" LCD with XBRITE
  • 3GB of RAM, 250GB SATA HDD
  • Magnesium Chassis with Titanium Gray Finish


The highest-end model, the VGN-FW180E ups the CPU to 2.4GHz, has 4GB of RAM and 320GB HD with a Blu-ray drive for $1749.99; it is still a solid deal in my opinion.

Source: Amazon.com
