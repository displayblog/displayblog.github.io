NTT DoCoMo: The NEC MEDIAS N-06C is a waterproof 6-bit 4-inch Android 2.3 smartphone that sports a 854x480 pixel format and good for 262,144 colors. The interesting thing about some smartphones that are coming out of Japan is that they are waterproof despite a slender body. I'd like to see more phone manufacturers waterproof their smartphones. The NEC MEDIAS N-06C will be available in Japan on the NTT DoCoMo network around June / July.
