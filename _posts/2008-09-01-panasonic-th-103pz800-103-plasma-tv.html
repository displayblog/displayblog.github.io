Panasonic's TH-103PZ800 isn't the largest plasma display panel (PDP) TV on the planet. That distinction goes to the 150" 4K2K version. But practically speaking, the TH-103PZ800 is as big as you'll see in ultra-rich people's living rooms. I'm sure they have even bigger screens in their home theaters. The TH-103PZ800 sports the 'regular' 1920 x 1080 pixel format and a contrast ratio of 10,000:1. There are other specifications but the most impressive is that it's 103". If you look at the dimensions, it is 174cm high. I'm 183cm tall. It's pretty simple: the TH-103PZ800 is HUGE.
