A new less-than-N8 seems to be lurking around Nokia. C7-00, part of Nokia's C-series, slots beneath the N series and sports what the company calls a nHD pixel display. Another day, another display-related jargon: nHD. What does nHD stand for? In landscape it refers to a pixel format of 640x360. Not quite the highest resolution around since most smartphones seem to come with 800x480. According to Engadget the parsed XML code has the C7 sporting 3G UMTS, HSDPA, WiFi and Bluetooth connectivity.
