Samsung: The Samsung NC10-14GBK is a 10.2" netbook sporting a 1024x600 pixel format. The 1024 horizontal pixels will allow for easy web-browsing. The 10.2" TFT LCD has a 220 cd/m2 brightness, which is quite high. The NC10 also has a 1.3MP webcam, is powered by a 1.6GHz Atom CPU and can be loaded with up to 2GB RAM. The NC10 sports an elegant chassis and a 93% full-sized keyboard.
