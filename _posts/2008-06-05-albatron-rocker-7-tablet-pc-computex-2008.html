Albatron Rocker Specifications:

  • Display: 7" Touch
  • Pixel Format: 800 x 480
  • CPU: 400MHz ARM 9
  • Memory: 128MB Mobile DDR
  • Connectivity: WiFi (802.11b/g), Bluetooth
  • Storage: 128MB NAND Flash, 2-in-1 Card Reader (SD, MMC)
  • Webcam: Yes
  • OS: Windows Embedded CE 6.0
  • Battery: 6 hours
  • Other: Cradle has USB, supports TV module
  • Dimension: 188.3 x 113 x 12.9mm
  • Weight: 343g

AVING → Gizmodo: Albatron showcased its 7-inch tablet PC called the Rocker. It is touch capable and sports an ARM 9 CPU running at 400MHz and powering Windows Embedded CE 6.0. The pixel format of 800 x 480 is standard fair, but the 128MB of flash storage that you're limited to is tiny. You'll need to bring your own memory (BYOM?) in SD or MMC flavors. I do like the fact that Albatron was able to squeeze an entire OS into just 128MB of flash.
