TFT Central: The LTM270DL02 by Samsung is a 27-inch Super Plane to Line Switching (S-PLS) LCD module with a pixel format of 2560x1440. S-PLS is Samsung's answer to LG Display's IPS. Other specs include: 12-ms response time, 1000:1 typical contrast ratio, a brightness of 300 nits, 178/178 viewing angles, 8-bit color, and single-side white LED backlight. The Samsung SA850, a 27-inch LCD monitor, will be the first to sport the LTM270DL02 LCD module.
