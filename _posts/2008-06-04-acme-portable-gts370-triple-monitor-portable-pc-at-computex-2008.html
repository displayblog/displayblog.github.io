Acme Portable GTS370 Specifications
LCD: 17" 1280 x 1024 Anti-glare Glass Protected LCD Monitor (3)
Motherboard: Standard ATX
Slot: 7 Expansion Slots
HDD: 3.5" Hot Swappable SATA (3)
Optical: Slim DVD-ROM
Dimension: 14.3 x 15.5 x 11.5"

Acme Portable's GTS370 is a unique portable PC. It's not a notebook PC since you'll need to lug around a tower case. But it's not your average portable PC: the GTS370 has three 17" LCDs built-in. You flip them out and instantly you have either a terrific workstation or a play station! The glass protected 17" LCDs sport a pixel format of 1280 x 1024 for a total of 3840 x 1024 pixels. When you're done playing or working, just fold the LCDs, snap on the keyboard and off you go. I can see this as an ultimate portable gaming rig, for sure.

Source: AVING
