Panasonic released a firmware update for the GF1 on November 25, 2009. Here are the list of improvements:

  • AF (Auto Focus) performance in movie recording
  • AWB (Auto White Balance) performance
  • Picture quality in high sensitivity photo recording
  • Picture quality in portrait program modes
  • Aperture control in scenery program modes
  • Accessibility to MF (Manual Focus) Assist function with MF lenses.


You can get the updated firmware at http://panasonic.jp/support/global/cs/dsc/. Source: DPReview
