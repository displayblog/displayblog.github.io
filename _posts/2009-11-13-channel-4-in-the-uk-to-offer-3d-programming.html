3D fans in the UK rejoice. Channel 4 will offer 3D programming soon starting on November 16. What's the content?

  • The Queen: A documentary about the Queen's coronation.
  • Derren Brown's 3D Magic Spectacular
  • The Paul O'Grady Show
  • The Greatest Ever 3D Moments
  • Hannah Montana & Miley Cyrus: Best of Both Worlds Concert
  • T4, etc.


Will you need to wear glasses? Yes, the "ColorCode 3D" variety that you can get from Sainsbury's for free. You'll also want to adjust the distance from your TV as there is a small sweetspot around 1.5 meters.
