Chimei Innolux (CMI) announced unaudited consolidated revenues for October of NT$40.7 billion, down 10.7% M/M. Large-size LCD panel shipments were down 8.2% M/M to 9.65 million. Small/medium panel declined too, by 1.1% M/M to 37.3 million.
