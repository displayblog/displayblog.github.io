Panasonic TC-P54Z1 Specifications

Display: 54" NeoPDP
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 40,000:1 (static), 2,000,000:1 (dynamic)
Other: External Tuner Box Wirelessly Connected (WirelessHD), THX Certified Display
Dimensions: 1" thick
Availability: Summer 2009Â 

Panasonic's TC-P54Z1 is just 1" thick. As you can see from the video the 54" plasma TV is super-thin and looks great. Panasonic was able to make it so thin by taking out the tuner and connecting the tuner to the TV via a WirelessHD connection. The TC-P54Z1 sports a 1920 x 1080 pixel format and has an incredible 2,000,000:1 dynamic contrast ratio. The super-thin 54" plasma TV from Panasonic will be available in the summer of 2009.
