Samsung's UN40B6000 is a 40" LCD TV with a LED backlight. The LED is edge-lit allowing for an extremely thin profile. The 40" UN40B60000 is just 1.2" thick. The pixel format is 1920 x 1080 and the frequency is 120Hz that reduces motion blur. There are four USB ports including four HDMI connections. The UN40B6000 has a feature called mega contrast that improves contrast by global dimming--modulating the entire LED backlight based on the overall brightness or darkness of the video. The Advanced Color Processing improves the color so that the color is more vibrant and colorful. The 40" LED TV consumes 40% less power than conventional 40" LCD TVs. The UN40B6000 features Samsung's InfoLink RSS service that gives you access news, weather, sports and stock information.
