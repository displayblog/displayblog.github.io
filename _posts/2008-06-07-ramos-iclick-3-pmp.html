RAmos iclick Specifications
Display: 3.0" Touch
Pixel Format: 400 x 240
Color: 26K
Storage: 4GB Flash
Audio Support: MP3, WMA
Video Support: FLAC, APE, AVI, RM, RMVB
Price: 599 Yuan (US$86)

Originally, RAmos was slated to unveil its V8, with a rather large 4.3" LCD and 16GB of flash storage. Instead, what came out was the RAmos iclick. The iclick is smaller sporting a 3" LCD with a pixel format of 400 x 240. The specifications look pretty dull until you get to the price: US$86.

Source: MP4 Nation via Engadget
