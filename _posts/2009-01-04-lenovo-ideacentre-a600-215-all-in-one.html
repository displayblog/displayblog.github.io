Lenovo IdeaCentre A600 Specifications

Display: 21.5" TN (Twisted Nematic) TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Webcam: 1.3MP or 2MP
GPU: Integrated, Optional 256MB or 512MB ATI
CPU: Intel Pentium Dual Core or Intel Core 2 Duo Mobile Processors
RAM: 1GB - 4GB DDR3 1066MHz
HDD: Up to 1TB
Optical: Optional Blu-ray Player
Connectivity: WiFi ABG or ABGN, Ethernet, 6-in-1 Media Card Reader, USB 2.0 (6), FireWire
Other: Optional hybrid analog/digital
OS: Microsoft Vista Premium or Basic
Price: Starts at $999

Lenovo's IdeaCentre A600 is a 21.5" all-in-one desktop computer that looks very nice. I am guessing the 21.5" TFT LCD will be of the TN (Twisted Nematic) variety and will feature a relatively fast reponse time (around 5ms), limited viewing angles (170/160) and a contrast ratio that's around 1000:1. The A600 sports a 1920 x 1080 pixel format (that's an aspect ratio of 16:9) that will mate quite well with the optional Blu-ray player. The external design is quite compeling but the picture looks like a CAD screenshot rather than an actual picture.
