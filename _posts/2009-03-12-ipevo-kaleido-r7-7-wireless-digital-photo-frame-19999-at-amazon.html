IPEVO's Kaleido R7 is a wireless digital photo frame sporting a 7" LCD with a 800 x 480 pixel format. The external design is nice and clean with a base that's a bit similar to the one found on Sony's XEL-1 (the 11" OLED TV). The base makes for a more solid feel when you're pushing buttons--better than having to worry about the display being pushed back, wobbling, etc. when the buttons are on the display. Sometimes you need two hands: one to hold the display and the other to push the buttons. Another nice feature of the Kaleido R7 is that the display can be rotated so you can view pictures taken in portrait and landscape. Of course if there was an auto-pivot feature that pivots depending on the photo now that would be cool. Finally the R7 is wireless and syncs up to your Mac, PC or your mobile phone. It is available at Amazon for $199.99.
