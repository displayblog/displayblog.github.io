The Airlife 100 is a smartbook by Compaq. Interesting, since Compaq is a more business-oriented brand. The HP Airlife 100 sounds more fitting.

The specs: Android OS, Qualcomm Snapdragon CPU (probably), 16GB SSD, 3G, WiFi, and a 10.1-inch touch TFT LCD.

Battery life is a whopping 12 hours and an even more impressive 10 days of standby.

The Airlife 100's design is nice and simple, utilitarian but cute in a way. The LCD bezel could be a bit thinner all around.

The Compaq Airlife 100 is available through Telefonica at a subsidized price in Europe and Latin America. Source: Engadget Spanish via Engadget
