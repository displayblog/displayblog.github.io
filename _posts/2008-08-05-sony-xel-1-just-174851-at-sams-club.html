Sony's XEL-1 is the only OLED TV that is commercially available. But at just 11" and with a very expensive price tag of $2500, the XEL-1 OLED TVs haven't been flying off the shelves. Well, Sam's Club has the right idea. Slash the price, to $1748.51 in this case, and let's see what happens! My guess is that there will be a bit more sales, but not a whole lot since $1748.51 for a super-thin 11" OLED TV is still very expensive.
