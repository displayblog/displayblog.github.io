The new iPod touch that Apple just announced has an anemic image sensor that allows 960x720 for stills and 1280x720 for videos. Does that make sense? I would think if the sensor is capable of 1280x720 for videos that you'd be able to take 1280x720 stills.

Or maybe the videos aren't 1280x720. Maybe they are actually 960x720 passed off as HD video. Does anyone know for sure?
