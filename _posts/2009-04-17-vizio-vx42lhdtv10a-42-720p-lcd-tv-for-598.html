720p 42" LCD TV In the market for a 42" LCD TV? Don't mind it being 720p instead of 1080p? (BTW, your eyes won't know the difference unless your mom yells at you, "You're too close to the TV!") Vizio's VX42LHDTV10A is a 42" 720p LCD TV that has all the standard features such as a very decent 1000:1 contrast ratio, a bright enough 500 cd/m2, and solid viewing angles of 178/178.

Decent But No 120Hz The only thing about this 42-incher is the response time: 8ms--it's a bit slow. So hopefully you're not a sports buff glued to Sportscenter. If you are, you should consider a TV with 120Hz. This one is 60Hz. The 42" Vizio has all the requisite ports: 2 HDMI (one more would have been better), VGA (for hooking up your PC), two component and two composite connections.

Awesome Price The best feature, if you can call it that, is the price. The VX42LHDV is uust $598 at Walmart!
