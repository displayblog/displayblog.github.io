AllThingsD: Apple sells 25% at its Apple Stores, and here's how the rest break down:

  • AT&T: 21%
  • Verizon: 18%
  • Best Buy: 13%
  • Sprint: 5%
  • Amazon: 5%

The rest are bought from large retailers: Target, Walmart, Costco, etc.
