Hitachi Wooo UT47-XP770 Specifications

Display: 47" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Brightness: 500 cd/m2
Tuner: ATSC
Connectivity: HDMI-CEC (3), DLNA, VGA
Speaker: 6W (2)
Other: 250GB HD
Colors: Black (UT47-XP700B), White (UT47-XP700W)
Power Consumption: 259W (operational), 0.5W (standby), 234kWh/year
Dimensions:
114.6 x 74.5 x 3.5 - 3.9cm (without stand)
114.6 x 79.5 x 31.0cm (with stand)
Availability: September 26, 2008 (UT47-XP700B), mid-October 2008 (UT47-XP700W)

Hitachi's Wooo UT47-XP770 has a funky name. Wooo? Huh? I thought Mazda's Zoom-Zoom was weird. Aside from the name, the UT47-XP770 LCD TV is pretty standard. The 47" LCD TV sports a pixel format of 1920 x 1080 with a brightness of 500 cd/m2. All pretty standard. Similar to what LG has done with some of its LCD TVs, Hitachi is incorporating a hard disk (250GB) into its UT47-XP770 line for DVR duties. I can't quite make out all the rest of the specifications as they are written in Japanese. I'm not sure if the UT47-XP770 has 120Hz, motion interpolation, dynamic contrast capability, etc. The UT47-XV700 is the same as the UT47-XP770 but without a hard drive. Anyone with English specifications, please let me know. You can email me at jin@displayblog.com or post a comment. Thanks!
