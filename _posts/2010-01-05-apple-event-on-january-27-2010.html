Originally the date was rumored to be the 26th of January for an Apple event. According to John Paczkowski at All Things D, Apple's event will be on the 27th instead, on a Wednesday. Nobody really knows what Apple will be announcing but there is a good chance that it will be a multitouch tablet of sorts. Mac4Ever is reporting that developers have been given an iPhone OS 4 beta SDK with a simulator that allows for reformatting apps for a new screen size and resolution. The Wall Street Journal is also postulating that this yet-to-be-announced tablet will be shipping in March. Source: All Things D, Mac4Ever, The Wall Street Journal via Engadget
