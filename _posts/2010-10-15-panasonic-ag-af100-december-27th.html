The Panasonic AG-AF100 is a Micro Four Thirds HD camcorder and it will be available on December 27th for US$4995. The AG-AF100 can record 1080/12-60p video and comes with SD SDI, XLR connections, two SDXC slots and can be equipped with Lumix G lenses. via DPReview
