Viewsonic VX1962WM
Size: 19"
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1680 x 1050
Contrast Ratio: 1,000:1 (native), 6,000:1 (dynamic)
Response Time: 2ms
Brightness: 300 cd/m2
Viewing Angle: 170/150
Lightsource: CCFL, 40,000 hours
Input: DVI-D, VGA
Power Consumption: 36W (operational)
Dimension:
w/ stand: 17.6 x 15.7 x 6.9" or 447 x 399 x 175mm
w/o stand: 17.6 x 12.5 x 2.0" or 447 x 317 x 51mm
Weight:
w/ stand: 8.6lbs or 3.9kg
w/o stand: 6.8lbs or 3.1kg
Price: MSRP $250.00

Viewsonic's VX1962WM is a unique LCD monitor. It is a 19" wide LCD monitor but unlike most other 19" wides the VX1962WM sports a much higher pixel format of 1680 x 1050. Others are limited to just 1440 x 900. In addition the fast response time of just 2ms will make gamers happy. Those who value good quality images will appreciate the 6,000:1 dynamic contrast ratio, which is quite terrific for a 19" LCD monitor. Because of the use of a TN LCD the viewing angle suffers just a little bit at 170/150. Just don't move up or down. Don't expect to use the VX1962WM for a lot of HD viewing via the DVI-D connection because I don't think the DVI has HDCP; just use good old VGA instead. [As of this writing the VX1962WM is not yet available.]
