The New York Times is offering $100 off on a Samsung Go netbook if you subscribe to Times Reader 2.0 for a year. The subscription costs $179.40. This is perfect for that special someone who was planning to purchase a netbook, the Samsung Go, and was renewing his or her subscription. J&R Music and Computer World will be the exclusive sellers. Samsung's Go netbook sports a 10.1-inch LCD with a 1024 x 600 resolution, 1GB RAM, 160GB hard drive and is powered by Intel's Atom N270. Source: The New York Times via BusinessWire, Engadget
