AOC 2218ph Specifications

Display: 22" Wide TFT LCD
Pixel Format: 1680 x 1050
LCD: 6-bit + FRC (Dithered 16.2 million colors)
Contrast Ratio: 12,000:1 (dynamic)
Response Time: 2ms
Connectivity: HDMI with HDCP
Price: MSRP US$429.99

On July 10, 2008, AOC announced its 2218ph 22" LCD monitor touting it as the monitor that "brings PC users the element of style Mac users have enjoyed for the last few years..." Well, that's nice, and it's got the Mac pricing to boot at US$429.99. For a 22" LCD monitor that's quite expensive, especially since the LCD portion of it is nothing special except for two things.

One is the 12,000:1 dynamic contrast ratio. This isn't much anything unless you're watching movies on it. The second special feature is indeed special. 2ms response time will allow you to frag as fast as you humanly can. Overall the design looks very nice, but to compare it to Apple's Cinema HD displays is, well, not quite an apples to apples comparison. For one Apple's displays look great but they are backed up with high-end IPS LCD panels.
