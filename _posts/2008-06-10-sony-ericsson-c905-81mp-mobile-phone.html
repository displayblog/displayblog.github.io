Connectivity: GPS, WiFi, Bluetooth, USB 2.0, TV-out

Original Post

Sony Ericsson's C905 device can be described in two ways. One: It is a digital camera with mobile phone functions. Two: It is a mobile phone with a full digital camera capability. Either way is right one describing the C905. The digital camera capability is astounding and the feature set comes straight out of a real digital camera spec sheet:

  • Auto focus
  • Red-eye reduction
  • Face recognition
  • Image stabilization: most compact cameras don't even have this feature!
  • Xenon flash for both photos and videos


If you really wanted a good reason to get rid of your digital camera for the sake of having just one device instead of two, then there isn't much out there that can rival the C905. The one thing I would have liked is optical zoom.
