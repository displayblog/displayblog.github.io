Audi has what it calls Multi Media Interface or MMI for short. The MMI on the 2011 A8 can recognize your handwriting: just scribble your destination and MMI takes care of the rest. German? Of course. English? Yes, and other languages are supported too: Cyrillic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean. There is a long video showing off the A8; if you want to see the handwriting recognition at work, jump to 7:47. Source: Audi via Auto Spies, Engadget
