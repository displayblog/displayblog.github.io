Samsung's favorite adjective? You guessed it: super. Samsung's super narrow bezel 55-inch DID LCD does indeed sport some very thin bezels: 3.8 mm on the top and left and just 1.9 mm on the bottom and right. Combined, the bezel thickness between these 55-inch DIDs is just 5.7 mm. Very cool. Now if only our monitors and notebook PC displays had bezels this thin. Source: Samsung Korea
