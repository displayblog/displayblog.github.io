The Conductor DMC350 Wireless-N Digital Music Center from Linksys looks mighty good. It is an all-in-one multimedia player that has built-in speakers and a 7" TFT LCD with touch capabilities for browsing all the multimedia files you have. Oh, there's also a CD player built-in. The price is TBD. I'm looking forward to putting my fingerprints on the 7" LCD during CES and possibly replacing my aging Denon music system (15+ years old) with one of these beauties.
