On December 8, 2009 Chi Mei Optoelectronics (CMO) announced November 2009 TFT LCD results. TFT-LCD related revenues for the month of November increased 8.7% M/M and 144.2% Y/Y to NT$29.9 billion. CMO's large-sized LCD panel shipments increased 5.2% M/M and 108.6% Y/Y to 7.1 million units. Small/medium LCD panel shipments decreased 25.2% M/M but increased 184.2% Y/Y to 14.3 million units. Source: CMO
