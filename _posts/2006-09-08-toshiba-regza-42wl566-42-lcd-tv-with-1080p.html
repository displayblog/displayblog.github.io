Toshiba's 42WL566 is a 42" LCD TV with Full HD resolution at 1920 x 1080. The folks at T3 pointed out the plasticky bezel but also noted that the picture quality was fantastic. At less than $2000, the 42WL566 is a bargain reported the article. Trustred Reviews gave the 42WLT66 a 10 out of 10 rating for value. The contrast ratio is 800:1, brightness at 550 cd/m2, and has two HDMI ports, two SCARTs, and a PC input among other non-HD input methods.

I must say the box itself is nothing to call home about. The brand Toshiba is also not on the top list of brands I would want to bring home and proudly display in my living for everyone to look at. However, the price is competitive for a 1080p unit.
