Now this is cool! (I hope it is real.) Texas Instruments (TI) somehow incorporated a pico projector into a BlackBerry Curve. The BlackBerry Curve seems to be the right shape, though I would like to have seen the BlackBerry functioning normally.

The two green-lit buttons and the viewing angle of the LCD (it doesn't change!) make me wonder if the pico projector is taking up the entire inside of the BlackBerry Curve case.
