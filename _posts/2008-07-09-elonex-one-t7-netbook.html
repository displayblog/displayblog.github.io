The Elonex One T is the successor to the One. The One was an interesting netbook that had all the guts behind the LCD. The One T has it in a more regular place: underneath the keyboard. There isn't much information regarding the specifications of the One, but it sports a 7" LCD with a pixel format of 800 x 480. The 800 x 480 pixel format won't be that great when crazily surfing the net, but the occasional surf and email shouldn't be too painful. The color of the Elonex One reminds me of the OLPC.
