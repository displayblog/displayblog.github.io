Fujitsu P70WN Specifications
Display: 8.9" Touch TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 15:9
Pixel Format: 1280 x 768
CPU: Intel Core Solo U1400
HDD: 60GB or 80GB
Weight: 2.2lbs

Fujitsu's P70WN is a 8.9" convertible tablet PC. It has a touch-enabled 8.9" LCD with a pixel format of 1280 x 768, quite a lot for something this small. Looks like the P70WN, along with its less powerful P50WN brethren, will be popular with the globetrotting folks.
