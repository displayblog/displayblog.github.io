NEC P221W Specifications

Display: 22" S-PVA TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1680 x 1050
Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
Brightness: 300 cd/m2
Response Time: 16ms
Color Gamut: 96% Adobe RGB
Adjustability: Tilt, Swivel, Pivot, Height Adjust
Price: US$636.90

On October 23, 2008 NEC announced its P221W. The P221W sports a 22" S-PVA TFT LCD from Samsung with a pixel format of 1680 x 1050. Other features include a contrast ratio of 1000:1, brightness of 300 cd/m2 and a response time of 16ms. This is definitely not geared toward gamers but focused on color professionals. The color gamut is 95% of Adobe RGB and is most likely using a wide color gamut CCFL (WCG-CCFL) backlight. Make sure to calibrate the P221W using a hardware calibration tool before starting color work on it. The price is a bit on the high side at US$636.90; color accuracy costs a bit more.
