This VIZIO TV is just as wide as the title with a 21:9 aspect ratio, and a 2560x1080 pixel format. Comes in three sizes: 50-inch (XVT3D500CM), 58-inch (XVT3D580CM), and 71-inch (XVT3D710CM).

Update: The 58-inch will be available in March for US$3499.

Update 2012.06.27: March came and went and now it's late June. For those of you who waited, it was worth it. The price is now just $2499.99 for the 58-inch model.
