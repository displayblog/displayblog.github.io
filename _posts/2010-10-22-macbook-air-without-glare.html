TechRestore is offering a matte screen replacement service for the new MacBook Air. For US$249 you get a non-glare LCD with the same everything: pixel format, LED backlight, etc. In addition TechRestore will install a custom black bezel that makes the entire package look a bit more like the MacBook Pro. Turnaround time of just 24 hours is promised. Source: TechRestore
