Shuttle's Fantasy (or Illusion?) mini PC was showcased during Computex 2008. The unique thing about the Shuttle Fantasy is that it looks like a shopping bag--a very nice one made out of some plastic and aluminum parts. I hope wherever you take the Fantasy you have access to a monitor and a keyboard at the least. It would have been better if this was a notebook PC instead.
