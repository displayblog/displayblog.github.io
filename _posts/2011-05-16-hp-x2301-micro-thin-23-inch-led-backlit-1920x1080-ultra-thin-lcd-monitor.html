Engadget: The HP x2301 Micro Thin is a 23-inch LED backlit LCD monitor with a 1920x1080 pixel format that is just 9.8-mm thin (about 0.39 inches). HP took most of the electronics from behind the LCD to the base. The ultra-thin x2301 Micro Thin sports a fairly quick 3-ms response time and will be available on June 10 for US$280.
