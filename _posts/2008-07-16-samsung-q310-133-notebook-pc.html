Samsung Q310 Specifications

  • Display: 13.3" Wide TFT LCD
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:10
  • Pixel Format: 1280x800 (?)
  • Webcam: 1.3MP
  • Chipset: Centrino 2
  • GPU: 256MB NVIDIA GeForce 9200M GS
  • Connectivity: HDMI, Bluetooth 2.0, WiFi (802.11n)
  • Weight: 2kg
  • Availability: August 2008

Le Journal du Geek: The Samsung Q310 uses the latest Centrino 2 chipset from Intel. The Q310 sports a 13.3" wide TFT LCD. The pixel format of the LCD is unknown but there could be a couple of choices: 1280x800 or 1440x900. The integrated webcam is 1.3MP and is nicely integrated into the bezel of the LCD.

Powering the 13.3" display will be NVIDIA's GeForce 9200M GS. While not the most powerful GPU, it should be enough for most users who need to work as well as play. A lot of the newer notebook PCs are featuring HDMI connects, and the Q310 is no exception. Samsung will make the Q310 available in August 2008, most likely limited to South Korea and other Asian markets.
