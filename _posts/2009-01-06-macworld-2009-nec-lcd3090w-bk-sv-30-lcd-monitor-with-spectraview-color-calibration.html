NEC LCD3090W-BK-SV Specifications

Display: 30" IPS (In-Plane Switching) TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 2560 x 1600
Color Gamut: 107% AdobeRGB
Response Time: 6ms (GTG), 12ms (typical)
Brightness: 350 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
Connectivity: DVI-D with HDCP, DVI-I
Other: SpectraView (SVII-PRO-KIT) color calibration kit
Warranty: 4-year Parts and Labor
Price: MSRP US$2449

NEC showcased its LCD3090W-BK-SV LCD monitor at MacWorld 2009. The LCD3090W-BK-SV is a 30" LCD monitor sporting a pixel format of 2560 x 1600 and uses an IPS (In-Plane Switching) LCD panel known for really great color fidelity and viewing angles. The LCD3090W-BK-SV comes with a color calibration kit called SpectraView and includes the hardware and software to make sure that colors are exactly as they should. The 6ms GTG (gray-to-gray) response is fairly fast for such a high pixel count monitor and the 1000:1 contrast ratio is excellent. Clearly the LCD2090W-BK-SV is geared toward the professional market for those who require high color fidelity. The price? US$2449 and is shipping this month.
