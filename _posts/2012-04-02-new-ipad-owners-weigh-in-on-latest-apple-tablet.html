ChangeWave:

User satisfaction with the new iPad is even higher than previous iPad ratings from a February 2012 ChangeWave survey. More than four-out-of-five new iPad owners (82%) say they are Very Satisfied with the device.

Only 200 new owners were surveyed, but it seems the new iPad is off to a good start.
