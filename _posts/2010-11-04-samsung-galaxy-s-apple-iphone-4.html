BCN → The Chosunilbo: The fine print is that this is for just one week, just for individual models, and in Japan.

According to a ranking by Japanese market researcher BCN, Samsung's flagship smartphone dethroned the 32GB iPhone 4 as the top-selling cell phone for the last week of October, its debut week there.

The 16GB iPhone 4 was ranked #3. Combine the two and you get: iPhone 4 > Galaxy S. The big news is that the Samsung Galaxy S broke through the top two models, both iPhone 4s, that have kept the top two spots for 18 weeks in Japan since their release in June.
