Engadget took the iPhone 4 and the Nokia N8 and took some pictures and videos. The conclusion? The N8 took more neutral and probably more accurate pictures. The photos from the iPhone 4 though they look more pleasant are blown up in terms of saturation. I'll take accuracy over artificial prettiness.

I've been considering the possibility of not having a separate camera and simply using a smartphone, like the iPhone 4, for picture-taking duties. But the more I see iPhone 4 sample photos the more I realize I really do need a nice digital camera.
