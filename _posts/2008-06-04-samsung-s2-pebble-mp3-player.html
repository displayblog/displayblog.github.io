Samsung's S2 MP3 player is very well designed. Simple. Cute. And has a huge "I want it" factor. If only flexible OLED displays were actually around, these S2 pebbles would be seriously cool.

The cute little MP3 players come in white, red, green, purple and black. I would like translucent versions of these please. You've got 1GB or 2GB options and these play back MP3, WMA and Ogg audio files.
