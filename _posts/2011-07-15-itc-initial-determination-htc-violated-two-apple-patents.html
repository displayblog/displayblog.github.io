Roger Cheng at CNET:

The ITC judge's initial determination was that HTC infringed on two of the 10 patents Apple had filed a complaint over in March, according to an HTC statement. A loss carries the threat that HTC's products would be banned from coming into the U.S., and Apple only needs to get a favorable decision on one of the patents.
