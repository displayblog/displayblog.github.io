Novelquest Emperor Workstation Specifications
Availability: July 2008

Novelquest's Emperor workstation was designed based on the emperor scorpion's tail. The tail section lifts up to allow the user to enter. Once seated the tail comes back down into operational position. And that's is brilliant! I was thinking whether the three smallish LCD monitors can be changed for three of Apple's 30" LCD monitors. I'm not sure if the tail can handle that much weight. The Emperor workstation can be custom ordered with a Mac or PC desktop or a notebook PC.
