Verizon (Flash required) → Engadget: The Samsung Galaxy Tab is now available on Verizon for $599.99 with a new two-year contract. If you've been wanting a Galaxy Tab on the more reliable network in the US this is very good news. You get two data plans to choose from: $20 per month for 1GB or $80 for 10GB. But if you don't want to pay that much you can get it for just $399.99 at Sprint.
