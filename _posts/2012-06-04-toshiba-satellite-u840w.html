Evan Rodgers, The Verge:

Today, Toshiba is announcing the Satellite U840W ultrabook with a surprising 21:9, 1792 x 768 display.

With a 2.33:1 aspect ratio the U840W is the widest notebook out there today. The other really wide notebook was the Sony VAIO P with 2.08:1.
