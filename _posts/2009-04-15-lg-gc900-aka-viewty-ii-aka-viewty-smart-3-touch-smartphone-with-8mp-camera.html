LG GC900, Viewty II, Viewty Smart Specifications

Display: 3" Touch TFT LCD
Camera: 8MP
Video: 720 x 480 Capture
Connectivity: GPS, WiFi
Network: Orange (HSDPA)
UI: 3D S-Class
Dimensions: 12.4mm thick
Weight: 3.17oz (90g)

LG's Viewty II. Or Viewty Smart... or just GC900. The nicely designed (for the most part) smartphone sports a 3" touch TFT LCD and makes use of the company's 3D S-Class user interface. The ultra-thin smartphone has a 8 megapixel camera that takes pictures. And video at 720 x 480. That's nice but where's 1280 x 720 video recording like Samsung's OMNIAHD? The unit looks sleek but I would get rid of the obnoxious logo on the front (and put it on the back), get rid of the "S-Class User Interface" text (who really needs to see that all the time?) and increase the LCD size a bit... to say 3.5".
