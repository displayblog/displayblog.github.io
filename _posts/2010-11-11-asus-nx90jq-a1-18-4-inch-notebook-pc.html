PC Magazine: The ASUS NX90JQ-A1 is a 18.4-inch notebook PC with a 1920x1080 pixel format, which matches up quite well with the slot-loading Blu-ray drive. The unique feature about this monster notebook is the 11-watt amp that drives the speakers, a collaboration with Bang & Olufsen. Other specs include: dual-640GB 7200 RPM hard drives, 1.6GHz Core i7, 8GB RAM, NVIDIA GeForce GT 335M. Priced at around US$2500.
