HP TouchSmart 2 Specifications
Display: 22" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1680 x 1050
GPU: 256MB NVIDIA GeForce 9300M GS HD
Connectivity: WiFi (802.11b/g/n), Bluetooth 2.0, FireWire
CPU: 2.16GHz Intel Core Duo T5850
RAM: 4GB
Storage: 500GB
Tuner: No (IQ504), Yes (IQ506)
Optical: DVD burner
Other: Wireless keyboard and mouse, Vista Media Center remote
Dimension: 2.6" thick
Price: $1299 (IQ504), $1499 (IQ506)

HP's TouchSmart 2 is a pretty smart looking all-in-one PC. The TouchSmart 2 sports a 22" TFT LCD that sports a 1680 x 1050 pixel format: not quite HD of any sort, but can easily handle 720p HD content. It could probably scale down 1080p HD content as well. The TouchSmart 2 is only smart about a single touch (and gestures) at a time; no multitouch business going on here. But to be quite technical, the TouchSmart 2 isn't quite touch capable. You see, it uses optical sensors in front of the LCD to determine if where your finger is to detect "touch". This might be a good thing since you won't need to constantly clean the massive (compared to the iPhone) 22" LCD.
