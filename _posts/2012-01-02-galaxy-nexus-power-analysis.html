via Ben Brooks. REDDIT user iansmith6 ran some tests on his Galaxy Nexus. USB power into the Galaxy Nexus is ~4.5W. Power consumption by Google Maps: 4W peaking at 5.1W. Brooks:

And that, folks, is how you drain a cellphone battery while it is attached to a charger.

By the way, in airplane mode with only a bright white solid color showing the Galaxy Nexus consumes 2.9W. OLED consumes a lot of power displaying white so if you're an owner make everything as dark colored as possible.

Update: via Engadget: A new version of Google Maps, with "better battery life".
