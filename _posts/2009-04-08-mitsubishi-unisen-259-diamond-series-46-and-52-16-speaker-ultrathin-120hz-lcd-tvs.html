Mitsubishi Unisen 259 Diamond Series Specifications

Display: 46", 52" TFT LCD with Wide Color Gamut Backlight
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Frequency: 120Hz Film Motion
Color Gamut: 90% NTSC
Connectivity: HDMI v1.3a (4), USB, Wired IR
Video Processing: Plush1080p G5 18-bit
Speakers: 16-speaker Audio Bar, 5.1-channel
Other: UltraThin Frame, iSP Calibration Microphone
Power Consumption: EnergyStar 3.0
Availability: May 2009
Pricing: 46" - US$2799, 52" - $3299

16 Speakers Mitsubishi's top-of-the-line Unisen series is the 259 Diamond LCD TVs. They come in just two sizes: 46" and 52". The most unique feature is the speaker system, which integrates 16 speakers in an audio bar located beneath the LCD. The 16 speakers are intelligent too: it directs "perfectly balanced audio waves beside and behind the user to reproduce 5.1 surround sound". For those that don't feel like lugging 6 or 7 speakers, wiring them all, and then fine-tuning them might want to check this out.

90% Color Gamut The LCD is no slouch either with a 1920 x 1080 pixel format and a motion blur-busting 120Hz frequency. The press release states a wide color gamut backlight that provides a color gamut that is 25% better than typical LCD TVs. Since typical LCD TVs have a 72% NTSC color gamut, upping that by 25% yields 90%. Not bad. Whether the Unisen models use a backlight based on LED or CCFL is not clear. But the thickness of the units at less than 1" seems to suggest that the backlight is LED. Just a guess.
