Engadget: The LG P330 sports a 13.3-inch narrow bezel technology LCD some refer to as Shuriken Display. But from the photos online the bezels don't look particularly narrow to me. The bottom bezel, maybe following Dell's lead, is especially thick, much thicker than those on the 13.3-inch MacBook Pro.

Another intriguing design choice is the trackpad location: it's not centered like it is on MacBooks, but off to the left a bit. Instead of centering the trackpad based on the width of the chassis, LG decided to align it with the space bar.
