The Milestone from Motorola is the Euro-Droid and will be available in the UK very soon. The Milestone ad is quite different from Droid ads and shows how Motorola sees the two different markets. We need explosions, slang-laden one-liners in the US while the customers in the UK and Europe would rather indulge in information delivered in a sophisticated way. Although I like the more sophisticated approach, it lacks punch. Perhaps Motorola should go for something in between. Source: YouTube via Pocket Lint, Engadget
