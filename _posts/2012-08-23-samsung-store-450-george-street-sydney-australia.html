Asher Moses, The Sydney Morning Herald:

Everything from the store layout to the sales staff to the products and even the packaging and promotional material is uncannily Apple-esque.

I don't see it. The tables are much smaller and are not made of thick solid wood. Samsung's blue shirt is a polo with the name tag pinned on; Apple's blue shirt doesn't have a collar and the name tag is not pinned on. The Samsung store is an entire block away from the Apple Store. There's absolutely no copying here folks.
