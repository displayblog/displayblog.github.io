Sony BRAVIA KDL-32L4000 Specifications

Display: 32" (31.5") TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1366 x 768
Contrast Ratio: 2000:1
Viewing Angle: 178/178
Connectivity: Component, Composite (2), HDMI (3), VGA, RF, S-Video
Tuner: ATSC, NTSC, QAM
Power Consumption: Energy Start compliant
Dimensions:
807 x 585 x 242mm (with stand)
807 x 548 x 101mm (without stand)
Weight: 16kg (with stand), 13.8kg (without stand)
Price: US$699.99

If you're in the market for a Sony LCD TV, but don't want to pay the premium price, the company's KDL-32L4000 might be just what you're looking for. The KDL-32L4000 does not have all of the latest features, but it doesn't break your bank either. The KDL-32L4000 is a 32" LCD TV and sports a pixel format of 1366 x 768. The limited pixel format, compared to 1920 x 1080, does not make much of a difference and actually looks better when you are watching regular DVDs and standard definition TV programs. Even cable channels that provide 720p content look better on 1366 x 768 than on a LCD TV with Full HD. Of course, keep in mind you'll get some motion blur since motion blur killing technologies such as 120Hz and motion interpolation is absent.

The KDL-32L4000 has all of the required inputs to get you connected with your video sources. I'm still playing GT4 on my PlayStation 2 and getting my arms sore on the Nintendo Wii and something like this 32" LCD TV would be a great fit; no need for a 1080p LCD TV. The bottom line is if you don't have 1080p video sources to hook up your TV to, but still want a Sony, the KDL-32L4000 will make it easy on the eyes and the wallet. It's Energy Star compliant too.

Source: Sony
