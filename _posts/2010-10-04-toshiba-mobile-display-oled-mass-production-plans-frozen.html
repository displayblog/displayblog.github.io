Toshiba Mobile Display (TMD), a joint venture between Toshiba and Panasonic, invested JPY16 billion in 2008 to build an OLED manufacturing line in the Ishikawa Prefecture. Masahiro Kume, TMD spokesperson, via Reuters:

The plan (for mass-production) is currently frozen. We'll review the production plan again from scratch.

There is only one OLED game in town and that's Samsung Mobile Display (SMD). And it's a special type of OLED using Nouvoyance's PenTile Matrix sub-pixel technology.
