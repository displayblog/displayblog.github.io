Epson: Just like the title says, announced on May 17, Sanyo Epson's newly developed technology enables 90-degree viewing angles from top, bottom, left, or right and will be available in five sizes: 2.4", 2.5", 2.8", 7.0" and 10.1". Mass production will commence in the fall of 2006. Possible applications are: car navigation, in-car entertainment systems, digital cameras and multi-function mobile phones.
