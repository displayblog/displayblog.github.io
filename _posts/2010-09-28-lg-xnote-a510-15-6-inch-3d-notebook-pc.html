Engadget: I'm not fond of 3D (read Dvorak: 3D Bad For Your Eyes), but if you're a fan, LG has announced the XNOTE A510, a 15.6-inch 3D notebook PC. The 15.6-inch LCD sports a 1920x1080 pixel format powered by NVIDIA's GeForce GT 425M with 1GB. Polarized 3D glasses are included and if you already wear glasses there's even a pair of clip-on 3D lenses. TriDef 2D-to-3D conversion software is included. Other specs include: HDMI 1.4 port, Blu-ray drive, fingerprint reader. The A510 will be available in October.
