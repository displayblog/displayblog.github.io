The Eee PC T91MT from ASUS is convertible netbook sporting an 8.9-inch multitouch LCD with a LED backlight. Other specs include:

  • CPU: 1.33GHz Intel Atom Z520
  • RAM: 1GB DDR2 (2GB Max)
  • Storage: 32GB SSD (White)
  • Connectivity: Bluetooth
  • OS: Windows 7 Home Premium
  • Weight: 2.1 pounds
  • Dimensions: 8.9 x 6.5 x 1-inch


The white version, T91MT-PU17-WT, is now available on Amazon for US$532.00. Battery life is rated at 5 hours.
