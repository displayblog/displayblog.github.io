On May 4, Acer launched the TravelMate 3020. It is white and has an Intel Core Duo CPU running at 1.66-2.16GHz, a 12.1" WXGA (don't know if it is 1280*768 or 1280*800), 802.11a/b/g, no internal optical drive, roughly 1" thick, and will retail for a little less than $800.
