Apple MacBook Specifications

Display: 13.3" TFT LCDwith LED Backlight
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1280 x 800
GPU: NVIDIA GeForce 9400M
Connectivity: DVI, Dual-DVI, VGA
CPU: 2.0 to 2.4GHz GHz Intel Core 2 Duo, 3MB L2 Cache
RAM: 2 to 4GB 1066MHz DDR3
HDD: 160, 250, 320GB 5400RPM, 128GB SSD
Optical: Slot-loading SuperDrive
Other: Backlit keyboard
Battery: 5 hours
Price: Starts at US$999 (not for the new MacBooks, those start at US$1299)

Update 2008.10.15 4:39PM PST I came to the Valley Fair (Westfield) Apple Store in San Jose to check out the new MacBooks but they are nowhere in sight. So I asked someone and the guy told me that, "We had it in stock and before we could set them up for display, they were all sold." So I asked him if there was a line and he replied, "Yup." I guess the new MacBooks and MBPs are popular! Updating from the Apple Store via a 20" iMac. :-)

Original The new MacBook has a LED backlit LCD, a multitouch glass trackpad, an anluminum unibody enclosure, the mini-DisplayPort connector, and the new integrated NVIDA GeForce 9400M GPU, which is quite a bit faster than the Intel part Apple was using up to now.

Wow, 2 out of 2. First the 24" LED Cinema Display comes sporting a LED backlight, just like I thought it would. And now the new MacBook has a LED backlight too. Way back on May 24, 2008, I guessed that MacBooks would go LED in 2008, a counter argument to a DigiTimes' report that Kenmos Technology, the Taiwan-based backlight unit (BLU) supplier, stated that Apple's MacBooks would go all LED BLU in 2009. I guessed that it would go 100% LED BLU in 2008. I wish I could have been this lucky with the stock market...
