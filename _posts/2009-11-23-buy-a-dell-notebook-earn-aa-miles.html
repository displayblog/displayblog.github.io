Dell and American Arlines (AA) have teamed up to provide an interesting business proposition: buy a Dell notebook PC and earn AA miles. For instance, buy an Inspiron Mini 10 for US$469 and earn 2345 miles. Or if you need power purchase the Studio XPS 16 for $1299 and earn 6995 miles. You need an AAdvantage account to access the Premier site on Dell. Source: Dell
