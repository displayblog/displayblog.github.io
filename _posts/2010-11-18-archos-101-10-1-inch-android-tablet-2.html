Archos: I think the Archos 101 is the largest shipping Android tablet at 10.1 inches. Unfortunately you don't get more pixels: just 1024x600, the same as on the much smaller 7-inch Samsung Galaxy Tab. Touch is thankfully capacitive. The Archos 101 is WiFi only without a 3G option and makes this 10.1-inch Android tablet quite affordable: the 8GB version goes for US$299.99 and the 16GB for $349.99.
