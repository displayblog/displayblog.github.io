NTT DoCoMo (Japanese): The LG L-03C looks like a retro digital camera. It has a 12 megapixel CCD image sensor and a 3x optical zoom even, with a Xenon flash. Video capture is in 720p. The L-03C sports a 3-inch TFT LCD with a smartphone-like 800x480 pixel format. Available in January 2011 on NTT DoCoMo. I can't help wonder if next-generation camera phones will sport interchangeable lenses.
