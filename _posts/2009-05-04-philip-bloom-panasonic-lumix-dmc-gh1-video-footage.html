Philip Bloom just uploaded an almost 4 minute long footage taken by a Panasonic Lumix DMC-GH1 (or just GH1) of the ReFrame collective shoot out that was held in Austin, Texas. Bloom states that the video that you see was taken straight out of the GH1 'ungraded'. This was the first time Bloom used the GH1 and promises that, "Much better stuff will come." I can't wait.

The image out of the camera is utterly terrific, so clean and noise free. Also very sharp. It's a dream to use operational wise and the auto functions make it even beter.


That's Philips Bloom's conclusion of the GH1. Read the entire review on his blog.
