The MultiTouch Cell Advanced is a 46-inch LCD billed as "the first multitouch display designed for retail and digital signage applications." The Cell Advanced sports a depth of 12 inches and weighs 82 pounds with standard VESA mounts. MultiTouch demonstrated the Cell 46 Advanced at IBC 2010 in Amsterdam. The chassis is made of aluminum and requires zero maintenance. The Cell Advanced is stackable up to 14 units, features a 1920x1080 pixel format, a direct-lit LED backlight and is expected to last more than 60,000 hours. Source: MultiTouch
