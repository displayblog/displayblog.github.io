Acer Aspire One 751 Specifications

Official pictures for Acer's Aspire One 751 are out. Well at least two. These pictures reveal a very well designed netbook--Acer's largest with a 11.6-inch LCD featuring a 1366 x 768 pixel format. I like the thin bezels surrounding the keyboard.

The LCD looks like it is exhibiting a lot more glare than the picture in the original post. But glare is a fact of life for most notebook PCs these days except for some high-end models. Isn't it interesting how this flip-flopped? Just a few years ago, glare was an option you had to pay for!
