Sony KDL-46W4100 Specifications

Display: 46" PVA TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Contrast Ratio: 3000:1 (static), 30,000:1 (dynamic)
Response Time: 8ms
Viewing Angle: 178/178
Frequency: 120Hz
Connectivity: HDMI (4), S-Video, Component (2), Composite (3), VGA, RS-232C
Tuner: ATSC, NTSC, QAM
Dimensions:
44.1 x 30.1 x 12.1" (w/stand)
44.1 x 29.3 x 4.6" (w/o stand)
Weight: 67lbs (w/ stand), 58lbs (w/o stand)

Sony's KDL-46W4100 is a 46" LCD TV that sports a 1920 x 1080 pixel format. The 1080p-capable 46" from Sony features a very good 3000:1 static contrast ratio, 8ms response time, 178/178 viewing angles and for those of you who watch a HD with a lot of fast motion: 120Hz. The doubled frequency significantly reduces motion blur. The 8ms response time is on the slow side, but overall motion blur shouldn't be a problem on the KDL-46W4100. Just make sure you have a good HD source. The TFT LCD is of the PVA (Patterned Vertical Alignment) variety that is manufactured by S-LCD, a joint venture between Samsung and Sony. PVA LCDs are very good in general but since contrast falls off (quite a bit) as you move toward the sides, make sure to grab the seat in the middle! I like the 4 HDMI inputs since you never know how many boxes you'll need to connect to your TV in the future. Right now, Best Buy is selling the KDL-46W4100 for just $1399.99, that's $1000 off the MSRP of $2399!
