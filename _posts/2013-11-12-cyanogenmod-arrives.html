The Verge: I wish I was, but I'm not a hacker, so to me installing CyanogenMod (CM) is a hassle. Not anymore: CM released a one-click installer and is available on Google Play. You need to connect the Android device to a Windows PC with a USB cable and run a companion PC app, but that's about it. Why CM? To get as much of the NSA off your back. There are more than 8 million CM users and I'm guessing that number will explode soon.
