The Edge, an ereader and tablet hybrid, from Entourage has two displays: one on the left and the other on the right. The unique feature about the Edge is that the left display is of an E Ink-type variety and the right display seems to be a LCD. The left is touch-enabled and seems to update quite quickly while drawing and writing with a stylus. The LCD part looks like it might be sporting Google's Android but I'm not sure.
