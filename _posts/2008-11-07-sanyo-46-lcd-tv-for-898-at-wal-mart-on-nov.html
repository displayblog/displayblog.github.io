Wal-Mart will be holding a special in-store sale on Saturday, November 8, 2008. The sale will begin at 8AM and will include a Sanyo 46" LCD TV for just $898. There will be a minimum of 4 per store. So I would recommend getting in line at your nearby Wal-Mart store if you're interested in getting any one of these great deals!
