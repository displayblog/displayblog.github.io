NEC MultiSync EA261WM Specifications
Display: 26" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1200
Connectivity: USB 2.0 (4), DVI-D, HDMI
Brightness: 400 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
Response Time: 5ms
Color Gamut: 92% NTSC
Other: Height, Tilt, Swivel, Pivot, EPEAT Gold
Speakers: Yes with headphone jack
Warranty: 3 year parts, labor, backlight
Availability: August 2008
Price: $679.99

NEC showcased its MultiSync EA261WM 26" LCD monitor at InfoComm 2008. The large 26" LCD monitor sports a pixel format of 1920 x 1200 and is very bright with a luminance of 400 cd/m2. The native contrast ratio of 1000:1 is extremely high (not dynamic) and the color gamut is 92% NTSC via a wide color gamut CCFL (WCG-CCFL). Companies and individuals will be glad to know that this 26" LCD monitor sips rather than gulps electricity as it is EPEAT Gold rated. The price is also quite aggressive at just $679.99 and that includes 3 years of warranty for virtually everything including the backlight.

Source: Akihabara News
