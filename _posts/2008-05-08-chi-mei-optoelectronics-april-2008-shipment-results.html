On May 7, 2008, Chi Mei Optoelectronics (CMO) announced April 2008 shipment results. CMO's results were much better than AUO's. TFT LCD related revenues increased 0.3% M/M and 90.43% Y/Y to NT$33.5 billion. Large-area shipments increased 4.9% M/M and 84.19% Y/Y to 6.26 million units. Small/medium LCD panel shipments were also up 8.75% M/M to 9.116 million units.

Source: CMO
