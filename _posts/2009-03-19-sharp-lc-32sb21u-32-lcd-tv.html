Sharp LC-32SB21U Specifications

Display: 31.5" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1366 x 768
Contrast Ratio: 1500:1 (static), 7500:1 (dynamic)
Brightness: 450 cd/m2
Viewing Angles: 176/176
Response Time: 6ms (GTG)
Tuners: NTSC, ATSC, QAM
Connectivity: HDMI (2), S-Video, Component (2), Composite (2), VGA
Power Consumption: 135W (operational)
Dimensions:
30.6 x 22.6 x 9.1" (w/stand)
30.6 x 20.6 x 3.7" (w/o stand)
Weight: 29.8lbs (w/stand), 25.4lbs (w/o stand)

Sharp's LC-32SB21U is a 32" LCD TV that sports a 1366 x 768 pixel format. The contrast ratio is a decent 1500:1 and the brightness is average at 450 cd/m2. The 6ms GTG response time is pretty good and the viewing angles are good at 176/176. Don't pay too much attention to the 7500:1 dynamic contrast ratio as it is based on global dimming (not local dimming) that modulates the brightness of the entire CCFL backlight depending on the video content. There are just two HDMI connections so keep that in mind especially if you have a lot of devices that you would like to connect to the LC-32SB21U. Prices for this 32" LCD TV can be found for less than US$500 so it is quite the bargain if you're not looking for top specifications.
