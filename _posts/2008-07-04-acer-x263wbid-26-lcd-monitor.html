Acer X263Wbid Specifications

Display: 26" (25.5" actual) Wide TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1200
Brightness: 350 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio: 3000:1
Response Time: 5ms
Viewing Angles: 170/170
Connectivity: DVI, HDMI, VGA
Price: EUR390

Acer's X263Wbid is a 26" wide LCD monitor (actual diagonal size is 25.5"). The EUR390 price seems a bit high, but we are talking about a fairly large monitor. The 1920 x 1200 is the same pixel format you find in smaller 24" LCD monitors so the fonts and icons will be larger right out of the box, something that folks with less than perfect vision will appreciate. The contrast ratio of 3000:1 should yield some good contrasty pictures. Brightness is a standard 350 cd/m2 while the viewing angles of 170/170 seem to suggest that this might be a TN panel. Not sure though. If it wasn't a TN panel, you'd be looking at a more expensive price.

Source: Akihabara News
