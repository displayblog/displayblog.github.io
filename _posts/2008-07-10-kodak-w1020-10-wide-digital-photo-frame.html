Kodak W1020 Specifications

Display: 10" Wide TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1024 x 600 (?)
Connectivity: WiFi
Storage: 512MB Internal, Memory Card Slot (2, SD), USB
Services: Flickr, FrameChannel, Kodak Gallery
Availability: August 2008
Price: US$280

Kodak's W1020 is a 10" digital photo frame that is not only wireless but touch capable. The LCD isn't touch capable, the border is and it's called Quick Touch Border. The Quick Touch Border illuminates a panel of yellow lights on the frame border to tell you where to touch. slide your finger across the bottom to scroll through your pictures. Pretty cool and it keeps your display clean and free of fingerprints.

The W1020 works with Kodak Gallery (given), Flickr and FrameChannel. The other two are self-explanatory, but FrameChannel is special. You get news, weather, sports, traffic and other news that get updated. Nice.
