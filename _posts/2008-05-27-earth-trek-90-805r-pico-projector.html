Earth Trek 90-805R
Projected Image Size: 22"
External Storage: SD
Battery: Lithium Ion, 1-2 hours
Dimension: 105 x 58 x 25mm (4.13 x 2.28 x 0.98")
Weight: 160g

Carry a 22" screen in your pocket. And your pocket doesn't have to be giant size. Earth Trek introduced its pico projector called 90-805R. It is small. Very small at just 105 x 58 x 25mm or 4.13 x 2.28 x 0.98". It's light too at just 160g.

The tiny 90-805R can project an image that's 22" in diameter and that's plenty big for a little impromptu Keynote presentation. Information about this pico projector is limited but I'm hoping the price will be right so all those travelers (those few remaining that is) can lighten their carry-ons.

Source: Engadget, Akihabara News
