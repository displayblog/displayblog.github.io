Akihabara News: The Sharp Galapagos 003SH and 005SH are Android smartphones featuring a 3.8-inch 800x480 Advanced Super View (ASV) TFT LCD with 3D Glassless technology.

The 003SH sports a 9.6 megapixel CCD image sensor, a 1GHz Snapdragon CPU, 400MB of RAM, WiFi, GPS and the ability to record 720p HD videos. The difference on the 005SH are a slide-out QWERTY keyboard, a slightly less packed 8 megapixel CCD, and a bit more RAM with 512MB.

3D displays without 3D content would be moot, so Sharp will include seven 3D movies and seven 3D games.
