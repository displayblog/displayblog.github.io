Sony has outed its new VAIO P. Five colors and a host of added features including an accelerometer, two touchpads, 3G, GPS, and compass. The 8-inch VAIO P packing a 1600x768 TFT LCD runs Windows 7 and is powered by Intel's Atom Z540 with GMA 500 graphics. Storage is via a 64GB SSD and comes with 2GB of RAM. Battery life is a claimed five hours. Available in June for a price that has not been determined. Source: Sony
