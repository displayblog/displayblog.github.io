Trusted Reviews took Toshiba's REGZA 32CV505DB LCD TV and, well, reviewed it. Despite other companies coming out with 32" LCD TVs that sport 1920 x 1080 pixel formats, Toshiba's REGZA 32CV505DB has just 1366 x 768. But what a bargain the 32CV505DB is at less than L500! The external design of the 32CV505DB is nothing fancy but simple and stylish, just the way I like it.

Like in most other Toshiba LCD TVs, the 32CV505DB features a Game Mode that enables a direct connection from it to a game console and reducing the lag that some gamers might experience. Game Mode also puts you in 1:1 pixel mapping mode so no additional blur is introduced, just make sure to set your PS3 or Xbox 360 to 720p mode. TR mentions a trace of motion blur, more than units that feature 100Hz, but overall the 32CV505DB gets a score of 8/10. You can't expect to have everything for a mere L500.
