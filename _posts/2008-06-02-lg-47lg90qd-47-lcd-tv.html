LG 47LG90QD
Size: 47"
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Backlight: LED
Contrast Ratio: 1,000,000:1 (dynamic)
Refresh Rate: 120Hz
Input: HDMI (4), etc.
Sound: Mark Levinson tuned
Price: KRW4,000,000 (US$3,905)

LG launched its 47LG90QD 47" LCD TV aka Xcanvas David LED in the South Korean market. The 47LG90QD sports an Aurora LED backlight that reduces the power consumption of the big bad boy by up to 70% and at the same time improves the contrast ratio. I'll take a guess and state that LG is using LED local dimming to get to 1,000,000:1 in contrast ratio (most likely dynamic). That's a huge number! And with huge numbers come a huge price!
