Packard Bell EasyNote Butterfly Specifications

Display: 13.4" TFT LCD with LED Backlight
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1366 x 768
GPU: ATI Mobility Radeon HD4330
CPU: Intel ULV
RAM: DDR3
HDD: SSD or HDD
Connectivity: WiFi, Bluetooth, 3G (optional), HDMI
Other: Multitouch Trackpad
Battery: 8 hours
Dimensions: 1" (2.54cm) thick
Price: TBD

Packard Bell's EasyNote Butterfly looks quite pleasing. The Butterfly sports a 13.4" TFT LCD with a 16:9 aspect ratio good for 1366 x 768 pixels. You'll find a similar LCD in the Adamo. The graphics is powered by ATI's Mobility Radeon HD4330 and the main duties will be an as-yet unidentified Intel ULV CPU. There is an option for SSD and the trackpad is multitouch. The Butterfly allows you to be out and about for up to 8 hours and the chassis is just 1" thick. Not bad for a brand that's been long absent in these neck of the woods (US).
