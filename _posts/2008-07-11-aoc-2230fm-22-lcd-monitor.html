AOC 2230Fm Specifications

Display: 22" Wide TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1680 x 1050
Contrast Ratio: 20,000:1 (Dynamic)
Response Time: 2ms
Connectivity: USB 2.0, DVI with HDCP, HDMI with HDCP, VGA
Price: US$399.99

AOC bills the 2230Fm as a 22" LCD monitor, HDTV and digital picture frame all rolled into one. Certainly the external design is simple and elegant. It has a black piano finish; it has to be elegant. ;-)

The feature that makes this 22" LCD monitor different from others is the built-in multi-format media card reader that takes in USB flash drives, SD, MMC, MS and xD memory cards. No luck with CF. Big is out, except for when it comes to display size.

The 2230Fm has a lot of input connections too as you can see from the picture above: HDMI, DVI, and VGA. But it falls short on being a HDTV since it doesn't have a tuner built-in. What AOC is trying to convince us is that all we have to do is put all of our video stuff unto memory cards and view them that way: PC-less and DVD player-less.

The 22" is most likely the TN LCD variety. You can tell by the fast response time of just 2ms. The dynamic contrast ratio of 20,000:1 is a great fit for what AOC intends the 2230Fm to be: a multimedia display. Moving pictures should look pretty good, though not as good as real LCD TVs with such technologies as 120Hz and LED backlights.
