LG W2442PA-BF Specifications

Display: 24" TN TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Response Time: 2ms (GTG)
Contrast Ratio: 1000:1 (static), 10,000:1 (dynamic)
Brightness: 300 cd/m2
Viewing Angles: 170/160
Color Gamut: 72% NTSC
Backlight: 4 CCFL
Connectivity: HDMI, DVI-D w/HDCP, VGA
Adjustability: Pivot, Rotate, Tilt
Dimensions: 582.2 x 437.5 x 270.2mm
Weight: 6.2kg

LG's W2442PA-BF is a 24" LCD monitor with an aspect ratio of 16:9. That means that it sports a 1920 x 1080 pixel format, standard for 1080p LCD TVs. LCD monitors are shifting toward 16:9. I guess most brands think that PC users want to watch 16:9 1080p content on their monitors. And they are right. The W2442PA-BF has a HDMI port and a DVI-D port so you can connect your PC via DVI as well as a gaming console like a PlayStation 3 via HDMI. Viewing angles are limited to 170/160 but that shouldn't be a concern since most likely you'll be the only one watching video on your LG monitor. One thing that I have noticed with TN TFT LCDs when pivoted is that colors are strange. If you are sensitive to color you might want to go with a monitor that sports either a PVA (Patterned Vertical Alignment) or IPS (In-Plane Switching) technology. The GTG (gray-to-gray) response time of 2ms is very fast and you'll enjoy the quick responses with PS3 games on this 24" 1080p LCD monitor.

Source: LG Japan
