Nanovision Mimo Monitor Specifications

Display: 7" TFT LCD (UM-710) with Touch (UM-750)
Pixel Format: 800 x 480 (landscape)
Brightness: 350 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio: 400:1
Webcam: 1.3MP
Connectivity: USB 2.0, DMB (UM-750)
Other: Mic
Dimensions: 197 x 97.5 x 180.7 - 210.4mm
Weight: 700g

Nanovision is a South Korean manufacturer and has come out with some really nifty monitors. These Mimo monitors are just 7" in size (diagonal) and uses a simple USB 2.0 connection. The Mimo monitor can be rotated to be viewed in portrait mode (great for IM sessions).

The UM-710 is the base version that goes for just $78 (probably a little higher when the KRW bumps back up a bit later on). The enhanced UM-750 adds touch capability, webcam and a DMB TV tuner for just $147 (again, probably will end up being higher soon due to fluctuating exchange rates).
