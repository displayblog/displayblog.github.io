LG W2452T-TF Specifications

Display: 24" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1200
Viewing Angle: 170/170
Brightness: 400 cd/m2
Response Time: 2ms (GTG)
Connectivity: VGA, DVI
Power Consumption: 80W (operational)
Dimensions: 22.6 x 16.0 x 9.6"
Weight: 13.5lbs
Price: US$369.99

LG's W2452T-TF is a 24" LCD monitor with a pixel format of 1920 x 1200. It has a very fast response time of just 2ms gray-to-gray (GTG) and a very bright panel at 400 cd/m2. Viewing angle is a bit limited but that is to be expected of a TN LCD panel.

Here are some reviews:

PCWorld: 76 (Good). Pros: Good image quality. Display looks good. Cons: Poorly designed menu. Minimal adjustments.

PC Advisor: 3/5 stars. "Considering the price, the LG W2452T doesn't make the grade: you can find competing wide-screen displays that have more features and yet cost substantially less."

Chip Online.de: 83.4%
