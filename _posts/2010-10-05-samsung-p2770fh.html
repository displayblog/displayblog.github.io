Engadget: The Samsung P2770FH is a 27-inch LCD monitor sporting a response time of 1ms, brightness of 300 cd/m2, HDMI connection, and a 1000:1 contrast ratio. The spec that kills it is a lowly 1920x1080 pixel format. Shouldn't a 27-inch monitor be packing in 2560x1440? Priced at US$400.
