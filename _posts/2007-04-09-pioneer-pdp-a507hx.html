Size: 50"

Pixel Format: 1365 x 768
Input: HDMI (2), VGA

Dimension: 1224 x 795 x 115mm

Pioneer's PDP-A507HX 50" plasma TV incorporates a new surface that reduces glare, which has been a problem for some consumers. In addition, Pioneer's PURE Black Panel enhances colors, brightness and especially contrast. The PURE Black Panel has developed into today's KURO, where black levels are so black the company states that the contrast ratio is virtually unlimited.
