Samsung A877 Specifications

Pixel Format: 480 x 320
Webcam: 3MP with Geotagging
Connectivity: Telenav GPS, Dualband HSDPA (850, 1900), Quadband EDGE/GSM, Bluetooth

Samsung's A877 sports a touch-enabled 3.2" AMOLED with a 480 x 320 pixel format. The A877 has a 3MP webcam with geotagging capability. As you can see from the picture above the A877 has a slide-out QWERTY keyboard that looks pretty solid and large enough for texting. There is a rumor out there that this AMOLED-touting smartphone will be available later this month.
