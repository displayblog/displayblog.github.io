The Verge: The Samsung Galaxy Note Edge sports a 5.6-inch OLED display with 2560x1440, but it curves over the right edge of the smartphone. The curved area can be used as an app launcher, a toolbar, show in-app settings or notifications. Gimmicky.

The Galaxy Note Edge is much like the new Galaxy Note 4 except for the curved display, with an aluminum frame, and included stylus.
