via Engadget: The Panasonic Toughbook Android tablet sports a matte 10.1-inch 1024x768 multitouch LCD. The display sports high brightness and daylight viewability. Multitouch supports an active stylus to capture signatures at point-of-sale environments.

The market for rugged tablets is huge: think kids. I'm waiting for something like the Panasonic Toughbook that's rugged, but more kid-friendly like an iPad. Waterproof would be good, too, since kids tend to spill all sorts of stuff on expensive things.
