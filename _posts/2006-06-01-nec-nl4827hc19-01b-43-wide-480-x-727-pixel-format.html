NEC will launch its NL4827HC19-01B in June. The 4.3" TFT LCD panel is wide and has a pixel format of 480 x 272. A bit on the low side, but enough to be a drop-in replacement for Sony's PSP. Sony's PSP maxes out at 200 cd/m2 brightness, but this panel has 300.The target of the 4.3" display is for PDAs, photo viewers, GPS...and probably portable gaming devices. It has a touch panel too. Maybe the PSP will have touch, soon. Here are the specs:

4.3" TFT LCD with 480 x 272 pixel format
262,000 colors
350 cd/m2 brightness
300:1 contrast ratio
50/50/70/40 LRUD viewing angles
23ms response time (rise+fall)

Source: Noticias, NEC
