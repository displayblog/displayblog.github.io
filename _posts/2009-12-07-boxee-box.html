Boxee Box, the hardware version of Boxee (now in beta), will be manufactured by D-Link. Unfortunate, IMO. The semi-cube-like Boxee Box features HDMI, SD, USB (two), WiFi, Audio Out and Ethernet. Available in Q2'10 for about US$200. Source: Boxee (Livestream), D-Link via Engadget
