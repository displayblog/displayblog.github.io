Lenovo ThinkPad SL Series Specifications
Display Size: 13.3", 14.1", 15.4"
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1280 x 800 (standard for all three sizes), 1680 x 1050 (option for SL500)
CPU: Intel Core 2 Duo
GPU: Integrated Intel GMA 4500MHD or Discrete NVIDIA GeForce 9300
Connectivity: ExpressCard, FireWire, USB 2.0 (3), WWAN, WiMAX, WiFi, Bluetooth, GPS
Optical: Blu-ray (option)

Lenovo's ThinkPad SL series come in three sizes, but I'm most interested in the 13.3" version. The pixel format of 1280 x 800 is pretty much standard; I would have preferred the 1440 x 900 from the X300. How fast the Core 2 Duo CPUs will be running is unknown as of yet. I would guess that battery life would be compromised, but most would prefer a discrete NVIDIA GeForce 9300 GPU. The WiMAX and WWAN connectivity would boost your ability to connect from anywhere, for a price. A Blu-ray option on the 13.3" ThinkPad SL with just 1280 x 800 pixels? Doesn't make sense. And it doesn't make sense even for the 1680 x 1050 option on the 15.4". There is no mention of a LED backlight. From the looks of it, the SL series will be the bulkier, uglier, fatter, but more powerful version of the X300.
