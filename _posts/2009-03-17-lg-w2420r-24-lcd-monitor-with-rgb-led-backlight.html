LG W2420R Specifications

  • Display: 24" S-IPS TFT LCD with RGB LED Backlight
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:10
  • Pixel Format: 1920x1200
  • Color Gamut: 135% NTSC
  • Response Time: 6ms (GTG)
  • Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
  • Brightness: 250 cd/m2
  • Viewing Angles: 178/178
  • Other: LGE Calibrator (software & hardware color calibration tool), Light Protection Hood, 12-bit Lookup Table

TFT Central: LG has introduced its W2420R, a 24-inch LCD monitor geared for professionals that require extreme levels of color fidelity. The 24-inch LCD panel is of the S-IPS (Super In-Plane Switching) variety that feature very good viewing angles, a color gamut of 135% NTSC and a 12-bit lookup table. Not only that, there is very little color shift at different viewing angles on IPS LCD panels. The 6ms GTG is average as is the 250 cd/m2 brightness.

The best part about the W2420R is the LED backlight: it's not the white LED variety; it's the RGB LED kind. That's how you get such a high color gamut. The body itself doesn't look all that special except for maybe the polished base. I do like simple and the W2420R is quite that. The high-end 24-incher from LG comes with a software and hardware color calibration tool to make sure that colors are exactly as they should be.
