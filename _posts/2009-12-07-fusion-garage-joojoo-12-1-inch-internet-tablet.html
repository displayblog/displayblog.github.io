Fusion Garage unveiled its JooJoo, a 2.4-pound 12-inch Internet tablet sporting capacitive touch, not a single physical button (except for the on/off switch), 9-second boot into an UNIX-based OS browser, 4GB SSD, WiFi, and a 5-hour battery. Price is US$499 and pre-orders start December 11. BTW, terrible name. Source: The JooJoo via Engadget
