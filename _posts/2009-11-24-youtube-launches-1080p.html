Back on November 16, 2009 Home Theater Review reported that Lifehacker.com expected YouTube to launch 1080p video service soon. Well, YouTube did. When you hit the 'HD' button on some videos you have two choices: 1080p or 720p. Note: You can view 1080p content from YouTube without having a 1080p capable display. Source: Home Theater Review
