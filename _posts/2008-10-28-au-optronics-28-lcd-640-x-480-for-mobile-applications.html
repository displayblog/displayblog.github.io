This one is interesting. AU Optronics (AUO) showcased a 2.8" TFT LCD with a pixel format of 640 x 480. That in and of itself is pretty good stuff but nothing extra ordinary. But this little LCD can sense ultra-violet rays. So if you're getting exposed to too much harmful UV rays, the 2.8" LCD will let you know. AUO is showcased the UV-sensing 2.8" LCD at FPD International along with a few other notables:

  • A 2.2" TFT LCD for mobile applications that has almost no bordor around the LCD.
  • A 1.9" TFT LCD for mobile applications that is just 0.56mm thick.
  • A 8.9" flexible display.
  • A 2.8" 320 x 240 TFT LCD for mobile applications that has image & fingerprint scanning technology.
