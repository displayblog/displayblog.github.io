Sky News: Lenses made of light-sensitive silicone is used to less implants that provide patients with "HD vision" that is superior to 20/20. The silicone-based lens can be fine tuned many times post surgery to give HD vision capability and adjusted for bifocal and varifocal use. There is a video at source links.
