Akihabara News: From the video the LG-LU3000 also known as Optimus Mach seems extremely fast compared to the Samsung Galaxy S and iPhone 4.

The Optimus Mach from LG sports a 3.8-inch 800x480 IPS LCD and runs Android 2.2. Other specs include: a 5 megapixel AF camera, HDMI out capable of 1080p, 1GHz Texas Instrument's OMAP 3630 CPU.
