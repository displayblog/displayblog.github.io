ASUS Lamborghini VX5 Specifications

Display: 16" TFT LCD
GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GT 130M with 1GB VRAM
CPU: Intel Core 2 Quad
RAM: 4GB
HDD: 1TB SSD
Optical: Blu-ray

The ASUS Lamborghini VX5 is supposedly inspired by the Lamborghini Reventon. I fail to see a direct resemblance but I suppose the few angular lines can be counted as similar. The Lamborghini Reventon is a work of art. Just look at the sculpted angular lines (after the break) that come together into a singular muscular but lean and mean alphalt-burning machine. The ASUS? Well, not quite. But it doesn't look bad.

The 16" notebook PC sports what is the world's largest SSD at 1TB! That is massive! The GeForce GT 130M that powers the 16" LCD comes with 1GB of VRAM and there is a "TwinTurbo" mode that utilizes both the CPU and GPU to improve system performance. Not much else is known about the technical specifications but the press release mentions that the 16" is a "full high definition" display so that could possibly mean that it sports a 1920 x 1080 pixel format. Or a 1920 x 1200.
