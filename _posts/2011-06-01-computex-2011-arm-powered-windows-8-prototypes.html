Engadget: Foxconn, Quanta, and Wistron unveiled Windows 8 prototypes at Computex 2011 powered by chips from Qualcomm, Texas Instruments, and NVIDIA. Although Microsoft is going about it the wrong way by forcing brands to initially work with a single chip supplier, I think Windows 8 will be on everything: netbooks, smartbooks, ultrabooks, notebooks, tablets, desktops, etc.
