DP-SLR is an external display for a DSLR. Just the thing for those who shoot video with DSLRs. The 5.6-inch display sports a pixel format of 1280x800 giving it a superb 270 PPI resolution. Dimensions are 4x6x1 inch and it weighs just 10 ounces. Connectivity options include HDMI, component, composite, and optional 3G/HD/SDI. Pricing isn't too exorbitant with the standard model going for US$899. The version with SG/HD/SDI connectivity goes for $1199. You can pre-order now and you'll get yours within 75 days.
