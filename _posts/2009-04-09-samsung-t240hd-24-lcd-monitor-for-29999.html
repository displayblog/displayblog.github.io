Are you looking to get a LCD TV and want to spend the least amount of money but still want that nice 1080p capability? Well look no further. Samsung's T240HD is a 24" LCD monitor... um... TV. It also sports the nice (I think) Touch of Color design theme.

So what's the price? PC Connection has it for just $299.99 with free shipping. A great deal on a multi-purpose display!
