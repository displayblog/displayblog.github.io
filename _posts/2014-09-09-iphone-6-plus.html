Plus. What's next? Super? Anyway, the 5.5-inch iPhone 6 Plus has a pixel format of 1920x1080. Finally, an industry standard pixel format. The resolution is 401 pixels per inch.

Still the iPhone 6 Plus is quite a ways behind when it comes to the display. The LG G3 and the Samsung Galaxy Note 4 both sport 2560x1440 pixel formats, and their resolutions — 534 ppi and 515 ppi respectively — are significantly higher too.
