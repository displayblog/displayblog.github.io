Kmart is selling the Augen GenBook 108, a 10.2-inch netbook, for just $190. The LCD sports a 1024x600 pixel format. Other specs include: 800MHz ARM11 CPU, 256MB DDR RAM, WiFi BG, SD/MMC, 1.8 lbs, and a keyboard that's 93% full-sized. And it runs Android, without Android Market. I think this will be great for those wanting to buy toddlers a computer to play with.
