Olevia 242T Specifications

Display: 42" TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1366 x 768
Response Time: 8ms
Contrast Ratio: 1600:1 (dynamic)
Tuner: NTSC, ATSC
Speakers: Yes
Connectivity: HDMI with HDCP (2), S-Video (2), Component (3), RF, Composite (2), VGA
Power Consumption: 220W
Dimensions: 42 x 34.7 x 13.8" (with stand)

Olevia's 242T is a 42" LCD TV that has two things going for it: a ton of connections and a rock-bottom price. First connections: 2 HDMI with HDCP, 2 S-Video, 3 Component, 2 Composite and 1 VGA. That's quite a bit. And then there's the price: $799. Sure you don't get 1080p capability, an out-of-this-world dynamic contrast ratio, or super fast response times, but come on, what do you expect when all you're asked to pay is $799 for a 42" LCD TV?
