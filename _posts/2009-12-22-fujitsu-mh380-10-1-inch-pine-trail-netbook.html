Fujitsu's MH380 is a 10.1-inch netbook with Intel's new Pine Rail Atom CPU running at 1.6GHz. The 10.1-inch LCD sports a 1366 x 768 pixel format with an LED backlight. Other specs include: 1.3MP webcam, Fast Ethernet, WiFi BGN, Bluetooth, 250GB 5400RPM SATA HDD, 2GB RAM, 6-cell battery, 1.3kg. The MH380 comes in a variety of colors: Ivory White, Gorgeous Brown (above), Black Diamond. The spherical indentation next to the trackpad is used to scroll by twirling your fingers in a circular pattern. I think that's unnecessarily complex compared to a single multitouch trackpad with dual finger scrolling.
