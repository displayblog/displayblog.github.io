Akihabara News: Look at how thin the bezel is on the Acer Travelmate 8481. The 1366x768 14.1-inch LCD is LG Display's Super Thin Bezel Display. Looking forward to LG Display's thin bezel technology showing up on a lot more products.

Update: After closer inspection of the photo, I realized the Travelmate 8481 has a cover glass that goes to the edge of the display chassis. The actual 14.1-inch Shuriken Display is behind the cover glass and adds a bit to the overall bezel. When compared to the design of a MacBook Pro, I'm not sure that the Shuriken Display saves that much in terms of bezel width.
