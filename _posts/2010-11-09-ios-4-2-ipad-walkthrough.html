In 10 minutes you get a brief overview of the new features of iOS 4.2 for the iPad: multitasking, widget control (orientation lock, brightness slider, multimedia controls, AirPlay button, volume slider), folders, Game Center, AirPlay, AirPrint, find text on Safari page, settings (new wallpapers, restrictions, Notes fonts), and mail (threaded, universal inbox). The video is based on the Golden Master (GM) with the official version expected to be announced any day now.
