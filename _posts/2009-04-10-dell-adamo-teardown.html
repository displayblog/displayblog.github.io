Dell Adamo Specifications

iFixit and TechRepublic partnered to tear down a Dell Adamo. It looks like the folks at Quanta Computers were responsible for putting the Dell Adamo together. Quanta Computers, the largest integrator for notebook PCs, builds for Apple as well.

The display is off and has a lot of antenna wires hanging off of it. The pixel format is 1366 x 768, which is a 16:9 aspect ratio. The maximum power consumption of the LED backlit 13" display is just 3.6W. Green.

I wonder who the LCD supplier is...
