Apple has the iPhone, which is probably the most popular smartphone on the planet for many good reasons: exceptionally well designed, a ton of apps, the best multitouch experience, etc. Motorola now has the Droid, a brute, unpolished technology tour de force, at least in the US. HTC has a whole bunch of smartphones sporting a combination of sexy and high-tech. Even Palm has the Pre that's quite unique. But what does Nokia have? Does Nokia have anything special? The company might be the biggest but there is nothing special about its phones. Not anymore.
