Acer's K11 looks downright sexy. The K11 is a DLP pico projector that beams 858x600 pixels with a brightness of 200 lumens. Expect the K11 to keep projecting those high-contrast (2000:1) pixels for 20,000 hours. Connectivity options include HDMI, VGA, and composite. You can use a SDHC card or the USB connection to playback music, video and photos. The K11 is just 1.34 pounds. Source: Engadget
