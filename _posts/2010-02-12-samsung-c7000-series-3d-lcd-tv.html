The C7000 Series 3D LCD TV from Samsung sports an edge-lit LED backlight, a 1.1-inch profile and comes in three sizes: 40-, 46-, and 55-inch. There are many features: 1920x1080 pixel format, 240Hz Clear Motion Technology, 2D-to-3D conversion, Samsung Apps, Internet@TV, Skype, AllShare DLNA media streaming, etc. The C7000 Series is compatible with the newly ratified 3D Blu-ray standard. And the prices aren't astronomical:

  • UN40C7000: 40-inch, US$1999, Feb. 26, 2010
  • UN46C7000: 46-inch, $2599, Apr. 16, 2010
  • UN55C7000: 55-inch, $3299, Feb. 26, 2010


Of course, you'll need to purchase a pair (or more) of neat active-shutter 3D glasses. If you are pre-ordering, consider supporting DisplayBlog by using this link to Amazon. Source: Vanns via HDTVReview, I4U, Engadget
