Slash Gear, UMPC Portal, I4U News, Liliputing: Medion Akyoa Mini E1210. MSI Wind U100. The same thing? Well, yes. The E1210 has got the same display, the same specifications and looks the same, except for the color: the E1210 comes in black or silver. The MSI Wind U100 comes in just white, as far as I know. The E1210 will be available via Aldi starting July 3.
