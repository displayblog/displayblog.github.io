Envision G918W1
Size: 19"
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1440 x 900
Contrast Ratio: 800:1
Brightness: 300 cd/m2
Response Time: 5ms
Input: DVI-D, VGA

Envision's G918W1 19" wide LCD monitor is rock-bottom priced display. The very good contrast ratio of 800:1 should give pictures some ooomph while the pixel format of 1440 x 900 should allow for easy viewing of websites, Office documents as well as the occasional DVD. Make sure to connect via the DVI port for best pictures, though VGA should be good enough for most applications. The best part about the G918W1 is the price. But. You will need to make sure that you don't get one with dead pixels, whether on or off.
