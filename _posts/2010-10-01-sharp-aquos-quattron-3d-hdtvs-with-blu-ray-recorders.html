Sharp's 52-inch LC-52LB3 and 46-inch LC-46LB3 are the latest in the AQUOS Quattron 3D line of HDTVs and now include Blu-ray recorders. The ASV LCD panels sport a 1920x1080 pixel format, 176-degree viewing angles, and a LED backlight.
