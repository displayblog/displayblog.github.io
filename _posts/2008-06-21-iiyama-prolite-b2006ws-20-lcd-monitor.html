Iiyama Prolite B2006WS Specifications
Display: 20" Wide TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:10
Pixel Format: 1680 x 1050
Contrast Ratio: 2000:1 (dynamic)
Brightness: 300 cd/m2
Response Time: 5ms
Connectivity: HDMI (1.1), DVI-D
Color: Black or White
Price: Euro 225

On June 20, 2008, Iiyama released its ProLite B2006WS 20" LCD monitor. It is a wide 16:10 LCD monitor with a pixel format of 1680 x 1050. The dynamic contrast ratio is 2000:1, which should provide very good contrast for moving images while the fairly standard 300 cd/m2 will be bright enough for almost all types of applications. For gaming, 5ms response times might not be good enough while HD buffs will be surprised to see a HDMI 1.1 connection instead of the new standard: HDMI 1.3

Source: Akihabara News
