Samsung Impression Specifications

Display: 3.2" Touch AMOLED
Pixel Format: 400 x 240 (landscape)
Camera: 3MP
Memory: 189MB
Storage: microSD
Network: GSM, GPRS, EDGE, HSDPA, UMTS
Connectivity: Bluetooth, GPS
Battery: 1000 mAh Lithium Ion
Dimensions: 4.48 x 2.28 x 0.61"
Weight: 5.3oz
Availability: April 7, 2009 via AT&T in the US
Price: $200 with 2-year Agreement

Samsung's Impression is a smartphone that sports the latest in display technology: a 3.2" touch AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED) featuring a 400 x 240 pixel format in landscape mode. The Impression also has a 3MP camera. From a short Brighthand preview video it seems the proprietary OS on the Impression is quite responsive. Some video previews after the jump.
