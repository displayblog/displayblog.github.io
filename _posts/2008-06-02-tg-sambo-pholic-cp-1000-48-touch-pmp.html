TG Sambo's CP-1000 is one slick-looking portable multimedia player (PMP). TG Sambo states that its image is becoming a Korean version of Apple. I wouldn't go so far, but the CP-1000 does look nice.

The company mentions the fact that it has absolutely no physical buttons on the front face of the slim CP-1000. On the top (when in landscape) there is the power, volume "tilt key" and menu buttons. I like the fact that the CP-1000 can be used as a USB host. That means you can take a portable external drive filled with multimedia files that you can access with the CP-1000. Something I wish the iPhone or other Apple products had.
