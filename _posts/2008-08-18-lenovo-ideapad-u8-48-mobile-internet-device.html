Lenovo IdeaPad U8 Specifications

Display: 4.8" Wide TFT LCD with Touch
Pixel format:
Webcam: Dual Cameras
CPU: 800MHz Intel Silverthorn-class Atom Z500
RAM: Up to 2GB
Storage: 6GB Flash
Connectivity: China Multimedia Mobile Broadcasting (CMMB), WiFi (802.11b/g), Bluetooth, GPS, EDGE, TD-SCDMA 3G (China), WiMAX (?), USB
Battery: 5 hours
Dimensions: 173 x 84 x 21mm
Weight: 350g (10.6oz)

Lenovo's IdeaPad U8 is a mobile Internet device, or MID. The U8 has a 4.8" wide TFT LCD but the pixel format is unknown. I would hope it is at least 800 x 480, hopefully more. The USB dongle is required to receive the China Multimedia Mobile Broadcasting (CMMB) signals and that makes it a bit less elegant. But the whole idea of a 173mm (6.8") portable device is a bit, let's say, in the dead zone.

The reason why I say this is because when I was visiting my family in South Korea and showed them my shiny new iPhone last year the response was twofold: "Cool!" and "It's kinda big." The iPhone is 4.5" tall. The U8 is 2.3" more. My question to anyone who is thinking of getting the U8 is this: "How will you carry it?"

Another source of less-than-elegance is the stylus. In a world that is rapidly moving toward finger-touch, the U8 seems to take a step back. Of course, the UI will need to be finger-centric for finger-touch to work and from the picture, it looks like the U8 was designed to work with a stylus.
