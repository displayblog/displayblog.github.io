Vizio VF550XVT Specifications

Display: 54.64" 10-bit TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Response Time: 5ms
Brightness: 500 cd/m2
Contrast Ratio: 50,000:1 (dynamic)
Viewing Angle: 178/178
Frequency: 120Hz
Connectivity: RF, HDMI with HDCP (5), Component (2), Composite (2), S-Video, VGA
Tuner: ATSC, NTSC, QAM
Dimensions:
51.5 x 36.0 x 13.5" (w/stand)
51.5 x 33.9 x 5.0" (w/o stand)
Weight: 86.0lbs (w/stand), 73.8lbs (w/o stand)
Price: MSRP US$1999.99

Vizio is setting another low-price standard with its VF550XVT that will be available in January 2009. The VF550XVT is the company's 55" LCD TV that sports a pixel format of 1920 x 1080. The 1080p-capable 55" LCD TV will feature a 10-bit TFT LCD that has a color palette of more than 1 billion colors. The response time is relative fast at 5ms and coupled with a 120Hz frequency, motion blur should be reduced significantly. Viewing angles are top notch at 178/178 while the 50,000:1 dynamic contrast ratio via the company's Mega DCR should make the images really nice. Also note the VF550XVT has 5 HDMI inputs so you can connect about as many video sources as you want (as long as it is 5). All of this for an unbelievable price of just $1999.99. Just one catch: you need to wait until January 2009.

Source: Vizio
