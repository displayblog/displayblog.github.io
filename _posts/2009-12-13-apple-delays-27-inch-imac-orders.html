Terrible news for those who have ordered 27-inch iMacs from Apple. The reason for the delay is some issues with the beautiful 27-inch IPS TFT LCD sporting a grand 2560 x 1440 pixel format. The problem seem to center around the ATI Radeon HD 4670 and 4850 graphics cards that lead to flickering and yellow-tinging. Apple resellers were told delays of 2 weeks for Apple to replace these graphics cards on the 27-inch iMacs. Source: AppleInsider
