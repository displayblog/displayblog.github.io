... Chimei Innolux (CMI) is expected to start supplying iPad panels in 2011 and account for 10% of the total iPad panels in the first quarter, rising to 15% in the second, 20% in the third, and up to 30% in the fourth. CMI is expected to start iPad panel production in January 2011...
