There are two NX series models in Fujitsu's FMV-BIBLO lineup: NX95W and NX70W. Both sport a 17" TFT LCD with a pixel format of 1440 x 900 but the NX70W really isn't worth mentioning because of its lowly specs. I will focus on the NX95W. The NX95W's graphics is powered by ATI's Radeon X1600 with 256MB while Intel's 2GHz Core 2 Duo T7200 serves as the CPU. The NX95W has a large storage capacity thanks to two 200GB hard disks.
