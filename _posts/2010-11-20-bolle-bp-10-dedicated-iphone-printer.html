MobileFun → Ubergizmo → Gizmodo: You physically dock your iPhone (2G, 3G, 3GS, 4) to the Bolle BP-10 and print 4x6 prints using Bolle's iPhone app. Sure AirPrint is just around the corner but if you don't have or want WiFi then this seems like a very good option. Priced at US$192 the one and only dedicated iPhone printer (so far) is available at MobileFun.
