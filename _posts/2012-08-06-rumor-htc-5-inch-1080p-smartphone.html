DIGITIMES → Vlad Savov, The Verge:

HTC is reputedly working on cramming Full HD resolution into its next flagship smartphone, a purported 5-inch device that is expected to launch in either September or October.

That would be good for a range-topping resolution of about 441 ppi. This seems to coincide with LG Display planning for a second half release of its 5-inch 1920x080 AH-IPS LCD. I wonder what Apple's got up its sleeve.
