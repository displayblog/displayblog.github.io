Royal Pingdom:

We found only one country where Android accounts for more than 50% of the mobile web traffic, which is also the only country where Android is the leading mobile OS.

That would be South Korea.

Note that South Korea is the home country of Samsung, and their Android phones have clearly been very successful there.

And that would be the Samsung Galaxy S.
