Is brown in? Here is Acer's brown laptop. The Aspire 3935 has a golden brown metal chassis. This just doesn't feel right to me. Brown and high-tech just isn't. But on with the specs. The Aspire 3935 has a 13.3" TFT LCD with a LED backlight the company calls CineCrystal. It doesn't have much graphics horsepower thanks to the integrated Intel GMA 4500MHD GPU. But you can connect the Aspire 3935 to almost anything: WiFi AGN, WiMAX and 3G (optional). The 13.3" notebook is one inch thick and weighs a bit more than 4lbs. You get all of this for $899. But you know what? I'd rather get a refurbished non-brown aluminum MacBook 2.0GHz for $200 extra.

I just can't get over the color...
