Planar → 3D Vision Blog: The Planar SA2311W is a 23-inch 3D LCD monitor. Here are the specs: 120Hz frequency, 1920x1080 pixel format, 2ms response time, with VGA, dual-link DVI, HDMI connectivity options. NVIDIA's 3D Vision system is employed. Priced at US$449 the SA2311W will be shipping in November.
