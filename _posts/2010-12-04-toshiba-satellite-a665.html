Notebook Review:

[...] The backlight is even and bright, contrast is sharp and colors are good though the colors are a little warm at the default settings. If there is a single obvious issue it has to be the narrow vertical viewing angles. While the horizontal viewing angles (side view) are nearly flawless, the colors become over exposed when viewed from above and significantly inverted and distorted when viewed from below. [...]

Although most of us wouldn't be looking at the Toshiba Satellite A665 from below, the narrow vertical viewing angles do indicate that this 16-inch TFT LCD is not high quality. Not to mention the limited pixel format: 1366x768.
