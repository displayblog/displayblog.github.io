Engadget: The Dell Inspiron Duo will be available during the first week of December for $549. The 10-inch hybrid netbook reinvents the convertible by rotating the display itself. The LCD packs 1366x768 pixels and sports capacitive touch. The $549 price is for the base model that includes: a dual-core Intel Atom N550 CPU, 2GB RAM, Broadcom Crystal HD GPU, 250GB hard drive, and Windows 7 Home Premium.
