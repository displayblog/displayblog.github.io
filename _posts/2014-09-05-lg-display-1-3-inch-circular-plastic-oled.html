LG Display: On September 4 (South Korea) LGD announced the commencement of circular plastic OLEDs (P-OLEDs) mass production. Hardware specifications:

  • Diameter: 1.3 inches
  • Thickness: <0.6mm

  • Pixel Format: 320x320 (maximum horizontal and vertical)
  • Brightness: 300 nits (maximum)

LGD's circular P-OLED display is used in the LG G Watch R.
