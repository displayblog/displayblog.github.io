I'm a sucker for simple, elegant design. I've not seen that many in any product category. Motorola's RAZR come to mind as well as Apple's iPod. Other than that, not that many. LG has come out with something that will hit a cord with those who appreciate simple and elegant designs, the KV6000 mobile phone. The display first: it has a QVGA (320*240) TFT LCD that can display 262,000 colors. Not bad. In addition, it has 512MB of internal memory you can tap into for your MP3 files.
