Really Thin Sony's NW-A840 is the company's thinnest Walkman at just 7.2mm thick (about 0.28-inch). That's pretty thin considering theÂ svelte-looking Zune HD has a thickness of 8.9mm--downright fat compared to the NW-A840. The iPod touch is a bit thinner than the Zune HD at 8.5mm, still quite a bit thicker than the thinnest aluminum-encased Walkman.

OLED LCD is still the volume king but when it comes to sexy, OLED is on top. The NW-A840 sports a biggish 2.8-inch OLED display, which should provide a super contrasty screen. With a black backgrounded UI an OLED-based portable like the NW-A840 should last quite a while. OLEDs only consume energy when it isn't black. Sony claims a 29 hours of music playback and 9 hours for video. Because OLEDs don't require a backlight, it can be very thin. I'm actually a bit unsure as to why the Zune HD is thicker than the iPod touch...
