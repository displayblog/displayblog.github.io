Nokia E71 Specifications
Display: 2.36" TFT LCD
Pixel Format: 320 x 240
Camera: 3.2MP (back), 640 x 480 (?, front)
Connectivity: WiFi, HSDPA, A-GPS
Storage: 110MB built-in, microSD
Battery: 20 days (standby), 10.5 hours (GSM talk), 4.5 hours (3G talk)
Keyboard: QWERTY
Color: Gray Steel, White Steel
Availability: US in Q3'08
Price: About US$500 (ouch!)

Nokia's E71 is a very slender smartphone with a built-in QWERTY keyboard. With 3G capability you can video call via the forward facing camera. Although I wouldn't want the person on the other end of the phone suffer through having to see me all the time, the option to inflict such pain from time to time sounds cool. The E71 is ultra-thin and the full QWERTY keyboard make for doing work as wall as texting as easy as pie.
