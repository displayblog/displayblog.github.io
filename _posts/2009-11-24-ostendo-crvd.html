Ostendo's CRVD is a curved 43-inch monitor sporting a 2880 x 900 resolution. Aspect ratio is an ultrawide 3.2:1. The exorbitantly high price of US$6499 is somewhat understandable as the CRVD is geared for defense training and simulation. Of course if money is no object and you must have a curved display to play your games the CRVD is it. Some person did just that and put three together: watch it on YouTube.
