Pioneer PRO-FPJ1

Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Display Technology: Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS)
Input: HDMI 1.3 (2)
Price: $9,000
Availability: June 2008

Pioneer is getting into the high-end projection business with its PRO-FPJ1 Elite KURO front projector that is geared toward cinematic installations. Pioneer is stating that advanced calibration is available on the PRO-FPJ1 and that black levels are just as black as the KURO TVs. The PRO-FPJ1 features three viewing modes including standard, dynamic and movie. The price of $9000 is incredible, which better describe the picture quality.

Source: Pioneer via Yahoo! Finance via Engadget
