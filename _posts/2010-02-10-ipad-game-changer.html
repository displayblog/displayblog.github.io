Walt Disney's CEO Bob Iger:

We find that iPad has a lot of potential. We think it's a really compelling device. We think it could be a game changer in terms of enabling us to create essentially new forms of content. Obviously it will be a great device to play games on and to watch videos because of the clarity of the screen.


It isn't surprising that Disney is excited about the iPad: Steve Jobs is the largest shareholder of Disney. I'm looking forward to all Disney content coming to the iPad.
