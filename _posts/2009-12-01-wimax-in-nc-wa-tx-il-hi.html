Clearwire's WiMAX service is a go in:

  • Hawaii (HI): Honolulu, Maui, Oahu, Lanai
  • North Carolina (NC): Charlotte, Greensboro, Raleigh
  • Texas (TX): Dallas, Fort Worth, San Antonio, Austin
  • Washington (WA): Puget Sound area including Seattle, King County, Pierce County, Kitsap County, Snohomish County
  • Illinois (IL): Chicago


The price for 4G? Just US$30/mo for the first six months. WiMAX is targeting not only mobile devices but the Internet connection you use at home too. That's quite affordable. Now if I can get an iPod touch that's WiMAX capable... Source: Clearwire (News Room) via Engadget
