MSI Neton M22 Specifications

Display: 22" Touch TFT LCD
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Pixel Format: 1920 x 1080
Optical: Blu-ray (optional)
Other: TV tuner, Remote Control
OS: Windows XP (single-core Atom) or Vista (dual-core Atom)
Availability: March 2009
Price: About US$799

MSI has introduced its all-in-one platform, the Neton. There are three sizes: 15.6" (M16), 18.5" (M19) and 22" (M22). I'll be focusing on the M22 here. The M22 has a 22" TFT LCD that has touch capability. The pixel format is a nice 1920 x 1080 that allows you to watch 1080p content without scaling. With products that are more IT-focused, it isn't such a great idea to scale HD content because in most cases those boxes do not have good scalers built-in. With the M22, you don't need to worry about that. However, I am not sure if a dual-core Atom CPU will be able to do the job of decoding MPEG2/MPEG4 video content fast enough. We'll see. It also depends on what type of GPU is being used. The $799 price is quite aggressive and I think will be popular with consumers wanting an attractive all-in-one PC with an even more attractive price.
