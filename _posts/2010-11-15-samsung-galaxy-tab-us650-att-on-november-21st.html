I'm not having a dandy-ol-time with my iPhone 4's data connection, so if I were in the market for a Samsung Galaxy Tab the only stores I would be heading towards would be marked Verizon. Which by the way sells it for $50 less and for just $5 more you get 1GB. The only big difference is that you're tied to a two-year contract.
