Walk into a Verizon Wireless store and you'll need to pay $300 plus tax to walk out with a Motorola DROID smartphone. You'll only pay $200 plus tax if you walk into a Best Buy store. But if you want to pay even less, go to Sears and you only have to pay $150 plus tax. I don't know if you can walk in, but the online store gives you free shipping via FedEx. So if you can wait a bit and want to save $50, Sears seems to be the best bet as of now.
