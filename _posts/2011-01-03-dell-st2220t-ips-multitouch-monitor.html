Dell: The 21.5-inch Dell ST2220T is a multitouch monitor sporting a fabulous IPS LCD. The pixel format is 1920x1080. The stand can be tilted forward, backward, and most importantly completely flat. Technical specifications:

  • 21.5-inch (54.61cm) 8-bit IPS TFT LCD
  • 1920x1080
  • Contrast Ratio: 1000:1
  • Brightness: 250 nits
  • Response Time: 8ms (GTG w/overdrive), 14ms (GTG w/o overdrive)
  • Viewing Angle: 178/178
  • Color Gamut: 82% CIE 1976, 72% CIE 1931
  • Color Depth: 16.7 million colors
  • Touch Technology: Optical
  • Connectivity: DVI-D w/HDCP, VGA, HDMI, USB 2.0 (3)

The ST2220T isn't quite portable, but if you want to multitouch your monitor like a tablet the ST2220T is the best thing going. I haven't played with the ST2220T, but don't expect the multitouch experience to be similar to an iPad. Optical touch technology isn't as precise as capacitive, but applying capacitive touch to a big monitor is difficult. Priced at $309.99.

The Dell ST2220T has been added to the list of IPS LCD Monitors.
